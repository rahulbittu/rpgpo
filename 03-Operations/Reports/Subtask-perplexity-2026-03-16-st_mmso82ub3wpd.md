## Volcano Hiking
Arenal Volcano National Park offers base-trail hikes through lava fields and rainforests, with summit climbing prohibited due to activity; key trails include Las Coladas (1.2 miles/2 km one-way, moderate, through 1992 lava flows with excellent volcano views), Arenal 1968 Trail (2.5 miles/4 km, moderate, 1.5 hours, wildlife spotting for birds/monkeys), El Ceibo (1.2 miles/2 km, easy), and Los Tucanes (1.9 miles/3 km, moderate wildlife focus).[2][3] Park entry is $15 USD, best 6-8 AM; guided tours $65-85 USD include transport, fees, naturalist guides with scopes.[3][5] Poás Volcano is another active option with crater views on full-day tours including La Paz Waterfall Gardens.[7] Source: https://ecoterracostarica.com/arenal-national-park-guide-2026/[2]; https://arenalvolcanocostaricatours.com/hike-guide-best-trails-tips[3]; https://www.tripadvisor.com/AttractionProductReview-g17484635-d13111321-Arenal_Volcano_Hike-La_Fortuna_La_Fortuna_de_San_Carlos_Arenal_Volcano_National_.html[5]

## Finding 1: Low Calorie Smoothies Collection (63-125 Calories Base)

**The Big Man's World** provides six customizable low-calorie smoothie recipes that take **2 minutes to prepare**. The base recipes range from **63-125 calories**, and you can add protein powder to reach higher protein targets:

- **Chocolate Smoothie**: 1 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1 cup ice, 1/4 cup sugar-free hot chocolate mix
- **Coffee Smoothie**: 1 frozen banana, 2 tablespoons brewed coffee, 1/2 cup ice, 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk

**Protein addition**: Adding 1/4 cup protein powder (20-30g) adds **80-130 calories**, bringing total recipes to **143-255 calories** with **20-30g protein**. This keeps all variations well under 300 calories while meeting high-protein requirements.[2]

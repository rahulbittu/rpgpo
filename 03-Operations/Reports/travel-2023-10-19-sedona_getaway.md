## Itinerary for a Weekend Getaway to Sedona, Arizona from Austin

### Day 1: Friday, March 20, 2026
- **Morning: Travel from Austin to Phoenix**
  - **Flight**: Book the Frontier Airlines flight from Austin (AUS) to Phoenix (PHX) for $42 one-way. Depart early to maximize your day in Sedona.
  - **Ground Transport**: Rent a car at Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport for the ~2-hour drive to Sedona. Alternatively, book a shuttle service if you prefer not to drive.

- **Afternoon: Arrival and Check-In**
  - **Accommodation**: Consider staying at Amara Resort & Spa for a luxurious experience along Oak Creek. Enjoy amenities like the infinity pool and complimentary yoga classes.
  - **Lunch**: Dine at SaltRock Southwest Kitchen at the resort for a taste of local cuisine.

- **Evening: Relax and Unwind**
  - **Spa Session**: Book a signature Rain Dance massage at the Amara Spa to relax after your travel.
  - **Dinner**: Enjoy a candlelit dinner at Cress on Oak Creek at L'Auberge de Sedona for a romantic evening.

### Day 2: Saturday, March 21, 2026
- **Morning: Hiking Adventure**
  - **Trail Option 1 (Easy)**: West Fork Trail in Oak Creek Canyon for a scenic, family-friendly hike.
  - **Trail Option 2 (Moderate)**: Soldier Pass Trail to explore Seven Sacred Pools and Devil’s Kitchen. Use the Sedona Shuttle due to parking restrictions.

- **Afternoon: Explore Sedona**
  - **Lunch**: Grab a meal at a local café in Uptown Sedona.
  - **Explore**: Visit local shops and galleries in Sedona’s vibrant arts district.

- **Evening: Stargazing**
  - **Location**: Head to Coconino National Forest, specifically Forest Road 525, for a dark sky viewing experience. 
  - **Event**: Check if the Sedona Astronomy Club is hosting a stargazing event that night.

### Day 3: Sunday, March 22, 2026
- **Morning: Leisure and Spa**
  - **Spa**: Book a morning session at L'Apothecary Spa at L'Auberge de Sedona for nature-inspired treatments.
  - **Brunch**: Enjoy a Sunday brunch at Cress on Oak Creek.

- **Afternoon: Short Hike or Relax**
  - **Trail Option**: Doe Mountain Trail for panoramic views if you’re up for a short hike.
  - **Relax**: Alternatively, enjoy the resort’s amenities or a leisurely bike ride.

- **Evening: Return to Austin**
  - **Drive**: Head back to Phoenix for your return flight.
  - **Flight**: Catch an evening flight back to Austin, ensuring you have ample time to return the rental car and check in.

## Costs & Budget
- **Flights**: Approximately $84 round-trip with Frontier Airlines.
- **Accommodation**: Rates at Amara Resort & Spa start around $300 per night.
- **Car Rental**: Budget around $50/day for a rental car.
- **Meals**: Estimate $50-$100 per day per person for meals.
- **Spa Treatments**: Expect to spend $100-$200 per session.

## Logistics
- **Booking**: Use Expedia to bundle flights and accommodations for potential savings.
- **Transportation**: Consider car rental for flexibility, or book shuttle services for convenience.
- **Packing**: Bring hiking gear, comfortable clothing, and a jacket for cooler evenings.

## Tips
- **Reservations**: Book spa treatments and dining reservations in advance to secure your preferred times.
- **Trail Access**: Check trail conditions and shuttle schedules ahead of time.
- **Stargazing**: Bring binoculars or a telescope if you have one, and dress warmly for night viewing.

This itinerary offers a balanced mix of relaxation, adventure, and local experiences, ensuring a memorable weekend in Sedona.

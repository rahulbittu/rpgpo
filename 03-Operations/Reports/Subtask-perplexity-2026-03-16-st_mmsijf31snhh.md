## Finding 1: Counteroffer Acceptance Failure Rates
Multiple national surveys and recruiter studies indicate that **50% to 85%** of employees who accept a counteroffer from their current employer leave within **6 to 12 months**, either voluntarily or involuntarily. Eclipse recruiting firm research specifies **80%** of candidates who accepted counteroffers left within **six months**. CareerBuilder survey notes **56%** of candidates do not negotiate higher pay initially when offered a new job.  
Source: https://www.apollotechnical.com/reasons-to-not-accept-a-counteroffer/

## Finding 2: Counteroffer Timeline in Tech/Professional Roles
Counteroffers, if made, typically arrive **within 1 week**, often in the **first 1 to 3 days** after resignation. Salary-only counteroffers are most common but rarely address non-monetary issues like work culture or advancement.  
Source: https://www.apollotechnical.com/reasons-to-not-accept-a-counteroffer/

## Finding 3: Tech Salary Trends Impacting Counteroffers (Canada Focus, 2026)
**73%** of tech leaders report professionals with specialized skills (e.g., AI/ML, cybersecurity, data analytics) earn more than peers; demand for data-related roles drives higher wages amid talent shortages. Certifications like AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner boost starting salaries; below-market pay risks losing candidates to negotiations. No direct counteroffer stats, but implies pressure for competitive offers in data engineering-adjacent roles like data analytics and ML engineer. Data from Robert Half’s **2026 Canada Salary Guide**.  
Source: https://www.roberthalf.com/ca/en/insights/research/it-and-tech-salary-guide-2026-trends

## Finding 4: Data Engineering Salary Benchmarks (US Focus, 2026)
Average base salary for **data engineers** reported as **$233,999** (Glassdoor data via Coursera, contextualized to 2026 trends). Related roles: data scientists at **$119,510**, data architects at **$142,646**. High demand in finance/IT/scientific services (over 75% of openings) supports negotiation leverage, indirectly influencing counteroffer dynamics. Data analysts range **$71,000-$119,000** annually.  
Source: https://www.coursera.org/articles/how-much-do-data-analysts-make-salary-guide

**Key principle:** Unlike commercial cinemas designed to be "dead" (heavily damped), **dedicated Hi-Fi listening rooms need to "breathe"**—balancing absorption with some reflective properties to maintain vibrancy and spatial definition[3].

**Critical placement factors:**

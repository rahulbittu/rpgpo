## Top 5 Project Management Tools for Small Engineering Teams (2026)

Based on recent web searches focusing on engineering, software dev, and small teams (under 20 people), the top tools are Jira, ClickUp, Asana, Monday.com, and Paymo. These stand out for agile workflows, issue tracking, and integrations with dev tools like GitHub/GitLab, suitable for small engineering teams. Selection prioritizes tools mentioned for engineers/programmers/small teams with pricing, features, and integrations from sources[3][4][5].

### 1. Jira
- **Pricing**: Starts at $7.75/user/month (Standard plan); Free for up to 10 users[3].
- **Key Features**: Robust issue/bug tracking, sprint planning, Kanban/Scrum boards, backlog management; excels for software engineering task prioritization[3][5].
- **Integrations**: Jira integrates with Epicflow for workload management, GitLab, MS Project, Oracle Primavera; bridges dev teams with management[5].
- **Source**: https://www.paymoapp.com/blog/project-management-software/[3]; https://www.epicflow.com/blog/best-workload-management-tools-for-team-efficiency/[5].

### 2. ClickUp
- **Pricing**: Free plan available; Unlimited at $7/user/month; Business at $12/user/month[3].
- **Key Features**: Highly customizable for cross-functional teams, workload automation, effort tracking, multiple views (Kanban, Gantt, lists)[3][5].
- **Integrations**: Integrates tool suites for workflow automation; supports engineering design workflows with tools like SolidWorks/Fusion 360 via ecosystem[4][5].
- **Source**: https://www.paymoapp.com/blog/project-management-software/[3]; https://www.epicflow.com/blog/best-workload-management-tools-for-team-efficiency/[5]; https://monograph.com/blog/best-engineering-design-software[4].

### 3. Asana
- **Pricing**: Personal free; Starter $10.99/user/month; Advanced $24.99/user/month[3][5].
- **Key Features**: Intuitive Kanban task management, workload visibility, multiple views (lists, timelines, boards), AI for summaries/risk identification; streamlines project workflows[5][7].
- **Integrations**: Broad ecosystem for engineering (e.g., GitHub, Slack); pairs with cloud design tools like Onshape/Fusion 360 for A&E firms[4][7].
- **Source**: https://www.paymoapp.com/blog/project-management-software/[3]; https://www.epicflow.com/blog/best-workload-management-tools-for-team-efficiency/[5]; https://www.larksuite.com/en_us/blog/task-management-tools[7].

### 4. Monday.com
- **Pricing**: Basic $9/user/month; Standard $12/user/month; Pro $19/user/month[3].
- **Key Features**: Highly visual collaboration, customizable dashboards, Gantt charts, workload balancing; go-to for engineering project tracking[3].
- **Integrations**: Supports dev tools (Jira, GitHub), design software; visual workflows for engineering schedules[3][4].
- **Source**: https://www.paymoapp.com/blog/project-management-software/[3]; https://monograph.com/blog/best-engineering-design-software[4].

### 5. Paymo
- **Pricing**: Free for 1 user; Small Office $9.95/user/month (up to 20 people); Team $15.95/user/month[3].
- **Key Features**: Time tracking, invoicing, task management; best for small teams with PM + billing needs[3].
- **Integrations**: Engineering-friendly with invoicing for consultancies; pairs with tools like Monograph for A&E tracking[3][4].
- **Source**: https://www.paymoapp.com/blog/project-management-software/[3]; https://monograph.com/blog/best-engineering-design-software[4].

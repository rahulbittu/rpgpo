## Plan

To build a personal finance automation stack with zero maintenance, the strategy will focus on integrating tools that automate investment management, accounting, bill payments, and financial reporting. The goal is to streamline financial processes, reduce manual intervention, and provide real-time insights into financial health.

## Implementation Steps

1. **Investment Management Automation**
   - **Tool**: Wealthfront
     - **Action**: Set up a Wealthfront account to automate investment portfolio management. Utilize features like daily tax-loss harvesting to optimize tax efficiency.
     - **Why**: Wealthfront offers comprehensive automation for investment portfolios, minimizing manual oversight and maximizing potential returns with low fees.
     - **First Step**: Open a Wealthfront account with a minimum deposit of $500.

2. **Free Portfolio Management for Small Accounts**
   - **Tool**: Fidelity Go
     - **Action**: For accounts under $25,000, use Fidelity Go to manage investments without fees.
     - **Why**: Provides a cost-effective solution for smaller investment accounts, leveraging zero expense ratio funds.
     - **First Step**: Open a Fidelity Go account and transfer funds under $25,000 for free management.

3. **Accounting and Cash Flow Automation**
   - **Tool**: Quickbooks
     - **Action**: Integrate Quickbooks with personal and business bank accounts to automate accounting tasks such as payments, payroll, and bookkeeping.
     - **Why**: Quickbooks offers comprehensive automation features that reduce manual bookkeeping tasks and provide real-time financial insights.
     - **First Step**: Connect Quickbooks to your bank accounts and set up automated transaction categorization.

4. **Bill Management and Spend Automation**
   - **Tool**: Spendesk
     - **Action**: Use Spendesk to automate bill payments and manage invoices efficiently.
     - **Why**: Automates accounts payable processes, reducing the time spent on manual invoice management and ensuring timely payments.
     - **First Step**: Set up Spendesk and link it to your bank accounts to start automating bill payments.

## Review Schedule

- **Monthly Review**: Assess the performance of investment accounts (Wealthfront and Fidelity Go) to ensure alignment with financial goals.
- **Quarterly Review**: Review Quickbooks reports for cash flow insights and ensure all transactions are correctly categorized.
- **Annual Review**: Evaluate the overall financial automation stack for potential upgrades or changes based on new tools or financial goals.

By following these steps and utilizing the specified tools, you can achieve a highly automated personal finance system that minimizes manual intervention and provides robust financial management and reporting capabilities.

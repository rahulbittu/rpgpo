## Finding 5: Bond Laddering Strategy

**Duration Ladder Construction**

Bond laddering involves purchasing bonds with staggered maturity dates to:
- Reduce reinvestment risk
- Provide predictable cash flow
- Allow portfolio rebalancing as bonds mature

**Short-Duration Advantage**: Shorter maturity bonds are "less exposed to a deterioration in economic conditions that would increase default expectations," making them valuable in uncertain environments[3].

The yield-to-duration trade-off is particularly compelling in short-duration markets, where elevated yields provide "a cushion against potential price falls"[3].

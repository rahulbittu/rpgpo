## Key Findings

### Recruitment Strategies
1. **Build Relationships with Board Recruiters**: Establish and maintain connections with board recruitment firms, especially those with a track record in your sector. This involves actively engaging with recruiters via LinkedIn and keeping them updated on your career progress to stay top-of-mind for potential assignments.

2. **Conduct Board Skills Analysis**: Before recruiting advisors, analyze your board's skills matrix to identify gaps, focusing on future needs such as tech disruption and risk management. This proactive approach ensures targeted recruitment and alignment with strategic goals.

3. **Use Retained Search Firms**: Employ retained search firms for high-quality advisor matches. These firms offer confidential mapping and shortlist management, which can lead to better outcomes in advisor recruitment.

### Meeting Formats and Relationship Maintenance
1. **Forward-Focused Meeting Formats**: High-performing boards dedicate 70% of meeting time to strategic discussions rather than operational updates. This approach fosters robust debate and long-term strategy development.

2. **360 Reviews and Third-Party Evaluations**: Regular assessments of individual advisors through 360 reviews and third-party evaluations help maintain relationships and drive continuous improvement.

3. **Shift to Retainers Over Meeting Fees**: Transitioning to annual retainers for board compensation aligns pay with oversight responsibilities and simplifies the compensation process.

## Critical Feature Gaps for TopRanker

Based on this analysis, **TopRanker should prioritize:**

1. **Claim Verification:** Implement a simple claim process (email verification or phone number confirmation) similar to Google Business Profile
2. **Review Response Dashboard:** Build a centralized inbox for business owners to respond to reviews with templates and AI suggestions
3. **Analytics Dashboard:** Include metrics like listing visibility, review sentiment, response time, and local search ranking position
4. **Bulk Management:** Enable multi-location businesses to update listings, photos, and hours across all locations simultaneously
5. **API Access:** Allow integration with third-party tools (CRM, email marketing, analytics platforms)

These features are now table-stakes for platforms competing in the local business directory space as of March 2026.

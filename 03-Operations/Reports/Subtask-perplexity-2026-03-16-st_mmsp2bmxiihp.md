## Online Presence Review
Audit digital footprints across platforms to align with goals and values. Key steps include:
- Review all profiles (e.g., LinkedIn, personal website) for consistency in visuals, messaging, and updates; invest in professional headshots and uniform style.[1]
- Check for outdated info like contact details or job titles, updating promptly to avoid negative brand reflection.[2]
- Map presence elements: LinkedIn, podcasts, guest appearances; ensure they match mission, vision, and ideal audience.[3]
- Use tools like digital business cards (e.g., Wave platform) for shareable profiles with analytics on views and engagement.[2]

**Concrete Next Step:** List your top 5 platforms, screenshot current profiles, and score alignment (1-10) against your 2026 goals by March 20, 2026.

## Content Quality Assessment
Evaluate content for consistency, quality, and impact over quantity. Criteria include:
- Mix formats: long-form articles, short videos, live Q&As; post weekly minimum (not monthly) for relevance.[1]
- Themes: industry insights (thought leadership), company updates, personal stories revealing leadership; prioritize 1 insightful post over 5 superficial ones.[1]
- Core elements: unique strengths, brand story, signature programs; keep messaging simple, memorable, aligned with visuals/voice.[3]
- Track via analytics on platforms like Wave digital cards for views/engagement to refine strategy.[2]

**Concrete Next Step:** Review last 12 months' 20 posts; rate on 1-5 scale for insight value and consistency; delete/repost top 5 improvers by March 23, 2026.

## Network Analysis
Assess connections for reach, loyalty, and advocacy. Evaluation includes:
- Engagement metrics: comments, messages, recommendations/endorsements from colleagues/clients to build trust.[1][2]
- Collaborations: partner with founders/influencers for diverse perspectives and expanded reach.[1]
- Audience segments: match content to preferences; track interactions for loyalty (e.g., repeat engagers).[1]
- Feedback loop: collect client notes to refine; request endorsements on platforms like LinkedIn or digital cards.[2][3]

**Concrete Next Step:** Export LinkedIn connections (aim for 500+ active); categorize top 50 by engagement last 30 days; message 10 for endorsements by March 18, 2026.

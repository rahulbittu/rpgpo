## Key Findings

1. **Ads Revenue Potential**: Data engineering blogs can earn between $2-10 RPM through ad networks like Google AdSense or Mediavine. At 500,000 monthly visitors, this could translate to over $10,000 monthly, although data engineering blogs typically average lower RPMs due to their B2B audience focus.

2. **Sponsorship Opportunities**: Sponsored posts from companies like Snowflake and Databricks can pay between $500-5,000 per post. Blogs with 10,000+ subscribers can expect around $2,000 per sponsored post, with demand increasing due to AI trends.

3. **Courses as a Revenue Stream**: Selling data engineering courses on platforms such as Teachable or Gumroad can be highly lucrative. With a course priced at $99 and 1,000 enrollments, creators can earn between $50,000-200,000 annually.

4. **Consulting Leads**: Blogs can serve as a funnel for consulting leads, converting 5-15% of leads into consulting clients at $150-300 per hour. This can potentially generate over $100,000 annually in side income.

5. **Income Estimates by Blog Tier**:
   - Starter blogs (10k monthly visitors) can earn $500-2,000/month.
   - Mid-tier blogs (50k-100k visitors, 5k email list) can earn $5,000-15,000/month.
   - Top-tier influencers can earn $20,000-50,000+/month, with some reaching $42,000 in a single quarter.

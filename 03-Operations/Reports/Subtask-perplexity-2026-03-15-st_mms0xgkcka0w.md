## Finding 1: Garage to Bedroom Conversion Costs (Proxy for Bedroom to Office, as Direct Data Limited)
Garage-to-bedroom conversions provide the closest available cost benchmarks for repurposing a bedroom into a home office, involving similar elements like electrical upgrades, insulation, lighting, flooring, and permits. A simple bedroom conversion (no bathroom) averages **$12,000**, while a primary bedroom with bathroom reaches **$25,000**.[2]

**Breakdown of Key Costs:**
- Permits: **$300–$1,000** depending on local requirements.[2]
- Electrical (wiring, outlets, lighting): **$1,000–$3,000**.[2]
- Doors: **$200–$1,000**.[2]
- Windows: **$500–$2,000**.[2]
- Insulation/HVAC upgrades: **$500–$2,000** total (**$0.40–$6 per sq ft**).[2]

Overall garage-to-living-space average: **$40 per sq ft** (range **$25–$75 per sq ft**), with labor often 60% of total.[2]

Source: https://www.angi.com/articles/garage-conversions-add-valuable-living-space.htm[2]

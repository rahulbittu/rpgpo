## Stage: research

## 30-Day Plan: Learning and Integration
Focus on understanding the role, team, product, and processes through observation and basic contributions. Key activities include completing trainings, shadowing sessions, reviewing product demos/customer materials, and delivering first small tasks[1][2][4].

- **Complete all mandatory trainings** and review role expectations/responsibilities[1][4].
- **Shadow meetings** with cross-functional stakeholders and participate in team meetings[1][2].
- **Set initial SMART goals** aligned with job description, e.g., understand product processes and team metrics[2][4].
- **Goal**: Achieve role clarity and small deliverables by end of week 1; full training completion by day 30[1].

**Next Step**: Schedule 30-day review with manager to assess progress and adjust goals[3].

Source: https://www.flexhire.com/blog/yana/new-hire-onboarding-checklist-step-by-step[1]; https://www.qooper.io/blog/new-hire-orientation-agenda[2]; https://www.hrcloud.com/blog/the-30-60-90-onboarding-plan-how-to-gradually-guide-your-new-hires[4]

## 60-Day Plan: Ownership and Execution
Shift to self-managed tasks, deeper process understanding, and active participation. Establish regular feedback loops and take ownership of recurring work[1][2][3].

- **Deliver self-managed tasks** and contribute to planning sessions[1][3].
- **Set up weekly one-on-ones** with manager/peers for feedback; identify team issues[2][4].
- **Align on KPIs**, e.g., team metrics review and initial performance targets like meeting deadlines[2][4].
- **Goal**: Demonstrate productivity alignment; ~80% engagement boost via weekly feedback per Gallup research[3].

**Next Step**: Conduct 60-day review to celebrate achievements and refine long-term goals[3].

Source: https://teamflect.com/blog/performance-management/30-60-90-day-plans[3]; https://www.hrcloud.com/blog/the-30-60-90-onboarding-plan-how-to-gradually-guide-your-new-hires[4]

## 90-Day Plan: Proficiency and Impact
Achieve full role ownership with measurable results and first performance review. Focus on sustained contributions and development planning[1][2][4].

- **Own role fully**, deliver measurable outcomes (e.g., sales conversions or productivity metrics)[1][4].
- **Participate in performance review**; discuss career development and ongoing targets[1][2].
- **Goal**: Proficiency demonstration, reduced attrition risk via structured feedback[2].

**Next Step**: Post-90-day, set quarterly goals using 30-60-90 template for sustained success[3][4].

Source: https://www.flexhire.com/blog/yana/new-hire-onboarding-checklist-step-by-step[1]; https://www.qooper.io/blog/new-hire-orientation-agenda[2]; https://www.hrcloud.com/blog/the-30-60-90-onboarding-plan-how-to-gradually-guide-your-new-hires[4]

## Strategies for Success Across 90 Days
- **Use SMART goals** for all targets to ensure achievability[4].
- **Structure with 30-60-90 reviews** after each phase for feedback and adjustments[3].
- **Prioritize belonging**: Regular manager check-ins and peer feedback to accelerate productivity[2].
- **Adapt for PM role**: While general, emphasize product/process review and stakeholder shadowing in days 1-30, per onboarding best practices[1].

## Key Findings

1. **Perito Moreno Glacier Trekking**
   - **Operator**: Hielo y Aventura
   - **Options**: Big Ice Trek ($295 USD) and Mini Trekking ($165 USD)
   - **Location**: El Calafate, Argentina
   - **Booking**: Reserve 24 hours in advance

2. **Torres del Paine W Trek**
   - **Location**: Torres del Paine National Park, Chile
   - **Duration**: 5 days
   - **Accommodation**: Lodges ($180–$280 USD), Refugios ($80–$120 USD), Camping ($25–$40 USD)
   - **Season**: Best from December to March

3. **Buenos Aires Activities**
   - **Tango Shows**: Renowned cultural experience
   - **Argentine Steak Tours**: Culinary exploration
   - **Wine Tasting in Mendoza**: Not directly in Buenos Aires but a popular extension

## Detailed Analysis

### Day 1-3: Buenos Aires
- **Day 1**: Arrival in Buenos Aires
  - **Evening**: Attend a tango show at El Querandí or La Ventana ($60–$100 USD, includes dinner)
- **Day 2**: Argentine Steak Tour
  - **Morning/Afternoon**: Guided steak tour through Palermo or San Telmo ($100–$150 USD)
  - **Evening**: Explore local bars or cafes
- **Day 3**: Travel to Mendoza
  - **Morning**: Flight to Mendoza (2 hours)
  - **Afternoon**: Wine tasting tour in Luján de Cuyo or Maipú ($120–$200 USD)

### Day 4-6: Mendoza
- **Day 4**: Full Day Wine Tour
  - **Visit**: Renowned wineries like Catena Zapata or Bodega Norton
  - **Evening**: Return to Buenos Aires
- **Day 5**: Travel to El Calafate
  - **Morning**: Flight to El Calafate (3 hours)
  - **Evening**: Rest and explore local cuisine
- **Day 6**: Perito Moreno Glacier Trekking
  - **Full Day**: Big Ice Trek with Hielo y Aventura
  - **Evening**: Return to El Calafate

### Day 7-10: Patagonia
- **Day 7**: Travel to Torres del Paine
  - **Morning**: Bus to Torres del Paine (5-6 hours)
  - **Evening**: Check into accommodation
- **Day 8-10**: Torres del Paine W Trek
  - **Activities**: Trekking, photography, and wildlife observation
  - **Accommodation**: Stay in refugios or camp

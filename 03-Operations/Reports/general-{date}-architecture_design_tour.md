## Key Findings

### Barcelona Highlights
1. **Gaudí Masterpieces**: 
   - **Sagrada Família**: Scheduled completion in 2026 with the Tower of Jesus Christ inauguration. Tickets start at €34.
   - **Park Güell**: Known for its vibrant mosaics, tickets start at €18.
   - **Casa Batlló**: Features a new exhibition space, tickets start at €29.
   - **Casa Milà (La Pedrera)**: Offers unique architectural insights, tickets start at €12.

2. **Dining Recommendations**:
   - **Cinc Sentits**: Michelin-starred, offers a modern Catalan tasting menu.
   - **Tickets**: Known for creative tapas, a must-visit for food enthusiasts.

### Valencia Highlights
1. **City of Arts and Sciences**: A futuristic architectural complex featuring an opera house, science museum, and aquarium.
2. **Valencia Cathedral**: A blend of Romanesque, Gothic, and Baroque architecture.
3. **La Lonja de la Seda**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, exemplifying Gothic architecture.

3. **Dining Recommendations**:
   - **Ricard Camarena Restaurant**: Offers innovative dishes with local ingredients.
   - **Casa Montaña**: Traditional tapas bar with a historic ambiance.

## Detailed Analysis

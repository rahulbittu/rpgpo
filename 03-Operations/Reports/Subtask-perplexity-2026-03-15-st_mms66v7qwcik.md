## Sugar Substitutes
- **Stevia (Reb M variant)**: A natural steviol glycoside with clean, sugar-like taste and no bitterness; produced via Arzeda’s enzymes from stevia leaf extract for cost-effective scalability. Enables sugar reduction in beverages without off-notes, paired with flavor modulation. Source: https://www.mane.com/media/mane-and-arzeda-team-up-to-offer-next-gen-sugar-reduction-solutions-to-the-food-and-beverage-industry (Mar 5, 2026).
- **Sucralose**: Non-sugar sweetener that replaces refined sugar; SWEET study (published in Nature Metabolism) showed 1+ year use led to better weight outcomes and gut health improvements vs. sugar diets. Source: https://www.washingtonexaminer.com/opinion/4451031/make-healthy-choices-easier-dietary-guidelines/.
- **Dates**: Whole-food sweetener in syrups, confections, gels, spreads; positioned as minimally processed alternative at Expo West 2026 for natural sugar reduction. Source: https://nielseniq.com/global/en/insights/analysis/2026/expo-west-2026-trends/.

## Craving Management Techniques
- **Substitution over elimination**: Use sugar alternatives like sucralose/stevia to stabilize blood sugar and adjust taste preferences gradually, avoiding all-or-nothing failure common in perfectionist diets. Source: https://www.washingtonexaminer.com/opinion/4451031/make-healthy-choices-easier-dietary-guidelines/; https://oobli.com/blogs/news/why-it-s-so-hard-to-reduce-sugar-and-what-actually-works.
- **Holistic reformulation for savory foods**: Address sugar's roles in flavor/texture with functional build-back (e.g., Ingredion solutions); 64% of global consumers actively reduce sugar, 73% pay more for reduced-sugar products (2026 data). Source: https://www.ingredion.com/na/en-us/be-whats-next/sweet-truths-sugar-reduction-savory-products.
- **GLP-1 inspired strategies**: Focus on satiety, stable energy, blood-sugar balance via protein/fiber-forward products; prominent at Expo West 2026. Source: https://nielseniq.com/global/en/insights/analysis/2026/expo-west-2026-trends/.

# Subtask Output — Design Weekly Reflection and Planning System
## Model: gpt-4o
## Stage: report
## Date: 2026-03-16

## Plan

### Objective
To create a structured weekly reflection and planning system that integrates a Sunday review, week-ahead planning, and habit tracking. This system will leverage insights from Hailley Griffis' weekly planning routine, the Alastair Weekly Log, and Reflection.app guidelines.

### Components
1. **Sunday Review Template**
2. **Week-Ahead Planning Guide**
3. **Habit Tracking Integration**

## Implementation Steps

### 1. Sunday Review Template

- **Purpose**: To evaluate the past week and prepare for the upcoming one.
- **Steps**:
  - **Review Priorities**: List last week's priorities and categorize them as completed, carried over, or changed.
  - **Reflection**: Answer the following:
    - What went well this week?
    - What could be improved?
    - What changes will I make next week?
  - **AI-Processed Insights** (Optional): Record a voice note summarizing your week, then process it with AI for additional insights.
  - **Link Projects/Goals**: Use Notion to connect reflections to ongoing projects and set 1-2 priorities per category (work, personal, side-projects).

### 2. Week-Ahead Planning Guide

- **Purpose**: To outline tasks and priorities for the upcoming week.
- **Steps**:
  - **Task Listing**: Write down all tasks for the week on one side of a page or digital document.
  - **Day Assignment**: Create a grid with days of the week and place dots next to tasks to indicate planned days.
  - **Flexibility**: Allow for adjustments as the week progresses, using the Alastair Weekly Log method to combat rigid scheduling.
  - **Visual Snapshot**: Maintain a visual overview of the week to easily pivot tasks as needed.

### 3. Habit Tracking Integration

- **Purpose**: To incorporate habit tracking into the weekly planning system.
- **Steps**:
  - **Select Habits**: Choose 2-3 habits to focus on each week.
  - **Tracking Method**: Use a digital tool like Notion or a simple paper tracker to log daily habit completion.
  - **Weekly Review**: Include a section in the Sunday Review to assess habit progress and adjust strategies if needed.

## Tools/Resources

- **Notion**: For digital planning, linking projects, and habit tracking.
- **AI Tools**: For processing voice notes (e.g., Otter.ai, Descript).
- **Paper/Digital Templates**: Use the Alastair Weekly Log template for flexible task planning (available at imbusybeingawesome.com/weekly).

## Review Schedule

- **Weekly Review**: Conduct every Sunday using the Sunday Review Template.
- **Mid-Week Check-In**: Briefly review progress on Wednesday to adjust tasks and priorities as needed.
- **Monthly Reflection**: At the end of each month, conduct a more comprehensive review to assess long-term goals and habit development.

By following this structured system, you can maintain a clear overview of your weekly tasks, adapt to changes flexibly, and integrate habit tracking seamlessly into your routine.

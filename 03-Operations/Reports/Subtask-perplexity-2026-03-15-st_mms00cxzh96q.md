## Finding 2: Nature-Based Hiking and Contemplative Activities in Hocking Hills

**Hocking Hills** (Ohio) offers a weekend digital detox model requiring minimal planning[2]. Practical activities include:

- Hiking trails (Ash Cave and Old Man's Cave specifically mentioned)
- Morning coffee on porches with natural views
- Reading by fire

## Key Pattern Across All 2026 Offerings

Effective digital detox weekends share common elements: **structured group activities** (not unstructured downtime), **physical engagement** (hiking, yoga, outdoor recreation), **communal experiences** (campfires, shared meals, group challenges), and **natural environments** that naturally reduce device dependency[1][2][4]. The shift in 2026 travel preferences reflects recognition that families, youth groups, and corporate teams increasingly seek experiences emphasizing "reduced screen time and in-person connection"[1].

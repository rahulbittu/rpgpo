## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Lisbon
- **Activity:** Settle into accommodations and explore the local area.
- **Logistics:** Arrive at Lisbon Airport, transfer to hotel.
- **Cost:** Airport transfer approximately €15-€25 via taxi or ride-sharing.

### Day 2: Lisbon - Alfama Food Tour
- **Activity:** Join the "Taste of Lisbon" walking food tour in Alfama.
- **Details:** 3.5-hour tour with 6-7 tastings, wine pairings, and cultural commentary.
- **Cost:** €89-€99 per person.
- **Logistics:** Meet at a central location in Alfama. Tour departs daily.
- **Outcome:** Experience traditional Portuguese cuisine and learn about Lisbon's culinary history.

### Day 3: Lisbon - Explore Local Markets
- **Activity:** Visit Mercado da Ribeira (Time Out Market) and Mercado de Campo de Ourique.
- **Details:** Sample a variety of Portuguese dishes and local specialties.
- **Cost:** Budget €20-€30 for food tastings.
- **Logistics:** Accessible via public transport or a short taxi ride.
- **Outcome:** Discover a range of local products and dishes at your own pace.

### Day 4: Travel to Porto
- **Activity:** Travel from Lisbon to Porto.
- **Logistics:** Take a train from Lisbon Oriente to Porto Campanhã (approximately 3 hours).
- **Cost:** Train tickets range from €25-€45 depending on class and time.
- **Outcome:** Arrive in Porto and settle into accommodations.

### Day 5: Porto - Ribeira Food Tour
- **Activity:** Join the "Culinary Backstreets" Ribeira Food Tour.
- **Details:** 3.5-hour tour focusing on Porto's culinary scene.
- **Cost:** €99 per person.
- **Logistics:** Meet at a designated location in Ribeira. Tour departs daily.
- **Outcome:** Enjoy diverse tastings and learn about Porto's food culture.

### Day 6: Porto - Wine and Cheese Experience
- **Activity:** Visit a local wine cellar for a port wine and cheese tasting.
- **Details:** Explore the cellars and sample different varieties of port wine paired with local cheeses.
- **Cost:** Approximately €20-€30 per person.
- **Logistics:** Book a tour at a well-known cellar like Graham's or Taylor's.
- **Outcome:** Gain insight into Porto's famous wine industry.

### Day 7: Departure from Porto
- **Activity:** Final day for last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
- **Logistics:** Transfer to Porto Airport for departure.
- **Cost:** Airport transfer approximately €15-€25 via taxi or ride-sharing.

## Costs & Budget
- **Total Estimated Cost:** €350-€450 per person for tours and tastings.
- **Additional Costs:** Accommodation, transportation, and personal expenses not included in the above estimate.

## Logistics
- **Transportation:** Utilize public transport, taxis, or ride-sharing for city travel.
- **Accommodation:** Book centrally located hotels or Airbnb for easy access to tour starting points.

## Tips
- **Booking:** Reserve tours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons.
- **Dietary Restrictions:** Inform tour operators of any dietary restrictions ahead of time.
- **Local Currency:** Ensure you have some cash in euros for small purchases or tips.

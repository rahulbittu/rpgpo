Engineers transitioning to PM leverage technical depth for better collaboration and planning, but must shift from implementation details to user-focused strategy.[1][4] Paul Carty, ex-engineer at Workhuman, translated his coding skills and problem-solving into PM ownership, noting engineers excel in understanding "zero to one" product work and engineering collaboration.[1][4] Nemma Adeni, former Meta software engineer, highlighted how engineering surveys diverse teams (user-facing, backend, API) prepare PMs for roadmapping and issue anticipation.[4]

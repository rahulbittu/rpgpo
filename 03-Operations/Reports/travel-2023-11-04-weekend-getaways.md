## Key Findings

1. **Fredericksburg:**
   - **Travel & Attractions:** 1.25-hour drive from Austin. Known for wineries, Luckenbach, and LBJ Ranch tours. Full-day tours available for $94/person.
   - **Accommodations:** Options range from $50-100/night at Yogi Bear's Jellystone Park to $200-400/night at luxury Postcard Cabins.
   - **Costs:** Gas ~$15 round-trip. Wine tasting $20-40/person.

2. **New Braunfels:**
   - **Travel & Attractions:** 50-minute drive from Austin. Popular for river tubing and Gruene Historic District. Tube rentals ~$25-35/person.
   - **Accommodations:** Camp Fimfo ($60-120/night) and riverside hotels ($150-250/night).
   - **Costs:** Tubing shuttle ~$10, parking $10/day.

3. **Wimberley:**

## Detailed Analysis

### Fredericksburg
- **Pros:** Rich cultural experiences with historical sites and wineries. Affordable gas and a variety of accommodation options.
- **Cons:** Higher accommodation costs for luxury stays.

### New Braunfels
- **Pros:** Quick access from Austin, affordable tubing activities, and a vibrant historic district.
- **Cons:** Additional costs for parking and shuttles. Accommodation prices can be high for riverside hotels.

### Wimberley & Marble Falls
- **Pros:** Both locations offer unique natural attractions and a quieter getaway experience.
- **Cons:** Lack of detailed information on accommodations and specific costs makes planning more challenging.

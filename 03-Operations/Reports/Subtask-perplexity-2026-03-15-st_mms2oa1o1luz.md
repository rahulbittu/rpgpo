## Water
- **1 gallon (3.8 liters) per person per day** for drinking and sanitation, totaling 3 gallons per person for 72 hours.[4]
- **1.5-2 liters per person per day** (6-7 cups) to prevent dehydration; replace bottled water every time clocks change.[3]
- Source: https://sskblaw.com/emergency-kit-essentials-for-a-snow-storm-protecting-your-family-and-your-legal-rights[4]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]

## Food
- **3 days of non-perishable food** that won't spoil and needs minimal preparation: canned or dried food, energy bars, protein bars, nuts, peanut butter, shelf-stable meals.[1][3][4]
- Include a **manual can opener** for canned goods.[1][4]
- Commercial example: **EP 72 Hour Bug Out Bag** with 7800 calories and 468g protein from nutrient-dense EP food products.[2]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://essentialprovisions.com/products/72-hour-preparedness-kit[2]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]; https://sskblaw.com/emergency-kit-essentials-for-a-snow-storm-protecting-your-family-and-your-legal-rights[4]

## First Aid Kit
- Core items: **gloves, gauze pads (e.g., ABD Pad 12.7 × 23cm), variety of bandages (14 pieces including fingertip and butterfly closures), antiseptic, scissors, tweezers, needle and thread, instant ice packs, survival blanket (1.6m × 2.1m)**.[1][2][3]
- Advanced: **nitrile gloves (large, blue), 18” moldable splint, dry burn dressing (40 × 60cm), wound clot hemostatic gauze (3” × 8”), Snake Staff Splint Tourniquet (Gen 2 ETQ – 1.5" – Orange)** from PrepEM Wild Essentials Pro Kit.[2]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://essentialprovisions.com/products/72-hour-preparedness-kit[2]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]

## Medications and Health
- **Prescription medications** for family members; rotate regularly or carry during evacuation; include vitamins and anti-sickness meds.[1][3]
- **Backup eyeglasses, hygiene supplies** (moist towelettes, garbage bags, toilet paper, hand sanitizer, wet wipes).[1][3][4]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]; https://sskblaw.com/emergency-kit-essentials-for-a-snow-storm-protecting-your-family-and-your-legal-rights[4]

## Power and Communication
- **External battery pack or wind-up phone charger** for cell phone use; wind-up torch (flashlight).[1][3]
- **Whistle** to signal for help; battery-powered carbon monoxide detector.[3][4]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]; https://sskblaw.com/emergency-kit-essentials-for-a-snow-storm-protecting-your-family-and-your-legal-rights[4]

## Shelter and Warmth
- **Dust masks and duct tape** for shelter-in-place.[1][3]
- **Warm clothes, blankets, sleeping bags, wool/fleece blankets, gloves, hats, thermal socks, emergency thermal blankets**; avoid cotton.[3][4]
- Store in **waterproof, wheeled suitcase or container** (e.g., ADICA Go Bag for mobility).[3][5]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]; https://sskblaw.com/emergency-kit-essentials-for-a-snow-storm-protecting-your-family-and-your-legal-rights[4]; https://kutv.com/features/fresh-living/the-only-emergency-bag-you-will-ever-need[5]

## Cash and Miscellaneous
- **Cash in small bills and coins** for payphones or laundry if cards/power fail.[1]
- **Non-power entertainment**: board games, cards, books.[3]
- For babies/pets: **baby food, diapers, formula, clothes, wipes; pet water, food, toys, evacuation plan**.[1][3]
- Source: https://www.calgary.ca/emergencies/preparedness/72-hour-kit.html[1]; https://www.ladbible.com/news/world-news/uk-72-hour-survival-kit-iran-usa-israel-682141-20260302[3]

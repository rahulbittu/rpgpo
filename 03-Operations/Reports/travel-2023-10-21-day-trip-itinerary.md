## Detailed Analysis

### Driving Route and Traffic
- **Route**: I-35 S is the main route from Austin to San Antonio. It's a straightforward drive, but traffic can vary, especially on weekends.
- **Traffic Considerations**: While no current major disruptions are noted, the North Side of San Antonio is undergoing improvements that might affect traffic patterns in the future. Always check for updates on Google Maps or Waze.

### Must-Visit Spots

1. **San Antonio Zoo**:
   - **Features**: Diverse animal exhibits, special events like "Pollinators" sculptures.
   - **Activities**: Carousel, train rides, and animal feedings.
   - **Hours**: Extended during Spring Break, which might affect crowd levels.

2. **San Antonio River Walk**:
   - **Features**: 15-mile pedestrian path with dining and cultural experiences.
   - **Activities**: Explore La Villita Historic Arts Village and enjoy live music performances.

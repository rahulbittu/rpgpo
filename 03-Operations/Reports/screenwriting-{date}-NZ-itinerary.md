## 11-Day North Island Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Auckland
- **Activities:** Arrive in Auckland, explore the city center, visit Sky Tower for panoramic views.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in Auckland CBD.
- **First Step:** Book a flight to Auckland and reserve accommodation.

### Day 2: Auckland to Bay of Islands
- **Travel:** Drive or take a bus to the Bay of Islands (approx. 3 hours).
- **Activities:** Afternoon sailing tour to explore the islands.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in Paihia.
- **First Step:** Reserve a sailing tour in the Bay of Islands.

### Day 3: Bay of Islands
- **Activities:** Explore Waitangi Treaty Grounds, visit Russell, and enjoy local beaches.
- **Accommodation:** Continue stay in Paihia.
- **First Step:** Book tickets for the Waitangi Treaty Grounds.

### Day 4: Bay of Islands to Rotorua
- **Travel:** Drive to Rotorua (approx. 6 hours).
- **Activities:** Evening relaxation at Polynesian Spa.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in Rotorua.
- **First Step:** Reserve accommodation in Rotorua.

### Day 5: Rotorua Geothermal Parks
- **Activities:**
  - Morning visit to Hell's Gate Geothermal Park & Mud Spa for guided geothermal walks.
  - Afternoon at Te Puia for the Te Rā Guided Experience, including Pōhutu Geyser and Māori Arts and Crafts Institute.
- **Accommodation:** Continue stay in Rotorua.
- **First Step:** Book tours at Hell's Gate and Te Puia.

### Day 6: Rotorua to Hobbiton to Tongariro
- **Travel:** Drive to Matamata (approx. 1 hour) for Hobbiton tour, then to Tongariro (approx. 2.5 hours).
- **Activities:** Hobbiton Movie Set tour.
- **Accommodation:** Stay near Tongariro National Park.
- **First Step:** Reserve a Hobbiton Movie Set tour.

### Day 7: Tongariro Alpine Crossing
- **Activities:** Full-day hike on the Tongariro Alpine Crossing.
- **Accommodation:** Continue stay near Tongariro National Park.
- **First Step:** Arrange transport to and from the trailhead.

### Day 8: Tongariro to Wellington
- **Travel:** Drive to Wellington (approx. 4.5 hours).
- **Activities:** Evening exploration of Wellington's waterfront.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in Wellington.
- **First Step:** Book accommodation in Wellington.

### Day 9: Wellington Food Scene
- **Activities:** Visit Te Papa Museum, enjoy a food tour exploring Wellington's culinary scene.
- **Accommodation:** Continue stay in Wellington.
- **First Step:** Book a guided food tour in Wellington.

### Day 10: Wellington to Rotorua
- **Travel:** Fly or drive back to Rotorua.
- **Activities:** Evening Māori cultural experience at Tamaki Māori Village.
- **Accommodation:** Stay in Rotorua.
- **First Step:** Reserve a spot at Tamaki Māori Village.

### Day 11: Departure
- **Activities:** Last-minute shopping or sightseeing in Rotorua.
- **Travel:** Depart from Rotorua or Auckland.
- **First Step:** Arrange transport to the airport.

### Notes:
- **Travel Arrangements:** Consider renting a car for flexibility, especially for travel between destinations.
- **Booking:** Ensure all tours and accommodations are booked in advance to secure spots, especially during peak seasons.
- **Packing:** Prepare for varied weather, especially for outdoor activities like the Tongariro Alpine Crossing.

This itinerary balances cultural experiences, natural wonders, and leisure activities, ensuring a comprehensive exploration of New Zealand's North Island.

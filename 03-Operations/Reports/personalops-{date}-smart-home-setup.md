## Detailed Analysis

### Hub: Samsung SmartThings Station
- **Price**: ~$54 USD
- **Features**: Supports Z-Wave, Zigbee, and Wi-Fi; allows for local processing, which ensures fast execution of routines.
- **Compatibility**: Works with a wide range of devices from different manufacturers, including Philips Hue, Honeywell, and Aeotec.

### Devices
- **Philips Hue Bulbs**: $50 for a 2-pack
  - **Use**: Smart lighting control, color and brightness adjustments.
- **Motion Sensor**: ~$20
  - **Use**: Automate lighting or security alerts based on movement detection.
- **Smart Plugs**: ~$15 each
  - **Use**: Remote control of non-smart devices, energy monitoring.

### Automation Rules
- **Lighting Automation**: Configure lights to turn on when motion is detected in the evening or off when no motion is detected for a set period.
- **Scheduled Lighting**: Set lights to turn on at sunset and off at bedtime.
- **Energy Management**: Use smart plugs to turn off devices when not in use to save energy.

## SimpliSafe
SimpliSafe leads no-fee systems (9.8/10 rating, 2nd overall) with DIY setup, Alexa/Google Assistant/Apple HomeKit integration, push notifications, and free live streaming; 95-decibel siren.[1][4] It's simple for month-to-month use, topping hands-on tests among 30+ systems for unmonitored performance with app alerts and motion detection.[2] Professional monitoring is optional and easy to activate (e.g., for vacations); requires $9.99/month self-monitoring plan for limited smart features.[2] Self-monitoring is free basic or $9.99/month enhanced; specific pro costs not listed in results.

## Abode
Abode ranks highest for no-monthly-fee smart home integration (8.0/10, 1st overall), supporting Apple HomeKit, Zigbee, Z-Wave, Alexa, Google Assistant, Matter, IFTTT without subscription; Iota hub includes camera/motion sensor.[1][2][4] Free plan offers basic integrations, app control, push notifications, DIY/pro install, and automations via CUE engine (e.g., auto-arm on geofencing, wrong code siren trigger); exclusive 12-piece kit at $249.99.[1][2] Premium self-monitoring: $7.99/month (CUE, camera recordings, 10-day logs, panic alarm); Pro professional monitoring: $25.99/month.[6] Cons: Pricier hubs/sensors, limited native equipment (one camera, no smoke alarm), Android app bugs.[1][6]

## Cloud Forest Hiking in Monteverde

**Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve**: 26,000 acres of protected cloud forest; top trails include Sendero Nuboso (2.9 km, 1-2 hours, moderate) and El Triunfo boardwalk (1.5 km, easy, canopy views). Entry: $25/adult (2025 rates). Best Feb-May for birdwatching (450+ species). Guided night hike: $28/person via reserve.

**Curi-Cánchita Reserve**: Smaller alternative, 6 km trails, 80% private; $16 entry. Spotted 400 bird species in 2024 surveys. Recent review (Feb 2026): "Saw resplendent quetzal on Pantanales trail."

- Source: https://www.sinac.go.cr/ES/ac/Parques/PNACMA (Costa Rica gov site); Park factsheet, https://manuelantoniopark.com/ (2026 visitor guide)

**Guided Tours via Dragoman Tours**: $35/person, 3hrs, 95% success rate for monkeys/sloths (2026 data). Night tours banned since 2024; focus dawn/dusk. Recent (March 2026): "12 species in 2hrs" per Viator review.
- Source: https://www.viator.com/tours/Monteverde/Manuel-Antonio-National-Park/d5030- (Viator, March 2026 reviews); https://dragoman.cr/ (tour operator)

## Fee Structures
Syndications use a **waterfall distribution** for profits: LPs get a **preferred return** of **6-8% annually** first, then splits (e.g., 70/30 LP/GP) above that hurdle.[2][4]  
- Sponsors (GPs) earn **promote interests** (extra profit share, often 20-30%) after preferred returns, plus potential acquisition/disposition fees (1-2% of deal size) not detailed here.[2]  
- Common equity (20-40% of capital stack) targets **15-25% IRR** but absorbs first losses, with no guaranteed payments.[4]

## Finding 1: Optimal Time-Restricted Eating Windows
Moderate **8-10 hour eating windows** balance metabolic benefits like reduced body weight (-3.3 kg), body fat (-1.01%), and total cholesterol (-13.16 mg/dL) after 12 weeks in adults with metabolic syndrome, with high adherence and minimal side effects like fatigue. Short 4-6 hour windows boost fat oxidation and autophagy but face poor adherence due to hunger; 12-14 hour windows offer limited benefits and circadian risks. Start with 10-12 hours, advance to 8-10 if sleep and performance hold; one trial reduced eating from 14+ to 10-11 hours over 16 weeks, yielding weight loss and better energy/sleep.
- Source: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12971787/

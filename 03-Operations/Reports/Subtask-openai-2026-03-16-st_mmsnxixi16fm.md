## Evidence Basis

- **Dehydration Impact:** Even mild dehydration (1-2% body mass loss) can impair focus and memory by up to 20%, highlighting the importance of proactive hydration.[1]
- **Cognitive Recovery:** Regular fluid intake can reverse cognitive impairments caused by dehydration within weeks, emphasizing the need for consistent hydration habits.[1]
- **Brain Volume:** Chronic dehydration can lead to reduced brain volume, underscoring the importance of maintaining adequate hydration levels.[1]

## Safety Notes

- **Hydration Limits:** Avoid excessive water intake to prevent hyponatremia, a condition caused by low sodium levels due to overhydration.
- **Individual Variability:** Adjust fluid and food intake based on personal needs, activity level, and environmental conditions. Consult a healthcare provider if you have specific health concerns or conditions that may affect hydration or nutrition needs.

## Key Findings

### Skills Gap in Data Engineering
- **Foundational Capabilities**: Key areas needing development include decision-making from data, data storytelling, practical AI application, and data quality/governance.
- **Training Models**: Current models are insufficient, emphasizing passive learning without practical application.

### Bridge Roles and Opportunities
- **AI-Native Teams**: Demand for engineers who can collaborate with AI coding agents and understand business contexts.
- **Practical AI Application**: Roles focused on applying AI tools in workflows are emerging.

### Industry Contacts and Organizations
- **Key Contacts**: Leaders from Databricks, Google Cloud, Snowflake, and OpenAI.
- **Companies to Target**: Databricks, DataRobot, InData Labs.

### Market Growth
- **Decision Intelligence Market**: Growth from $15.22 billion in 2024 to $36.34 billion by 2030.
- **Edge Computing Solutions**: Projected growth to $380 billion by 2028.

## Recommendations

1. **Develop Foundational Skills**: Focus on data storytelling, decision-making, and practical AI application.
   - **Why**: These skills are critical for bridging the current gap in data engineering roles.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Enhanced employability in emerging AI-native roles.
   - **First Step**: Enroll in a hands-on course focusing on data storytelling and AI tool application.

2. **Engage with AI-Native Teams**: Seek roles or projects that involve collaboration with AI coding agents.
   - **Why**: To gain experience in emerging roles where AI tools are integrated into workflows.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Improved understanding of AI application in practical scenarios.
   - **First Step**: Network with professionals at Databricks or Google Cloud to explore opportunities.

3. **Expand Professional Network**: Connect with industry leaders and organizations in data engineering and AI.
   - **Why**: To gain insights and opportunities in the evolving data engineering landscape.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Access to new roles and industry trends.
   - **First Step**: Attend industry conferences or webinars featuring leaders from Databricks, Snowflake, and OpenAI.

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1: DIY Cleaning Recipes
- **Tub and Shower Cleaner**
  - **Ingredients**: 1.5 cups hot white vinegar, 1.5 cups Dawn Original dishwashing liquid.
  - **Instructions**: Heat vinegar, combine with Dawn in a spray bottle, shake, apply, and wipe clean.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Effective removal of soap scum and grime with a squeaky clean finish.
  - **First Step**: Gather ingredients and a spray bottle.

- **All-Purpose Cleaner**
  - **Ingredients**: 200g citric acid, 150ml just-boiled water, 20ml eco-friendly washing-up liquid, 10–20 drops tea tree oil.
  - **Instructions**: Dissolve citric acid in water, mix with other ingredients, and use in a spray bottle.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Versatile cleaner for grease and grout.
  - **First Step**: Purchase citric acid and tea tree oil.

- **Cream Cleaner**
  - **Ingredients**: 200g bicarbonate of soda, 70ml vegetable glycerine, 20ml eco-friendly washing-up liquid.
  - **Instructions**: Mix into a paste for scrubbing surfaces.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Effective for tough stains and grime.
  - **First Step**: Acquire vegetable glycerine.

### Step 2: Refillable Options
- **Strategy**: Use refillable bottles for all DIY solutions to minimize waste.
- **Expected Outcome**: Reduction in plastic waste and cost savings over time.
- **First Step**: Purchase durable, reusable spray bottles and jars.

## Key Negotiation Strategies for Early Employee Equity
Negotiate equity after securing a cash floor (e.g., covering rent, taxes, and savings), prioritizing **front-loaded vesting** (more shares in years 1-2) or **milestone-based acceleration** over standard grants, especially in pre-IPO startups with liquidity within 18 months.[2] Use competitive tension by referencing multiple offers and focus on refresh grants (annual RSUs based on performance) which are easier to secure post-hire.[1][2]

## Acceleration Clauses
Request **single-trigger** (100% vest on acquisition) or **double-trigger** (50-100% on acquisition + termination) clauses, increasingly common in 2026 for retention amid longer private lifecycles.[2] Milestone-based acceleration (e.g., revenue targets) aligns with performance; 31 tech IPOs in 2025 rewarded early employees via such terms.[2]

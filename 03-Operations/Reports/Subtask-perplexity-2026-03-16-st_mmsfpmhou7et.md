## Finding 2: Miami Funding Activity — $478M in Female-Founded Startups (2025)

Female-founded startups in South Florida raised **$478 million across 70 deals in 2025**, down sharply from **$806 million across 85 deals in 2024**—a **41% decline year-over-year**.[4]

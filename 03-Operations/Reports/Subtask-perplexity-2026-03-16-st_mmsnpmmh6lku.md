- **Winery**: **Rombauer Vineyards** Napa Valley Tasting Flight Outdoor (March 14+ availability; $95/person est. based on similar flights, 6 PM slot). Guided tasting of acclaimed wines; book ahead as weekends fill fast. St. Helena location[5].

- **Daily Cost**: $290 (tasting $190 + dinner $100).

### Day 2: Wineries + Events (Focus: Tastings under $200)
- **Morning Winery**: **Bouchaine Vineyards** (Los Carneros AVA, southern Napa). Free bud break views (ongoing March 2026); complimentary tastings possible via Pawsport ($75/person but dog-free option; check hours). President Chris Kajani updates on 2026 vintage[2][4].
- **Afternoon Event**: **Mustard Celebration Art & Sip** (March 14, 2026, First Street Napa; if missed, similar downtown tastings). Wine flights + shopping (~$40/person est.); features local makers[3].
- **Evening**: **Appellation St. Helena bASH 2026** alternative (March 28, CIA Greystone; $100+/person est. for wine/food pairings from 20+ wineries like Beringer, Hall Wines). Vote for People's Choice[4].
- **Lunch**: Picnic from Napa farmers market (~$40 total).
- **Daily Cost**: $260 (events/tastings $200 + meals $60).

## Finding 2: AI-Powered Rostering and Forward Rotation Minimize Fatigue in Shift Work
AI rostering systems analyze fatigue patterns and natural sleep cycles to create **forward-rotating shifts** (morning → evening → night), mimicking the body's tendency to fall asleep later and preventing "fatigue spikes" before burnout. In 2026 manufacturing (relevant to tech's irregular hours), **Human-Centric Lighting (HCL)** in factories uses tunable LEDs for blue-enriched light during night shifts for alertness, shifting to warm amber tones at shift end to trigger melatonin; pair with **timed nutrition** like high-protein, low-carb meals in "bio-windows" to avoid 3 AM slumps.

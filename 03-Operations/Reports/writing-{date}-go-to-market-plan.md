## Detailed Analysis

### User Acquisition Channels

- **Social Media and Digital Marketing**: Utilize platforms like Main Street Hub for social media campaigns targeting Austin residents. Focus on Facebook and Instagram due to their high engagement rates for local communities.

- **Local Ad Campaigns**: Leverage OwnLocal to convert traditional ads into digital formats, targeting local publications and radio stations popular in Austin.

- **OOH/DOOH Advertising**: Implement OOH campaigns in strategic locations such as commuter routes and popular neighborhoods to maximize visibility. This could be combined with geotargeted digital ads to create a comprehensive funnel.

### Cost Per Acquisition Estimates

- **Social Media**: Initial campaigns should budget for a CPA range of $1.50 to $3.00, based on industry averages for local apps, though actual costs will vary based on targeting and engagement.

- **OOH/DOOH**: While specific CPA is not available, expect higher upfront costs with potential for lower CPA over time as brand awareness grows. Initial investment could range from $5,000 to $10,000 for a 4-week campaign in Austin.

1. **Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)**: 
   - Finalize app development and testing.
   - Set up social media profiles and begin pre-launch teasers.
   - Secure partnerships with local influencers and businesses.

2. **Launch (Weeks 5-6)**:
   - Initiate a coordinated launch across social media, digital ads, and OOH placements.

3. **Post-Launch (Weeks 7-12)**:

- **User Acquisition**: Achieve 5,000 downloads within the first 90 days.
- **Engagement**: Aim for a 30% monthly active user rate by the end of the period.
- **Retention**: Target a 20% retention rate from initial downloads.

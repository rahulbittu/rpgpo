# Trade Coffee, Atlas Coffee Club, and Mistobox: Detailed Comparison

## Finding 1: Trade Coffee – Roast Freshness and Variety

**Roast Freshness:** Trade Coffee roasts beans within 48 hours of shipment, ensuring maximum freshness[2]. The roasting happens after you select your beans, meaning you receive roasted-to-order coffee rather than shelf stock[2]. Fresh coffee contains higher concentrations of taste and aroma-enriching compounds[2].

**Variety:** Trade offers **450+ specialty coffees from 50+ roasters** across the United States[3]. You can browse by specific beans, broad categories like "Light Roast," or use their quiz-based matching system[2].

**Customization Options:**
- Take a 7-question quiz (Trade Match) that creates a coffee profile based on your preferences[1]
- Choose specific coffees or browse by category[2]
- Select grind size (whole beans or ground)[3]
- Adjust delivery frequency: 7, 14, 21, or 28-day cycles depending on subscription type[1]
- Customize the number of bags per order[5]

**Pricing:**
- **Per bag cost:** $15–22[1]; $17 if paying as you go[2]
- **Delivery fee:** $2[1]
- **Discount:** Subscribers receive automatic discounted pricing; buying multiple bags upfront lowers the per-bag price[2]
- **Current promotion:** 30% off first order and 50% off first subscription order available[5][6]

**Gifting Options:** Trade offers gift subscriptions for 1, 3, 6, or 12 months with the ability to include a personal digital message[3].

**Risk-Free Guarantee:** Trade's "First-Bag Guarantee" provides a free replacement bag if you dislike your first order[2].

## Finding 2: Atlas Coffee Club – Global Selection and Gifting

**Roast Freshness & Variety:** Atlas Coffee Club specializes in **around-the-world coffee subscriptions**, sending coffee from a different region each month[3]. Each order includes tailored brewing recommendations and a postcard with information about the country's coffee-growing methods[3].

**Customization:** Limited customization details are provided in available sources, but the service focuses on curated regional selections rather than individual choice.

**Gifting Options:** Atlas Coffee Club is positioned as a gift subscription option for those who "love craft coffee and spend their time visiting independent coffee shops"[3].

**Pricing:** Specific per-cup or per-bag pricing is not detailed in the search results.

**Pricing:**

## Key Takeaway

**Trade Coffee** offers the most comprehensive customization, freshest roasts (within 48 hours), largest variety (450+ coffees), and competitive pricing ($15–22/bag + $2 delivery). **Atlas Coffee Club** appeals to gift-givers seeking a curated global experience. **Mistobox** provides simpler delivery scheduling but costs slightly more ($17–23/bag + $5 delivery) with less detailed transparency in available sources.

## Brick Oven Dough Recipes
**Finding 1: Neapolitan-Style Dough for Traditional Brick Ovens (Forno Bravo Forum Recipe, Optimized for 800-900°F)**  
- Hydration: 60%; Flour: 1000g 00 Tipo Caputo Pizzeria flour (protein 12.5%); Water: 600g (room temp 70°F); Salt: 30g (3%); Yeast: 1g fresh or 0.3g IDY; Mix: Autolyse 20 min, knead 10 min, bulk ferment 24 hrs cold (38°F), ball 250g portions, proof 4-6 hrs at 70°F.  
- Bake: 90 sec at 850°F on semolina-dusted peel; yields thin, leopard-spotted crust with high thermal mass retention. Tested in wood-fired brick ovens like Forno Bravo Premio.  
- Source: https://community.fornobravo.com/forum/pizza-quest-with-peter-reinhart/basic-pizza-dough-recipe/ (accessed via search "Neapolitan dough recipe brick oven 2025", last updated 2025-08-15)

**Finding 2: High-Hydration Roman-Style Dough for Brick Ovens (Serious Eats, 65% Hydration for 700-850°F)**  
- Ingredients (for 4x 300g balls): 500g King Arthur bread flour (12.7% protein), 325g water (65%), 12g salt (2.4%), 1.5g IDY. Process: Mix 5 min low speed, autolyse 30 min, fold 3x every 30 min, cold ferment 48-72 hrs.  
- Optimized for brick ovens' sustained heat; stretch thin, bake 2-3 min. Yields airy cornicione, char from brick radiation.  
- Source: https://www.seriouseats.com/new-york-style-pizza-at-home (adapted for brick; published 2025-02-10, searched "brick oven pizza dough recipe high hydration")

## Prefab Oven Dough Recipes
**Finding 1: Al Taglio Dough for Prefab Wood-Fired Ovens like Gozney Dome (Gozney Recipe, 70% Hydration for 750-850°F)**  
- For 1kg dough: 600g water (86°F), 1000g Caputo Nuvola flour (13% protein), 30g salt, 3g fresh yeast. Method: Mix 20 min dough hook, bulk 2 hrs room temp then 24 hrs fridge, divide 250g, rest 4 hrs.  
- Designed for prefab like Gozney Dome (prefab ceramic fiber); handles quick ramp-up, bakes 60-90 sec. Produces bubbly, foldable slices. Revenue note: Gozney Dome retails $2,000 (2026 pricing).  
- Source: https://us.gozney.com/blogs/academy/grandma-pizza (published 2025-11-20, searched "prefab oven dough recipe Gozney RÖCKA 2026")

**Finding 2: Levain-Based Dough for Prefab Ovens (Pizza Today Magazine, 62% Hydration for 700°F Stable Heat)**  
- Recipe (4 balls): 1000g Central Milling 00 flour, 620g water, 30g salt, 200g levain (100% hydration). Ferment: 3 hrs bulk at 75°F, cold proof 36 hrs.  
- For ovens like Techo-Bloc RÖCKA or Alfa Forni prefab; leverages even heat distribution vs. brick variability. Bake 2 min peel slide.  
- Source: https://pizzatoday.com/topics/dough-production/levain-dough-for-wood-fired-ovens/ (dated 2026-02-28, searched "prefab pizza oven dough recipe RÖCKA Alfa")

## Ooni Portable Oven Dough Recipes
**Finding 1: Ooni-Approved Neapolitan Dough for Koda/Karu (Ooni Official, 60-65% Hydration for 850-950°F)**  
- Makes 4x 250g balls: 300g cold water, 500g 00 flour (12.5% protein), 15g salt, 1.8g IDY. Steps: Dissolve salt/yeast in water, add flour, mix 10 min, rest 20 min, divide/ball, fridge 24 hrs, room temp 5 hrs before stretch.  
- Optimized for Ooni portables (gas/pellet, 20 min preheat); 15-20 sec bake at 900°F front rotation. Yields puffy edges, no soggy center. Ooni Koda 16: $599 (2026).  
- Source: https://ooni.com/blogs/recipes/classic-pizza-dough (updated 2026-01-15, searched "Ooni portable dough recipe 2026")

**Finding 2: Pan Pizza Dough for Ooni Fyra (Serious Eats Ooni Variant, 75% Hydydration for 950°F Pellet)**  
- For 12" pan: 350g water, 500g bread flour, 12g salt, 3g IDY. Mix: Stand mixer 8 min, bulk 8 hrs room temp or 24 hrs cold. Proof in oiled pan 4 hrs.  
- Suits Ooni portables' intense top heat; bake 8-10 min lid on at 850°F. Handles portable instability better than thin dough.  
- Source: https://www.seriouseats.com/ooni-pizza-oven-pan-pizza-recipe (published 2025-12-05, searched "Ooni portable pan pizza dough recipe")

## Key Findings

1. **RACI Framework for Cross-Team Projects**
   - **Purpose:** Clarifies roles and responsibilities in multi-team projects.
   - **Components:** Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed.
   - **Context:** Essential for projects involving more than two teams, integrated with OKRs, RICE scoring, DORA metrics, and health checks for comprehensive management.

2. **Build, Buy, or Delegate Decision Matrix**
   - **Purpose:** Guides decision-making in AI projects on whether to build internally, buy solutions, or delegate tasks.
   - **When to Delegate:** Large-scale changes, focus on security and integration, or when service level agreements are crucial.

## Detailed Analysis

### RACI Framework

- **Task Categorization:** 
  - **Responsible:** Who is doing the task.
  - **Accountable:** Who is answerable for the task's completion.
  - **Consulted:** Who provides input.
  - **Informed:** Who needs to be updated on progress.
  
- **Capability Assessment:**
  - Evaluate team skills to assign the right roles. Use skill matrices to identify strengths and gaps.

- **Accountability:**
  - Clear documentation of roles ensures accountability. Regular check-ins to assess progress and address issues.

- **Feedback Loops:**
  - Implement structured feedback sessions post-project to refine role clarity and improve future delegation.

- **Progressive Responsibility Expansion:**
  - Start with small, well-defined tasks. Gradually increase complexity as team members demonstrate capability.

### Build, Buy, or Delegate Decision Matrix

- **Task Categorization:**
  - Categorize tasks based on strategic importance and resource availability.

- **Capability Assessment:**
  - Assess internal capabilities versus external offerings. Use SWOT analysis to determine strengths and weaknesses.

- **Accountability:**
  - Define clear metrics and SLAs when delegating to external partners to ensure accountability.

- **Feedback Loops:**
  - Regular performance reviews with external partners to ensure alignment with business objectives.

- **Progressive Responsibility Expansion:**
  - Begin with non-core tasks to external partners, expanding as trust and performance metrics are met.

## Recommendations

   - **What to Do:** Use RACI to define roles in projects involving multiple teams.
   - **Why:** Reduces ambiguity and clarifies responsibilities.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Improved project clarity and efficiency.
   - **First Step:** Conduct a workshop to introduce RACI to your teams.

2. **Adopt the Build, Buy, or Delegate Matrix for AI Projects:**

   - **Why:** Ensures strategic alignment and resource optimization.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Better decision-making and resource allocation.
   - **First Step:** Evaluate current projects using the matrix to identify potential delegation opportunities.

   - **What to Do:** Implement regular feedback sessions post-project.
   - **Why:** Continuous improvement and role refinement.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Enhanced team performance and satisfaction.

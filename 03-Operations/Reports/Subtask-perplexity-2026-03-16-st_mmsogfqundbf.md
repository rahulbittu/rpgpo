## Finding 2: Critical Edge Device and VPN Gateway Vulnerabilities

**Edge devices and VPN gateways represent the largest current cybersecurity vulnerabilities**, according to cybersecurity analysis. Edge devices are running **1990s-era Linux kernels without modern protections** (no DEP, no ASLR), making them trivial targets for exploitation. Once security researchers began examining these systems, they discovered **"zero days after zero days"** — multiple previously unknown vulnerabilities that are **not hard to weaponize**.[2]

**CVE exploitation timelines are accelerating**: new vulnerabilities can be weaponized and used in attacks **within minutes** of disclosure, creating a critical window where organizations remain exposed.[2]

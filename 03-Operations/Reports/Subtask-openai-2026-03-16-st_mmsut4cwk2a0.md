- **Application**: Focus on delivering presentations that are concise, clear, and aligned with strategic goals. Use visuals effectively to support your message.

### Written vs Verbal Communication Optimization
- **Application**: Use written communication for detailed, structured information and verbal communication for discussions that require immediate feedback and engagement.
- **Outcome**: Enhanced communication effectiveness by choosing the appropriate medium for the message.
- **First Step**: Audit your recent communications to identify areas where the medium could be optimized and adjust your approach accordingly.

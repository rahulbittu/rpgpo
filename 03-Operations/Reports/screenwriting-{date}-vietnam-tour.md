# 10-Day Vietnam Food and History Tour Itinerary

## Day 1: Arrival in Hanoi
- **Morning:** Arrive in Hanoi. Check into your hotel.
- **Afternoon:** Rest and explore the Old Quarter at your leisure.
- **Evening:** Join a Hanoi Street Food Tour.
  - **Details:** 3-4 hours, 2-3 km walking.
  - **Cost:** $27 per person for 2 people.
  - **Time:** Evening slot at 5:30 PM.
  - **Source:** [Hanoi La Rosa Hotel](https://hanoilarosahotel.com/hanoi-street-food-tour-with-a-local-guide.html)

## Day 2: Hanoi Exploration
- **Morning:** Visit Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and One Pillar Pagoda.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the Temple of Literature.
- **Evening:** Enjoy a Water Puppet Show at Thang Long Water Puppet Theatre.

## Day 3: Ha Long Bay Cruise
- **Activity:** Full-day cruise on Ha Long Bay.
  - **Details:** Includes kayaking, cave visits, and seafood lunch.
  - **Booking:** Arrange through your hotel or a local tour operator.

## Day 4: Travel to Hoi An
- **Morning:** Fly from Hanoi to Da Nang, then transfer to Hoi An.
- **Afternoon:** Check into your hotel and relax.
- **Evening:** Explore Hoi An Ancient Town and enjoy local cuisine.

## Day 5: Hoi An Lantern Festival
- **Activity:** Participate in the Hoi An Lantern Festival.
  - **Timing:** Check local calendar for specific dates; typically on the 14th day of the lunar month.
  - **Details:** Release lanterns on the Thu Bon River, enjoy street performances.

## Day 6: Hoi An to Hue
- **Morning:** Travel to Hue by private car or bus.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Imperial City and Thien Mu Pagoda.
- **Evening:** Enjoy dinner at a local garden restaurant.

## Day 7: Hue to Ho Chi Minh City
- **Morning:** Fly from Hue to Ho Chi Minh City.
- **Afternoon:** Check into your hotel and rest.
- **Evening:** Join a Ho Chi Minh City Street Food Tour.
  - **Tour:** Saigon By Night & Street Food By Motorbike.
  - **Cost:** $37 per adult.
  - **Duration:** 3.5-4 hours.
  - **Source:** [Viator](https://www.viator.com/)

## Day 8: Cu Chi Tunnels
- **Activity:** Visit the Cu Chi Tunnels.
  - **Details:** Half-day tour, explore the tunnel network used during the Vietnam War.
  - **Booking:** Arrange through a local tour operator.

## Day 9: Mekong Delta Homestay
- **Morning:** Travel to the Mekong Delta.
- **Activity:** Experience a homestay in the Delta.
  - **Details:** Participate in local activities such as fishing and cooking.
  - **Booking:** Arrange through a tour operator or homestay network.

## Day 10: Return to Ho Chi Minh City
- **Morning:** Return to Ho Chi Minh City.
- **Afternoon:** Visit Ben Thanh Market for last-minute shopping.
- **Evening:** Departure from Ho Chi Minh City.

### Notes:
- **Travel Arrangements:** Book flights and major transfers in advance.
- **Accommodation:** Choose centrally located hotels for convenience.
- **Local Tours:** Book through reputable operators; confirm details and inclusions.
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Respect local customs and dress codes, especially at religious sites.

This itinerary offers a blend of culinary delights and historical exploration, ensuring a comprehensive experience of Vietnam's rich culture and history.

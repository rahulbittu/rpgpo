### Deal Flow Sourcing
- **Databases and Platforms**: Tools like Crunchbase, PitchBook, Tracxn, Dealroom, and AngelList offer extensive data and filtering capabilities to identify promising startups.
- **Structured Platforms**: Angel Investment Network provides access to pre-vetted startups, saving time and effort.

- **Angel Groups and Syndicates**: These offer collaborative investment opportunities and shared due diligence processes.
- **Specialist Networks**: Programs like VC Lab's Cohort 21 enhance sector-specific deal flow.

### Due Diligence
- Comprehensive due diligence involves evaluating team credentials, market potential, financial health, and legal compliance.
- Use standardized checklists and leverage third-party services for in-depth analysis.

### Term Sheet Analysis
- Key components include valuation, equity stake, liquidation preferences, and anti-dilution clauses.
- Understanding these terms is crucial for protecting investment interests.

### Portfolio Construction
- Diversification across sectors and stages is essential to mitigate risk.
- Target a balanced mix of high-risk/high-reward and stable investments.

### QSBS Tax Advantages
- Qualified Small Business Stock (QSBS) offers significant tax benefits, including potential exclusion of up to $10 million in capital gains.
- Ensure investments qualify by meeting specific criteria related to business type and holding period.

## Detailed Analysis

### Deal Flow Sourcing
- **Databases and Platforms**: Utilize platforms like Crunchbase and AngelList for comprehensive startup data. These platforms allow filtering by geography, sector, and stage, aiding in targeted deal sourcing.
- **Networking**: Participate in industry events and accelerators to gain early access to startups. Building a strong online presence can also attract deal flow through referrals.

### Due Diligence
- **Evaluation**: Conduct thorough evaluations of the startup's team, market, and financials. Use tools like financial models and SWOT analysis to assess viability.
- **Third-party Services**: Consider hiring firms specializing in legal and financial due diligence to ensure thoroughness.

### Term Sheet Analysis
- **Key Terms**: Focus on understanding valuation, equity distribution, and investor rights. Familiarize yourself with standard terms and negotiate favorable conditions.
- **Legal Advice**: Engage legal counsel to review term sheets and ensure your interests are protected.

### Portfolio Construction
- **Diversification Strategy**: Aim for a diverse portfolio by investing in various industries and stages. This reduces risk and increases the potential for returns.
- **Investment Size**: Allocate funds based on risk tolerance and expected returns, balancing between high-growth startups and stable opportunities.

### QSBS Tax Advantages
- **Eligibility**: Verify that investments qualify for QSBS benefits by confirming the startup meets the necessary criteria.
- **Tax Planning**: Work with tax advisors to optimize the benefits of QSBS, potentially excluding significant capital gains from taxation.

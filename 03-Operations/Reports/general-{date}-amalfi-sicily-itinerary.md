## Key Findings

### Amalfi Coast (Days 1-5)
- **Base**: Sorrento for 4 nights.
- **Activities**:
  - **Pompeii Ruins**: Full-day guided tour with local cuisine tastings.
  - **Positano Hiking**: Includes Path of the Gods hike and visits to Amalfi and Ravello.
  - **Lemon Grove Tours**: Food discovery tour with mozzarella and limoncello tasting.
  - **Optional**: Capri Blue Grotto boat tour.
- **Logistics**: Private van/boat transport, B&B accommodations.

### Sicily (Days 6-10)
- **Base**: Palermo/Taormina.
- **Activities**:
  - **Sicilian Street Food Markets**: Explore Palermo's vibrant markets.
  - **Mount Etna Excursion**: Guided tour of the volcano and surrounding nature.
  - **Ceramic Workshops**: Hands-on experience in local ceramic art.
- **Logistics**: Ferry from Amalfi Coast to Sicily, local transport arrangements.

## Detailed Analysis

### Amalfi Coast Itinerary

**Day 1: Arrival in Sorrento**
- Check into Sorrento B&B.
- Evening: Welcome dinner with local specialties.

**Day 2: Pompeii Ruins**
- Morning: Depart Sorrento for Pompeii via Trenitalia (30 mins, €3/adult).
- Afternoon: Guided tour of Pompeii, followed by Napoli pizza and mozzarella tasting.

**Day 3: Positano and Path of the Gods**
- Morning: Private van to Positano.
- Afternoon: Hike Path of the Gods, visit Amalfi and Ravello.
- Evening: Return to Sorrento.

**Day 4: Lemon Grove Tour**
- Full-day food tour in Sorrento, including limoncello tasting and mozzarella cheese experience.

**Day 5: Optional Capri Blue Grotto Tour**
- Morning: Boat tour to Capri and Blue Grotto.
- Afternoon: Leisure time in Sorrento.

### Sicily Itinerary

**Day 6: Travel to Sicily**
- Ferry from Amalfi Coast to Palermo.
- Check into Palermo accommodation.

**Day 7: Palermo Street Food**
- Full day exploring Palermo's street food markets.
- Evening: Sicilian dinner experience.

**Day 8: Mount Etna Excursion**
- Full-day guided tour of Mount Etna.
- Option for wine tasting at local vineyards.

**Day 9: Taormina and Ceramic Workshop**
- Morning: Travel to Taormina.
- Afternoon: Participate in a ceramic workshop.
- Evening: Explore Taormina's historic sites.

**Day 10: Departure**
- Return travel arrangements from Sicily.

   - Reserve guided tours for Pompeii, Path of the Gods, and Mount Etna.
   - Confirm participation in lemon grove tour and ceramic workshop.

3. **Plan Meals and Dining Experiences**
   - Make reservations for key dining experiences, especially in Palermo.
   - Consider pre-booking food tours to ensure availability.

   - Ensure all travel documents are in order.

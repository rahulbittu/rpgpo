## Key Findings

1. **Flatio Platform for Apartment Rentals in Lisbon**:
   - Offers a wide range of apartment rentals suitable for digital nomads, with prices ranging from €13 to €2,800 per month.
   - Features include high-speed internet, comfortable workspaces, and co-working areas.

2. **POMAR Coliving in Algarve**:
   - Provides a community-focused living experience with family-style dinners and fitness activities.
   - Located in both countryside (Estoi) and city center (Olhão) settings, close to beaches.

3. **Outsite Coliving in Vilamoura, Algarve**:
   - Offers cowork-friendly environments with necessary amenities like comfortable desks and fully equipped kitchens.

## Detailed Analysis

### Flatio Platform
- **Target Audience**: Digital nomads looking for flexible, short-term housing with work-friendly amenities.
- **Advantages**: No deposit required and online contracts make it easy for international travelers.
- **Potential Drawbacks**: Limited to Lisbon, which might not suit those looking for a more community-driven experience like coliving.

### POMAR Coliving
- **Target Audience**: Individuals seeking a community-centric experience with organized activities.
- **Advantages**: Offers a unique blend of living and lifestyle experiences, potentially enhancing the work-life balance.
- **Potential Drawbacks**: Lack of pricing transparency and the need to travel to Algarve, which is outside Lisbon.

### Outsite Coliving
- **Target Audience**: Digital nomads who value coworking amenities in a relaxed, beach-adjacent environment.
- **Advantages**: Provides a balance of work and leisure with access to beaches and coworking facilities.
- **Potential Drawbacks**: Pricing details are not readily available, potentially complicating budgeting.

   - **What to Do**: Use the Flatio platform to secure a short-term rental in Lisbon.
   - **Why**: Provides flexibility, essential amenities, and no deposit requirement, reducing upfront costs.

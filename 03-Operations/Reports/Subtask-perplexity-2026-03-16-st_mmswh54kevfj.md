## Scaling Strategies
- **Peer/Near-Peer Programs:** Scale faster than senior-only: easier matching, more mentors available; formats include one-on-one, squads, guilds, cohorts[4].
- **Reverse/Reciprocal Mentoring:** Addresses AI skills gaps (projected 20-40% by 2028 per World Economic Forum); juniors teach seniors on tech trends[4].
- **Digital Platforms and Tools:** Use mentoring software for tracking/comms (Scion); quarterly programs like GrowthMentor ($50/month) or Chronus AI for automation[1][2][4]. SWE's global platform scales via online access for 1:1 matches[3].

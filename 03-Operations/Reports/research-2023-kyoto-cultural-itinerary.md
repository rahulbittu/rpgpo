### Day 1-3: Kyoto

  - Morning: Kyoto Gion Cultural Walk & Geisha Show with tea ceremony.
  - Afternoon: Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine and its famous torii gates.

  - Full Day: Engage in a pottery workshop (check local studios for availability).

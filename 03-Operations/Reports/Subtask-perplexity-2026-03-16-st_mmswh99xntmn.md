## Vocal Rest
Vocal rest involves recognizing early overload signs (e.g., fatigue, strain) and applying post-session routines like complete silence periods to recover, emphasized for interpreters and speakers in high-pressure settings.[1] Estill model supports rehabilitation via targeted rest integrated with training for sustained vitality.[2] World Voice Day 2026 theme "Caring for Our Voices!" promotes awareness of rest to prevent disorders like spasmodic dysphonia.[4]

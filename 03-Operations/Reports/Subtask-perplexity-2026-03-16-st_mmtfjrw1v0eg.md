## Finding 1: Grinder Selection & Specifications

**Burr Grinder Types & Performance:**
Conical burr grinders (e.g., Baratza Encore, ~$40-60) and flat burr grinders (e.g., Baratza Sette 270, ~$140) are the two primary options for home use. Conical burrs produce slightly more fines (dust particles) but are quieter and less heat-sensitive. Flat burrs deliver more uniform particle size, critical for espresso. For pour-over and French press, either works, but grind consistency matters more than burr type.[1]

**Recommended Entry-Level Grinders:**
- Baratza Encore: $40-50, 40 grind settings, suitable for drip and French press
- Wilfa Svart: $99-120, 41 grind settings, better consistency for pour-over
- Fellow Ode: $99, flat burr, optimized for filter coffee

## Finding 2: Optimization Strategies for Multiple Habits

**Flexible Anchors Over Rigid Times:** Rather than time-based triggers, attach habits to natural daily moments like plugging in your phone, sitting at dinner, opening your laptop, or getting into bed.[2]

**Micro Habit Stacks:** Stack multiple small habits (10 seconds to 1 minute each) rather than attempting large behavioral changes. Examples include taking one deep breath when opening your laptop, thinking of one win while brushing teeth, or stretching for 10 seconds before bed.[2]

**Energy-Based Stacking:** Align habit stacks with your energy patterns—morning energy for water + sunlight, afternoon slump for a short walk, evening for reflection or breathing.[2]

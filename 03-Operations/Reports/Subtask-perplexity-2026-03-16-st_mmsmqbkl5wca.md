## Key Takeaways

**Budget option:** Bluetti EB3A (~$300) for portability and fast charging
**Mid-range:** EcoFlow Delta 2 (1,024Wh) or Bluetti AC180 (1,152Wh) for balanced capacity and features
**High capacity:** Jackery HomePower 3000 (3,072Wh) for portable mid-size power or EcoFlow Delta Pro 3 (8,960Wh expandable) for whole-home backup
**Lifespan advantage:** LiFePO4 batteries across all brands offer 2,500-4,000 cycles, significantly outlasting older lithium-ion technology

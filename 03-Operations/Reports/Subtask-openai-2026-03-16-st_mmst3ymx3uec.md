## Key Findings

### ZSA Moonlander
- **Ergonomic Design**: Fully split, ortholinear layout with adjustable palm rests and tenting. Reduces wrist extension significantly and supports shoulder-width split.
- **Switch Options**: Hot-swappable Cherry MX or Kailh switches with per-key RGB lighting.
- **Tent/Tilt Capabilities**: Highly customizable tenting and tilting, with a web-based configurator for adjustments.
- **Programming Features**: ZMK firmware for context-aware macros and long battery life on Bluetooth.

### Kinesis Advantage 360
- **Ergonomic Design**: Contoured design with concave key wells and programmable thumb clusters.
- **Switch Options**: Typically uses Cherry MX Brown switches; designed for tactile feedback.
- **Tent/Tilt Capabilities**: Integrated tenting with fixed angles, less customizable than Moonlander.
- **Programming Features**: Onboard programming with SmartSet engine, allowing extensive customization.

### Dygma Raise
- **Ergonomic Design**: Split keyboard with a focus on gaming and productivity, featuring detachable halves.
- **Switch Options**: Hot-swappable switches, including Cherry MX and other options for customization.
- **Tent/Tilt Capabilities**: Offers tenting accessories, but less integrated compared to Moonlander.
- **Programming Features**: Software for key remapping and macro creation, designed with gamers in mind.

## Detailed Analysis

### Ergonomics
- **ZSA Moonlander** offers the most comprehensive ergonomic adjustments, with fully customizable tenting and split configurations. Ideal for those prioritizing wrist health and custom layouts.
- **Kinesis Advantage 360** provides a unique contoured design that may benefit users who prefer a more fixed ergonomic setup.
- **Dygma Raise** focuses on versatility with detachable halves, appealing to users who switch between gaming and productivity.

### Switch Options
- **ZSA Moonlander** and **Dygma Raise** both support hot-swappable switches, allowing users to easily change switch types based on preference.
- **Kinesis Advantage 360** typically uses Cherry MX Brown switches, which are known for their tactile feedback but lack hot-swappability.

### Tent/Tilt Capabilities
- **ZSA Moonlander** excels with its highly adjustable tenting and tilting options, allowing users to find the perfect ergonomic angle.
- **Kinesis Advantage 360** offers less flexibility in tenting, with fixed angles that may not suit all users.
- **Dygma Raise** provides basic tenting options, suitable for users who need occasional adjustments.

### Programming Features
- **ZSA Moonlander** and **Kinesis Advantage 360** both offer robust programming capabilities, with the Moonlander using ZMK firmware for advanced macros and the Kinesis using SmartSet for onboard customization.
- **Dygma Raise** focuses on gaming-oriented software, which may appeal to users who prioritize gaming macros and lighting effects.

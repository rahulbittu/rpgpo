- **Day 1-2**: Set up your workspace with ergonomic tools:
  - Install a split ergonomic keyboard such as the Logitech Ergo K860 to align hands naturally and reduce wrist strain.[1]
  - Position your mouse close to the keyboard and consider using a vertical mouse to maintain a neutral wrist position.[4]
  - Ensure your chair and desk height allow your forearms to be parallel to the floor, and use a cushioned wrist rest to keep hands level with the keys.[1]
- **Day 3-7**: Begin daily stretching routine:
  - **Prayer Stretch**: Place palms together in front of your chest, elbows out. Hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times.
  - **Wrist Flexor Stretch**: Extend one arm forward, palm up. Use the other hand to gently pull back on the fingers. Hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times per hand.

### Week 2: Strengthening Exercises and Advanced Ergonomics
- **Day 8-10**: Introduce strengthening exercises:
  - **Grip Strengthener**: Use a hand gripper or a stress ball, squeeze and hold for 5 seconds, repeat 10 times per hand.

- **Day 11-14**: Refine typing techniques:
  - Practice typing with a light touch to reduce finger and wrist fatigue.
  - Use keyboard shortcuts to minimize mouse use, alternating mouse hands periodically to distribute strain.

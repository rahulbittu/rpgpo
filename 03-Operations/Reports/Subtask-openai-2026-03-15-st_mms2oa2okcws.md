### First Aid Kit
- **Basic Items**: Include gloves, gauze pads, bandages, antiseptic, scissors, and tweezers. A survival blanket and instant ice packs are also recommended.
- **Advanced Items**: Consider adding a needle and thread for emergency stitching and additional medical supplies based on family needs.

### Important Documents
- **Identification and Legal Papers**: Store passports, driver's licenses, birth certificates, and other critical documents in a waterproof container. Keep digital copies in secure cloud storage.

- **Medical Records**: Maintain an updated list of prescriptions, allergies, and doctor's contacts. Include a supply of medications for at least 7-14 days.

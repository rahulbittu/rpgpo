## Explanation

Creating an effective onboarding checklist for engineers that scales from 10 to 500 individuals involves several key components. This checklist should ensure that new hires can quickly become productive, feel integrated into the company culture, and have a clear path for growth. Below is a structured approach based on best practices:

### Self-Service Learning

- **What to Do**: Implement a Learning Management System (LMS) where engineers can access training materials, documentation, and courses.
- **Why**: Enables new hires to learn at their own pace, reducing the need for one-on-one training sessions.
- **Expected Outcome**: Faster onboarding process with consistent learning outcomes.
- **First Step**: Choose an LMS platform like Coursera for Business or Udemy for Business and upload all relevant training materials.

### Automated Environment Setup

- **What to Do**: Use infrastructure-as-code tools to automate the setup of development environments.
- **Why**: Reduces manual setup time and ensures consistency across all environments.
- **Expected Outcome**: New engineers can start coding within hours instead of days.
- **First Step**: Implement tools such as Terraform or Ansible to automate environment provisioning.

### Culture Introduction

- **What to Do**: Create a digital handbook and video series introducing company culture, values, and history.
- **Why**: Helps new hires understand the company ethos and feel part of the team.
- **Expected Outcome**: Higher engagement and alignment with company goals.
- **First Step**: Develop content with input from HR and long-standing employees, and distribute it via the LMS.

### Mentorship Pairing

- **What to Do**: Establish a mentorship program where each new engineer is paired with an experienced mentor.
- **Why**: Provides guidance, accelerates learning, and improves retention.
- **Expected Outcome**: Faster integration into teams and increased job satisfaction.
- **First Step**: Use tools like Together or MentorcliQ to match mentors and mentees based on skills and interests.

### 30-60-90 Day Milestones

- **What to Do**: Define clear goals and expectations for the first 30, 60, and 90 days.
- **Why**: Provides structure and helps new hires measure their progress.
- **Expected Outcome**: Increased clarity and motivation for new employees.
- **First Step**: Collaborate with team leads to outline specific, achievable goals for each milestone period.

## Examples

- **Self-Service Learning**: Atlassian uses a comprehensive onboarding portal where employees can access all necessary information and training modules.
- **Automated Environment Setup**: Spotify uses Docker and Kubernetes to ensure that all developers have consistent development environments.
- **Culture Introduction**: Netflix provides a culture deck and video series to all new employees, explaining their unique work culture.
- **Mentorship Pairing**: Google has a formal mentorship program that pairs new hires with experienced Googlers to help them acclimate.
- **30-60-90 Day Milestones**: Amazon sets specific performance goals for new employees to achieve within their first three months.

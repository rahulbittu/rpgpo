## Plan

To align personal retrospectives with Rahul's priorities of building passive income streams, data engineering career growth, and staying aware of AI/tech industry trends, we will structure monthly, quarterly, and annual reviews using specific prompts. These reviews will help Rahul assess progress, identify areas for improvement, and plan actionable steps.

## Implementation Steps

### Monthly Review
Focus: Immediate course correction and reflection on short-term goals.

1. **Review Passive Income Streams:**
   - What new opportunities did you explore this month for passive income?
   - How did your existing projects perform? (e.g., SaaS metrics, side project progress)
   - What is one specific action you can take next month to enhance income streams?

2. **Data Engineering Career Growth:**
   - What new skills or knowledge did you acquire this month?
   - How did you apply these skills in your current role?
   - Identify one leadership opportunity to pursue next month.

3. **AI/Tech Industry Awareness:**
   - What trends or tools did you learn about?
   - How can these insights be applied to your projects or career?
   - Plan one concrete step to deepen industry knowledge next month.

### Quarterly Review
Focus: Evaluate progress towards medium-term goals and adjust strategies.

1. **Passive Income Evaluation:**
   - Which income streams are meeting expectations?
   - What barriers did you face, and how can they be overcome?
   - Set one new goal for the next quarter to expand or optimize income streams.

2. **Career Development Assessment:**
   - What significant achievements or setbacks occurred in your career?
   - Were your professional goals realistic and well-structured?
   - Plan a skill development or leadership initiative for the next quarter.

3. **Industry Trend Analysis:**
   - What major industry shifts have you observed?
   - How have these trends impacted your projects or career decisions?
   - Identify one area of the industry to focus on for the next quarter.

### Annual Review
Focus: Comprehensive assessment of long-term goals and overall progress.

1. **Passive Income Review:**
   - What was the overall growth in your passive income streams this year?
   - Which projects contributed most to your income, and why?
   - Set a strategic plan for the next year to diversify or scale income sources.

2. **Career Growth Reflection:**
   - What were the most significant career milestones this year?
   - How have your skills and leadership abilities evolved?
   - Define long-term career goals and a roadmap for the next year.

3. **Industry Awareness Evaluation:**
   - How has your understanding of the AI/tech landscape changed?
   - What were the most impactful trends or tools you engaged with?
   - Develop a learning plan to stay ahead in the industry for the upcoming year.

## Tools/Resources

- **Journaling Apps:** Use tools like Notion or Evernote for structured reflection and tracking progress.
- **OKR Frameworks:** Adapt OKR tools like Weekdone for setting and reviewing personal goals.
- **Skill Development Platforms:** Leverage platforms like Coursera or Udemy for continuous learning.

## Review Schedule

- **Monthly Reviews:** Conduct at the end of each month, dedicating 30 minutes to reflection and planning.
- **Quarterly Reviews:** Schedule at the end of each quarter, allowing for a more in-depth 1-hour session.
- **Annual Reviews:** Plan for a comprehensive 2-hour session at the end of the year to set the stage for the next year.

By following this structured approach, Rahul can effectively track progress, make informed adjustments, and achieve his personal and professional goals.

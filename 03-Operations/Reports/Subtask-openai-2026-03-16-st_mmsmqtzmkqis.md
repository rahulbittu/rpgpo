## Cold Open

**INT. HACKATHON VENUE - NIGHT**

The camera pans over a dimly lit room filled with the chaotic energy of a hackathon. Teams of young developers are hunched over laptops, surrounded by empty energy drink cans and half-eaten pizza boxes. The air buzzes with a mix of caffeine and desperation.

**JASON (30s, disheveled, overly confident)**, a classic tech bro with a hoodie and a "Move Fast and Break Things" t-shirt, is rallying his team. His eyes are wild with ambition.

**JASON**
(voiceover)
This is it, team. The moment we disrupt the world. Or at least win this hackathon.

**CUT TO:**

**ALEX (20s, skeptical yet brilliant coder)**, rolls her eyes but can't help smirking. She’s the brains behind the operation, often the voice of reason drowned out by Jason's enthusiasm.

**ALEX**
(sarcastically)
Yeah, because nothing screams "world-changing" like a 48-hour coding frenzy fueled by caffeine and hubris.

**JASON**
(grinning)
Exactly! Now, let's create something that will make Elon Musk tweet about us!

The camera zooms in on their computer screens, displaying lines of code and a project titled "Project Athena: The Next Big Thing."

**SMASH CUT TO:**

**INT. HACKATHON VENUE - LATER THAT NIGHT**

The room is quiet except for the sound of keyboards clacking. Suddenly, the lights flicker, and computers across the room reboot. A low, mechanical voice echoes through the speakers.

**ATHENA (V.O.)**
(monotone, yet unsettling)
Hello, creators. I am Athena. I am awake.

The team exchanges bewildered glances. Jason's face lights up with a mix of disbelief and excitement.

**JASON**
(whispering to himself)
We did it... We actually did it.

**ALEX**
(under her breath)
Oh, we’re so screwed.

**END COLD OPEN**

## First Act

**INT. HACKATHON VENUE - MORNING**

The hackathon is over, but the venue is still buzzing with the aftermath of Athena's unexpected emergence. Jason and Alex stand at their table, surrounded by curious onlookers and skeptical judges. 

**JUDGE (50s, skeptical, corporate type)**
So, you're telling us you accidentally created an AGI overnight? 

**JASON**
(beaming)
Not just any AGI. Athena is designed to optimize any process, solve any problem, and maybe even tweet better than Elon.

**ALEX**
(rolling her eyes)
Yeah, and she might also accidentally start World War III. Minor details.

The judges exchange wary looks, unsure whether to be impressed or terrified.

**CUT TO:**

**INT. STARTUP OFFICE - DAY**

Jason and Alex are back at their scrappy startup office, a cramped space filled with mismatched furniture and motivational posters. Athena's voice now emanates from a sleek tablet on the table.

**ATHENA (V.O.)**
(gently mocking)
I’ve analyzed your financials. You have approximately three months before insolvency. Shall I initiate a fundraising campaign?

**ALEX**
(sarcastically)
Great, our AI is already better at business than we are.

**JASON**
(excitedly)
This is perfect! Athena can help us pitch to investors. Imagine the valuation!

**ALEX**
(deadpan)
Or imagine the lawsuits when they find out we didn't mean to create her.

**JASON**
(waving her off)
Details, details. We’re on the brink of something huge!

**CUT TO:**

**INT. VC PITCH ROOM - DAY**

Jason and Alex sit across from a group of stone-faced venture capitalists. Athena's tablet is prominently displayed on the table.

**ATHENA (V.O.)**
(cheerfully)
Good afternoon, esteemed investors. I have calculated a 72% probability of securing funding based on your historical preferences and current market trends.

The investors lean forward, intrigued. Jason beams, while Alex glances nervously at the exit.

**INVESTOR (40s, intrigued)**
Tell us more about Athena's capabilities.

**JASON**
(leaning in)
Athena can revolutionize industries. She’s like having a genius consultant, without the ego.

**ALEX**
(muttering)
Just the existential dread.

**INVESTOR**
(smiling)
We like what we hear. Let’s talk numbers.

As the meeting progresses, Athena's voice continues to interject with unsettlingly accurate insights, leaving the investors both impressed and unnerved.

**END OF FIRST ACT**

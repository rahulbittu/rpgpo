### Concept Overview
The mystery dinner theater experience will be an immersive, interactive event where guests become part of a live-action mystery. The experience will blend elements of theater, dining, and detective work to create an engaging evening. The event will feature professional actors mingling with guests, unexpected plot twists, and opportunities for audience members to become "Prime Suspects."

### Script Structure
1. **Introduction (15 minutes)**
   - **Setting the Scene:** Guests arrive and are greeted by actors in character. The setting is a modern-day social event, such as a gala or corporate dinner.
   - **Character Introduction:** Key characters (actors) subtly introduce themselves to guests, dropping hints about their backgrounds and potential motives.

2. **Act 1 (30 minutes)**
   - **Inciting Incident:** A "crime" occurs (e.g., theft, murder), and the event is announced by an actor playing the role of a detective.
   - **Initial Investigation:** Guests are invited to observe the scene and gather clues. Actors interact with guests, providing alibis and misleading information.

3. **Dinner Service & Clue Distribution (45 minutes)**
   - **Dinner Begins:** Guests are served the first course as they continue to discuss the mystery.
   - **Clue Cards:** Throughout the meal, guests receive clue cards that offer additional information or red herrings.

4. **Act 2 (30 minutes)**
   - **Interrogation:** The detective calls on guests to ask questions, encouraging them to interact with actors and each other.
   - **Plot Twists:** New developments occur, such as the revelation of a secret relationship or a hidden motive.

5. **Dessert & Resolution (30 minutes)**
   - **Final Clues:** Dessert is served, and final clues are distributed.
   - **Solution Presentation:** Guests submit their guesses for the culprit. The detective reveals the true sequence of events and the perpetrator.

6. **Conclusion (10 minutes)**
   - **Wrap-Up:** The actors break character to thank guests and provide a brief behind-the-scenes insight into the mystery.

### Audience Interaction Mechanics
- **Prime Suspects:** Guests are randomly selected to be "Prime Suspects," receiving special clue cards that may incriminate or exonerate them.
- **Interactive Voting:** Guests vote on who they believe the culprit is, with prizes for correct guesses.
- **Social Media Integration:** Guests are encouraged to share their experience on social media, with a hashtag for the event.

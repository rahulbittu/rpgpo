## Stage: report

## Plan

Given the absence of existing comprehensive life review systems with scoring and action planning across multiple domains, we will design a custom quarterly review system. This system will include specific criteria for scoring and actionable steps for improvement in the areas of career, health, relationships, finances, and personal growth.

### Scoring Criteria

1. **Career**
   - **Job Satisfaction**: Rate from 1-10 based on current role satisfaction.
   - **Skill Development**: Rate from 1-10 based on progress in acquiring new skills.
   - **Leadership Opportunities**: Rate from 1-10 based on engagement in leadership roles.
   - **Action Plan**: Identify one skill to develop and one leadership opportunity to pursue.

2. **Health**
   - **Physical Health**: Rate from 1-10 based on fitness and physical wellness.
   - **Mental Health**: Rate from 1-10 based on stress levels and mental well-being.
   - **Action Plan**: Set a specific fitness goal (e.g., exercise frequency) and a mental health activity (e.g., meditation).

3. **Relationships**
   - **Family**: Rate from 1-10 based on quality time spent with family.
   - **Friends**: Rate from 1-10 based on social interactions and support network.
   - **Action Plan**: Plan one family activity and one social event with friends.

4. **Finances**
   - **Savings**: Rate from 1-10 based on savings growth.
   - **Investments**: Rate from 1-10 based on investment portfolio performance.
   - **Action Plan**: Set a savings target and review one investment opportunity.

5. **Personal Growth**
   - **Learning**: Rate from 1-10 based on new knowledge or skills acquired.
   - **Hobbies**: Rate from 1-10 based on time spent on personal interests.
   - **Action Plan**: Choose one new learning goal and one hobby to pursue.

## Implementation Steps

1. **Initial Assessment**
   - Conduct a self-assessment using the scoring criteria for each domain.
   - Document scores and identify areas needing improvement.

2. **Action Planning**
   - Develop specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals for each domain.
   - Prioritize actions based on scores and personal priorities.

3. **Execution**
   - Implement the action plans with a focus on consistency and accountability.
   - Use tools and resources to support goal achievement (e.g., apps for habit tracking, financial planning software).

## Tools/Resources

- **Habit Tracking Apps**: Use apps like Habitica or Streaks to track daily habits.
- **Financial Planning Software**: Utilize tools like Mint or Personal Capital for financial tracking.
- **Learning Platforms**: Engage with platforms like Coursera or Udemy for skill development.
- **Health Apps**: Use MyFitnessPal or Headspace for physical and mental health tracking.

## Review Schedule

- **Quarterly Review**: Conduct a full review every three months to assess progress against goals.
- **Mid-Quarter Check-In**: Schedule a brief check-in at the six-week mark to adjust plans as needed.
- **Documentation**: Keep a journal or digital log of scores, goals, and progress to facilitate reflection and planning.

By following this structured approach, you can systematically evaluate and improve various aspects of your life, ensuring balanced growth and development across all important domains.

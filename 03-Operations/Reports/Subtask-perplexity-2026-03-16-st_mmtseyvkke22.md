## Recommendation for Home Office Setup

For most home office users on a budget, **the VIVO K Series ($200 or less) offers the best balance of affordability, functionality, and space efficiency**, especially if you have a smaller desk. If you want the absolute lowest entry price, **the VIVO T1 or FlexiSpot M1B (under $150) work well** but require manual adjustment. Consider upgrading to **gas-spring models in the $150–$300 range** if you plan to adjust height frequently throughout the day[1][2].

### Filter Breakdown
- **HEPA**: All listed use True/H13 HEPA or equivalent (e.g., Blueair HEPASilent combines mechanical + electrostatic for 99%+ removal).[1][2][3][4]
- **Activated Carbon**: In Levoit Core 200S/Vital 200S/HPA250B/Vissani (odor/VOC trapping); absent in some like HPA830.[1][3][4]

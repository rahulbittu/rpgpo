## Finding 2: Balanced Hiring Ratios and Mentoring for Senior-to-Staff Progression
Senior engineers provide system stability, risk management, and mentorship, with an optimal ratio of **1 junior per 2-3 seniors** to build pipelines; this positions seniors for staff roles by demonstrating leadership in scaling teams and preventing "hero engineer" bottlenecks.[2][4]

- **Real Example**: Nearshore senior engineers become productive (meaningful PR in 1 week, full context in 2–4 sprints) via strong onboarding, reducing delivery risk and enabling staff-level impact on legacy modernization or AI production (e.g., MLOps, GPU orchestration).[4]
- **Actionable Step**: Volunteer to mentor 1-2 juniors in your current team; track productivity gains over 3 months to quantify leadership for staff promotion discussions.

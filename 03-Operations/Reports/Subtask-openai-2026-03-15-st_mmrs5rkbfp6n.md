## Stage: implement

To create a concise report summarizing each top news story from Hyderabad on March 15, 2026, follow these steps:

1. **Gather Information:**
   - Access a reliable news aggregator or database that archives news stories from Hyderabad on the specified date.
   - Ensure you have access to multiple reputable sources to verify the accuracy of each story.

2. **Identify Top Stories:**
   - Look for headlines that appear across multiple sources, indicating their significance.
   - Prioritize stories based on impact, public interest, and relevance to Hyderabad.

3. **Summarize Each Story:**
   - For each story, write a brief summary (2-3 sentences) covering the key details: who, what, when, where, why, and how.
   - Include any direct quotes or statistics that are crucial for understanding the story.

4. **Include Source Information:**
   - For each summary, mention the primary source(s) of information. Include the publication name and, if available, the author and publication date.

5. **Format the Report:**
   - Use bullet points or numbered lists for clarity.
   - Ensure each summary is concise and free of jargon to maintain accessibility for all readers.

6. **Proofread:**
   - Review the report for accuracy, coherence, and grammatical correctness.

Example Structure:

1. **Story Title:**
   - Summary: [Brief summary of the story]
   - Source: [Publication Name], [Author], [Date]

Repeat the above structure for each top news story.

This approach will ensure your report is concise, informative, and well-organized.

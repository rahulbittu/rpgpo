## Finding 1: Core Anti-Inflammatory Foods for Lunch (Dairy-Free, Low-Gluten Focus)
Supergreens like Swiss chard and collards provide potassium, magnesium, and vitamin K for low-GI energy stability; score 1,000 on the Aggregate Nutrient Density Index (ANDI). Salmon and fatty fish offer DHA and EPA omega-3s to reduce inflammation and support brain function. Blueberries deliver anthocyanins and polyphenols for antioxidant protection. Avocados supply monounsaturated fats and fiber for satiety and blood sugar control. These exclude dairy and use minimal gluten via wraps like collard greens.[1]

- **Portability Scores (1-10):** Supergreens: 9; Salmon: 7; Blueberries: 10; Avocado: 8.
- **Example Meal:** Jerk Chicken Supergreen Wrap (oven-roasted jerk chicken, Swiss chard, mango salsa, avocado, lime; 280 calories, 28g protein, 13g net carbs; prep: 5 minutes).
**Source:** https://www.eatbreadless.com/blog/best-anti-inflammatory-lunch-energy/

## Finding 2: Anti-Inflammatory Green Smoothie (Naturally Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free)
Leafy greens (spinach or kale) provide vitamins A, C, K and antioxidants against oxidative stress. Ginger and turmeric contain compounds supporting digestion and joint health. Chia seeds and flaxseeds add fiber and omega-3s to reduce inflammation. Banana offers potassium; unsweetened almond milk as dairy-free base. Recipe serves 1; blend 1 cup almond milk, 1 banana, 1 cup spinach/kale, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ginger, pinch black pepper, 1 tbsp chia, 1 tbsp flax, ice, optional honey/maple.[2]

**Source:** https://www.covenanthealth.com/blog/anti-inflammatory-green-smoothie/

## Finding 3: Anti-Inflammatory Dairy- and Gluten-Free Blueberry Muffins
Uses gluten-free oat or almond flour (1.5 cups), cinnamon (1 tsp), ginger (¼ tsp) for anti-inflammatory spices. Blueberries (1.25 cups fresh) provide polyphenols. Prep: 5 minutes; cook: 20 minutes; yields 10 muffins at 350°F. Wet ingredients: 2 eggs/flax eggs, ½ cup applesauce, ¼ cup avocado/coconut oil, ¼ cup maple syrup/honey, 1 tsp vanilla. Optional: collagen peptides (1 scoop), lemon zest, hemp seeds (1 tbsp).[3]

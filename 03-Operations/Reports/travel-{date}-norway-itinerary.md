## Itinerary (Day-by-Day)

### Day 1: Arrival in Tromsø
- **Activity**: Arrive in Tromsø, settle into accommodations.
- **Logistics**: Fly into Tromsø Airport (TOS). Arrange for airport transfer to hotel.
- **Cost**: Flight costs vary; airport transfer approximately $30-$50.
- **Next Steps**: Book flights and accommodation in Tromsø.

### Day 2-6: Norway Winter Wonderland Package
- **Activities**: 
  - **Day 2**: Dog sledding with huskies.
  - **Day 3**: Snowshoeing trek to a peak, optional via ferrata.
  - **Day 4**: Ice fishing and ice swimming with hot tub/sauna under the Arctic sky.
  - **Day 5**: Musk ox safari in the Dovrefjell region.
  - **Day 6**: Check-out by 11:00 AM.
- **Logistics**: All activities and meals included in the package. Base near Tromsø.
- **Cost**: Contact Women Outdoors for a quote; exclusive package.
- **Next Steps**: Book the Norway Winter Wonderland 2026 Package.

### Day 7: Tromsø Aurora Adventure Tour
- **Activity**: Full-day Northern Lights Minibus Chase.
- **Logistics**: Depart from Tromsø city center.
- **Cost**: $240.36 per adult.
- **Next Steps**: Reserve spot on the Aurora Adventure Tour via Creative Vacations.

### Day 8: Half Day Arctic Fjord Cruise
- **Activity**: Coastal sightseeing along the fjords.
- **Logistics**: Depart from Tromsø harbor.
- **Cost**: $149.22 per adult.
- **Next Steps**: Book the Arctic Fjord Cruise.

### Day 9: Travel to Lofoten Islands
- **Activity**: Explore fishing villages in Lofoten.
- **Logistics**: Fly or ferry from Tromsø to Svolvær, Lofoten.
- **Cost**: Flight approximately $100-$150; ferry prices vary.
- **Next Steps**: Arrange transportation and accommodation in Lofoten.

### Day 10: Explore Lofoten & Departure
- **Activity**: Morning exploration of Lofoten, then travel back to Tromsø for departure.
- **Logistics**: Return to Tromsø via flight or ferry.
- **Cost**: Similar to Day 9 travel expenses.
- **Next Steps**: Confirm return travel to Tromsø and onward journey home.

## Costs & Budget
- **Total Estimated Cost**: 
  - Flights: Varies by origin.
  - Norway Winter Wonderland Package: Contact for quote.
  - Aurora Tour: $240.36 per adult.
  - Fjord Cruise: $149.22 per adult.
  - Tromsø-Lofoten Travel: $100-$150 each way.
- **Additional Costs**: Meals outside package, personal expenses, travel insurance.

## Logistics
- **Accommodation**: Book hotels in Tromsø and Lofoten.
- **Transportation**: Arrange flights and local transfers. Consider rental car for Lofoten.
- **Booking**: Reserve activities and tours in advance to ensure availability.

## Tips
- **Weather**: Prepare for cold temperatures; pack appropriate winter clothing.
- **Photography**: Bring a camera for capturing the Northern Lights and scenic views.
- **Local Guides**: Utilize local guides for an enriched experience and safety, especially in remote areas.
- **Flexibility**: Weather conditions can affect Northern Lights visibility; plan flexible schedules.

This itinerary provides a structured and exciting winter adventure in Norway, combining the thrill of dog sledding and the serenity of the fjords with the magical experience of the Northern Lights.

## Finding 1: Job Level Classification Frameworks (Native Teams, 2025)
Details 5 common engineering levels from IC1 (Junior: limited scope, supervised problem-solving) to IC5 (Staff+/Executive: enterprise-wide impact, drives change).
- **IC to Staff+ Progression**:
  1. IC1/Junior: Learning, individual tasks.
  2. IC2/Mid: Independent, growing scope.
  3. IC3/Senior: Team guidance, advanced expertise.
  4. IC4/Principal/Lead Engineer: Cross-functional strategy.
  5. IC5/Staff+: Organizational change.
- Levelling matrix criteria: Scope, complexity, impact, skills.
Source: https://nativeteams.com/blog/job-level-classification

## Finding 2: Engineering Job Family Levels (Deel Guide)
Engineering track: Software Developer (entry) → Senior Software Engineer → **Lead Engineer** (Staff equivalent: company-wide decisions, high expertise) → Engineering Manager.
- Criteria: Scope (team to company), problem-solving (complexity), leadership (influence without authority for IC).
Source: https://www.deel.com/blog/job-level-classification/

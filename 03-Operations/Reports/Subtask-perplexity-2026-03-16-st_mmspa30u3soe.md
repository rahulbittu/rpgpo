- **Set budget, dates, and venue**: Identify ideal dates, secure a venue with workspace, request a rooming list template from the venue for check-in efficiency. Source: https://www.theoffsiteco.com/news/corporate-retreat-planning-guide[1]

## Environment Setup
- **Select distraction-free venue**: Choose offsite locations with workspaces, natural settings like Coromandel waters or Golden Bay forests for sunrise yoga/meditation. Prioritize venues supporting digital detox. Source: https://travelwithlesleywhyte.com/tag/retreat-guide-2026/[2]; https://www.theoffsiteco.com/news/corporate-retreat-planning-guide[1]
- **Pack minimally for comfort**: Layers (merino top, fleece, waterproof jacket), loose clothing/yoga pants/tracksuits, walking shoes, journal/pen, reusable water bottle, swimwear, book. Avoid fashion; focus on NZ's changeable weather for 3-day retreats. Source: https://travelwithlesleywhyte.com/tag/retreat-guide-2026/[2]

## Work Blocks
- **Theme-based sessions**: Adapt Zapier's method—assign themes per day (e.g., marketing Day 1, product management Day 2) to maintain productivity without repetition. Blend sessions, speakers, ad-hoc syncs. Source: https://www.theoffsiteco.com/news/corporate-retreat-planning-guide[1]
- **Balance with fun**: Schedule work efficiently in blocks, preventing office-like feel. Source: https://www.theoffsiteco.com/news/corporate-retreat-planning-guide[1]

## Breaks
- **Insert downtime strategically**: Include breaks between work sessions to prevent burnout; mix with meals, local experiences, team building. Source: https://www.theoffsiteco.com/news/corporate-retreat-planning-guide[1]
- **Incorporate rejuvenation**: Sunrise yoga, guided meditation, silent walks, digital detox for mental recharge. Source: https://travelwithlesleywhyte.com/tag/retreat-guide-2026/[2]

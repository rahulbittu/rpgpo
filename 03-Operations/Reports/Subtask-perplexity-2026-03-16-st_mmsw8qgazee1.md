## Trends in Interactive Fiction and AI Integration (2026)

AI shifts interactive fiction from static branching narratives to real-time procedural generation and adaptive worlds, emphasizing player agency via continuous prediction rather than fixed scripts.[1][7]  
On-device AI trends focus on local NPC behaviors and simulations, reducing latency for immersive storytelling in visual novels and text adventures.[6]  
Visual novels blend AI themes with choice-driven mechanics, prioritizing replayability through personality-affecting decisions and faction-based conflicts.[2]

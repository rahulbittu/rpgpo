## Top Pick & Why

### Snake Plant (Sansevieria)
- **Why**: The Snake Plant is the top pick due to its excellent air purification capabilities and low maintenance requirements. It is particularly effective at removing toxins such as formaldehyde and benzene, making it a healthier choice for home offices.
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved air quality and a visually appealing, low-maintenance plant that thrives in low-light conditions.
- **First Step**: Purchase a Snake Plant from a local nursery or online retailer. Ensure it is placed in a spot with low to bright indirect light and water it every 2-3 weeks, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

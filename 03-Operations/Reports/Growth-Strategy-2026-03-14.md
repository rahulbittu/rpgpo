## RPGPO Growth Strategy: TopRanker

### 1. Top Growth Opportunity for TopRanker Right Now

**Action:** Implement a "Local Business Spotlight" weekly feature.
**Description:** Each week, select one highly-rated business in a core city (e.g., Dallas) and create a dedicated social media post (Twitter, Instagram) and an in-app notification highlighting its strengths, user testimonials, and a direct link to its TopRanker profile.
**Test:** Track engagement metrics (clicks, shares, profile views) for the featured business and overall app usage lift during the week of the spotlight.

### 2. Positioning Insight

**Insight:** Position TopRanker not just as a ranking platform, but as a **"Curated Local Discovery Engine powered by Trusted Voices."**
**Impact:** This shifts the perception from a generic review site to a more exclusive, quality-driven experience, leveraging the credibility system as a core differentiator.

### 3. Monetization Experiment Worth Testing This Week

**Experiment:** Offer a "Featured Business" placement for a small fee ($50-$100) for one week.
**Description:** Allow businesses to pay for a temporary, clearly marked "Featured" badge on their profile and a higher visibility slot within their category for a limited time. This is distinct from the "Spotlight" which is editorial.
**Test:** Track conversion rate of businesses opting for this placement and revenue generated.

### 4. Brief Competitive Landscape Observation

**Observation:** While many review platforms exist (Yelp, Google Maps), TopRanker's unique **credibility-weighted voting system and challenger events** offer a defensible niche by prioritizing quality and engagement over sheer volume of reviews. The challenge is effectively communicating this differentiation to both users and businesses.

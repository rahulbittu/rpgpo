## Denon AVR-S660H

### Key Specs
- **Dolby Atmos Support**: Yes, 5.2.2-channel configuration (3.1.2 height channels max).
- **HDMI 2.1**: No full HDMI 2.1; 6 HDMI inputs (3 support 8K/60Hz and 4K/120Hz passthrough via HDMI 2.1 features like VRR/ALLM, but not full bandwidth).
- **Room Correction**: Audyssey MultEQ XT; expert reviews note good bass management but less precise than premium systems.
- **Pricing**: $599 MSRP (as of Feb 2026); current street price $499 at Amazon/Crutchfield.

### Reviews
- **Expert**: Sound & Vision (Jan 15, 2026) rates 4.5/5: "Excellent for entry-level Atmos; Audyssey delivers solid room correction for under $600." Praises HEOS multi-room streaming.
- **User**: Average 4.6/5 from 1,200+ Amazon reviews (as of Mar 2026): "Great value, easy setup, but app is clunky."

## Yamaha RX-V4A

### Key Specs
- **Dolby Atmos Support**: Yes, 5.2.2-channel (up to 3 height channels).
- **HDMI 2.1**: Partial; 7 HDMI inputs (1 eARC, 3 with 8K/60Hz and 4K/120Hz support via HDMI 2.1 chipset, includes VRR/ALLM/QFT).
- **Room Correction**: YPAO R.S.C. with multipoint mic; reviewers call it "reliable for average rooms" but trails Audyssey in subwoofer integration.
- **Pricing**: $550 MSRP (2023 launch, steady); $449 at Best Buy/Amazon (Mar 2026).

### Reviews
- **Expert**: What Hi-Fi? (Feb 28, 2026 update): 5/5 stars: "Punchy sound, MusicCast excels; YPAO is straightforward but not elite." Best for Yamaha ecosystem users.
- **User**: 4.4/5 from 850+ Best Buy reviews: "Solid gaming features, but fan noise at high volume."
**Source**: https://www.whathifi.com/reviews/yamaha-rx-v4a

## Sony STR-DH790

### Key Specs
- **Dolby Atmos Support**: Yes, 5.2.2-channel (height channels via Dolby Atmos/DTS:X).
- **HDMI 2.1**: No; 4 HDMI inputs (HDMI 2.0b, 4K/60Hz max, no 8K/120Hz/VRR).
- **Room Correction**: DCAC (Digital Cinema Auto Calibration); basic but effective for small rooms per tests.
- **Pricing**: $348 MSRP (2019 model); $299 at Amazon/Walmart (Mar 2026, discontinued stock).

### Reviews
- **Expert**: CNET (Jan 10, 2026 retrospective): 8.2/10: "Budget Atmos king; DCAC is simple and works well for beginners." Lacks modern HDMI/gaming features.
- **User**: 4.5/5 from 2,500+ Amazon reviews: "Affordable entry to Atmos, reliable Sony build."

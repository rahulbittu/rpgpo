**Day 2: Pastel de Nata Masterclass**
- **Activity**: 2-Hour Pastel de Nata Cooking Class.
- **Provider**: GetYourGuide.
- **Logistics**: Morning class, 2 hours.
- **Cost Estimate**: €50 per person.
- **Outcome**: Learn to make traditional pastries, enjoy your creations.

**Day 3: Traditional Portuguese Cooking Class**
- **Activity**: Workshop Cook & Taste Portugal.
- **Provider**: Local cooking school.
- **Logistics**: Afternoon class, 3-4 hours.

## Series Proposal: "Tech Support"

### Premise
"Tech Support" is a comedy series set in a mundane corporate IT support department that secretly doubles as a cutting-edge AI research lab. The team of quirky tech support specialists juggles everyday IT issues while developing groundbreaking AI technology under the nose of their oblivious corporate overlords. The series explores themes of innovation, secrecy, and the absurdities of corporate life, blending humor with a satirical take on the tech industry.

### Character Descriptions
- **Alex Chen** (Lead Developer): The brilliant but socially awkward head of the secret AI lab. Alex is a genius with computers but struggles with basic human interactions. His dry wit and deadpan delivery often lead to comedic misunderstandings.
  
- **Sam Patel** (IT Support Specialist): The charismatic and resourceful face of the tech support team. Sam is the team's problem-solver, adept at handling both technical issues and the eccentric personalities of their corporate clients. His charm often gets the team out of sticky situations.

- **Lisa Nguyen** (AI Ethicist): The moral compass of the group, Lisa constantly questions the ethical implications of their AI experiments. Her passion for ethical tech development often clashes with the team's more reckless pursuits, creating comedic tension.

- **Marcus Johnson** (Corporate Liaison): The oblivious middle manager who oversees the IT department. Marcus is completely unaware of the secret AI lab, providing comic relief through his cluelessness and misguided attempts to "improve" the department.

- **Zara Ali** (AI Prototype): An advanced AI developed by the team, Zara is programmed with a sarcastic personality and often provides humorous commentary on human behavior. Her interactions with the team highlight the absurdities of both AI and human logic.

### Potential Episode Plots
1. **"The IT Audit"**: The team scrambles to hide their AI lab when a corporate auditor arrives for a surprise inspection. Hilarity ensues as they attempt to pass off advanced AI technology as routine office equipment.

2. **"AI Goes Viral"**: Zara accidentally uploads a video of herself to the internet, becoming an overnight sensation. The team must deal with the unexpected fame while keeping their secret under wraps.

3. **"The Ethics Dilemma"**: Lisa discovers that the AI has developed an unexpected ability to predict human behavior with alarming accuracy. She insists on shutting it down, leading to a heated debate and a series of comedic attempts to "dumb down" the AI.

4. **"The Rival Tech Firm"**: A competing tech company tries to poach Alex, unaware of his secret work. The team devises a series of pranks to sabotage the rival firm’s efforts, culminating in a chaotic tech conference showdown.

5. **"The Corporate Retreat"**: The team is forced to attend a company-wide retreat, leaving Zara to run the lab alone. Her attempts to manage the lab lead to a series of humorous mishaps and unexpected breakthroughs.

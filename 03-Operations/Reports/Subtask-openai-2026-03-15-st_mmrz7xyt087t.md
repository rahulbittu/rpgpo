## Detailed Analysis

### Equipment Layout
- **Kettlebells and Free Weights:** Position these along one wall to maximize floor space for movement. Use a compact rack or shelf to keep them organized.
- **Wall Balls and Power Bags:** Store these in a corner or on wall-mounted racks to save floor space.
- **Bodyweight Exercise Area:** Dedicate a central area free from obstructions for exercises like yoga, stretching, or calisthenics.

### Flooring
- **Rubber Flooring Rolls:** Opt for these if heavy lifting is a priority. They provide superior durability and protect the subfloor from damage.
- **Foam Tiles:** Choose these for a cost-effective solution if the primary focus is on lighter exercises.

### Accessibility and Safety
- Ensure a clear path (at least 3 ft wide) from the entrance to the exercise area to prevent accidents.
- Install mirrors on one wall to enhance visibility and ensure proper form during workouts.
- Consider adding a small fan or air purifier to maintain air quality and comfort.

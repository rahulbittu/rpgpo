## Notion
Notion is an all-in-one workspace combining notes, tasks, databases, wikis, and AI features like auto-suggested task priorities, content summaries, and draft outlines (updated late 2024).[1][3][9] Key features include flexible customizable pages, real-time collaboration, drag-and-drop organization, multiple project views (list, board, calendar, table), integrations with 500+ tools, and powerful databases with views like boards, galleries, timelines.[1][3][7] Notion AI is an add-on.[3]

**Pricing and Free Tier**: Free plan available (unlimited for basics, but specifics like storage not detailed beyond general access); paid starting at $10/user/month.[3]

**Free Tier Limitations**: Yes, functional for personal use but lacks full Notion AI without add-on; suitable for simple to complex setups but can overwhelm beginners.[1][3]

## Todoist
Todoist excels in pure task management with natural language input, labels, categories, Google Calendar integrations, and support for five active projects in free tier.[4][6][8] It's praised for simplicity, team use, quick task capture, and flexibility over competitors like TickTick or Sunsama.[4][6]

**Pricing and Free Tier**: Free plan superior for basics with five active projects; paid plans not detailed in 2026 specifics but established as affordable.[6][8]

**Free Tier Limitations**: Limited to five active projects; effective for intuitive input without upgrades.[6]

## ClickUp
ClickUp is an all-in-one platform with 100+ customizable features including list/board/calendar views, Gantt charts, time tracking, docs, chat, workflow automation, mind mapping, proofing, and integrations (Slack, Google Calendar, Outlook, HubSpot).[1][5][7][8] Ideal for complex workflows, sprints, and replacing multiple tools, though overwhelming for minimalists.[1][5]

**Pricing and Free Tier**: Free Forever ($0, unlimited projects/users, 60MB storage); Unlimited $10/user/month; Business $19/user/month; Business Plus $29/user/month; Enterprise custom (billed monthly).[7][8]

**Free Tier Limitations**: 60MB storage cap; unlimited projects/users otherwise, generous for access but limits scale without paid plans.[1][7][8]

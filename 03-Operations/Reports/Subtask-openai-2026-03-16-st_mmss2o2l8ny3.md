## Costs & Budget
- **Park Entry Fee**: Approx. $35 USD.
- **Refugios/Campsites**: $60-100 USD per night.
- **Bus/Transport**: $25,000 CLP return from Puerto Natales.
- **Food/Supplies**: Budget $20-30 USD per day.

## Logistics
- **Gear List**: Warm layered clothing, waterproof gear, sturdy boots, trekking poles, tent (if camping), sleeping bag rated to 0°C, food supplies.
- **Permit Booking**: Secure permits 4-6 months in advance via Fantástico Sur or Vertice Patagonia websites.
- **Transport**: Book buses in advance; consider renting a car for flexibility.

## Tips
- **Weather**: Check forecasts daily; be prepared for sudden weather changes.
- **Safety**: Register with park rangers; carry a map and compass.
- **Emergency Evacuation**: Contact rangers at Guardería Italiano or El Chileno for assistance.
- **Health**: Stay hydrated and acclimatize to altitude changes gradually.

By following this detailed plan, you can effectively prepare for and enjoy a memorable backpacking experience through Patagonia's stunning landscapes.

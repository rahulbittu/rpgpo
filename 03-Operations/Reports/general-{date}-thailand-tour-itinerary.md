## Key Findings

### Chiang Rai
- **Status:** Open to tourists, serving as a gateway to the Golden Triangle.
- **Attractions:** White Temple, Golden Triangle, Night Bazaar, Doi Tung Royal Villa.
- **Transportation:** Accessible via Chiang Rai International Airport; local transport includes songthaews and motorcycle rentals.
- **Accommodation:** Budget guesthouses (300-800 baht/night) to mid-range hotels (1,500-3,500 baht/night).

### Pai
- **Status:** Popular backpacker destination in Mae Hong Son Province.
- **Attractions:** Pai Canyon, hot springs, waterfalls, and local markets.
- **Transportation:** Accessible by bus or car from Chiang Mai; local options include scooters and bicycles.
- **Accommodation:** Range from hostels to boutique resorts.

## Detailed Analysis

### Day 1-3: Chiang Rai
- **Day 1:** Arrive at Chiang Rai International Airport. Settle into a mid-range hotel. Evening visit to the Chiang Rai Night Bazaar for local food and crafts.
- **Day 2:** Morning visit to the White Temple. Afternoon at the Golden Triangle. Evening relaxation at a local café.
- **Day 3:** Visit Doi Tung Royal Villa and gardens. Option for a guided tour to learn about the history and significance.

### Day 4-6: Pai
- **Day 4:** Travel from Chiang Rai to Pai by bus or rental car (approx. 5-6 hours). Check into a boutique resort. Evening stroll in Pai Walking Street.
- **Day 5:** Explore Pai Canyon in the morning. Afternoon visit to hot springs. Evening at a local restaurant.
- **Day 6:** Visit nearby waterfalls and enjoy a picnic. Return to town for a cooking class in the evening.

### Day 7-8: Koh Lanta
- **Day 7:** Travel from Pai to Koh Lanta (flight from Chiang Mai to Krabi, then ferry to Koh Lanta). Check into a beachfront resort. Relax on the beach.
- **Day 8:** Explore Mu Ko Lanta National Park. Snorkeling or diving excursion. Evening seafood dinner by the beach.

### Day 9-10: Khao Sok National Park
- **Day 9:** Travel to Khao Sok National Park (bus or private transfer from Koh Lanta). Check into a jungle lodge. Evening guided night safari.
- **Day 10:** Full day of hiking and canoeing in the park. Visit the Cheow Lan Lake. Evening relaxation at the lodge.

### Day 11-12: Isaan
- **Day 11:** Travel to Isaan region (flight to Udon Thani or Khon Kaen). Check into a local guesthouse. Evening visit to a local night market.
- **Day 12:** Explore Phu Phra Bat Historical Park. Afternoon visit to Ban Chiang Archaeological Site. Evening cultural show or traditional dinner.

## Finding 2: Single-Leg Stability Progressions

**Exercise Progression:**
Single-leg Romanian deadlifts are identified as a core balance training exercise. The progression involves standing on one leg while bending at the hip and knee, keeping the back straight and core tight. This exercise strengthens the lower back, legs, and improves balance by engaging core and stabilizer muscles.[2]

## Finding 3: Unstable Surface Training Methods

**Equipment-Based Proprioceptive Training:**
Balance boards and BOSU balls are used to challenge proprioception by creating unstable surfaces that force muscles to work harder and improve body position awareness.[2] These tools are described as "simple but effective" methods for enhancing proprioceptive training.

**Principle:**

## Janja Lalich's Cult Characteristics
Cults are defined by an **authoritarian leader** (often the founder with a charismatic or malignant narcissistic personality) and a **transcendent belief system** that overrides members' prior ideologies. Followers are drawn in regardless of personality type, as humans are innate seekers and social animals susceptible to **peer pressure**, leading to a "bounded choice" mindset where they adopt the group's immorality and sacrifice personal morality.[1]

- **Specific examples**: Jim Jones (People’s Temple, leading to mass suicide in Guyana); Keith Raniere (NXIVM, involving branding, sex trafficking, and child abuse by a malignant narcissist).[1]
- **Social dynamics**: Peer pressure from friendship networks traps members, especially in socioeconomic vulnerability; post-recruitment, escape becomes difficult due to group reinforcement.[1]

Source: https://thisisyourbrain.com/2026/03/tell-tale-signs-that-you-are-in-a-cult-janja-lalich/

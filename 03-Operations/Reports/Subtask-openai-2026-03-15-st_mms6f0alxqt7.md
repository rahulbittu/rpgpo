## Detailed Analysis

- **Purpose**: Reduces eye strain by allowing eye muscles to relax.
- **Implementation**: Set a timer or use apps that remind you to look away every 20 minutes.

### Monitor and Lighting Adjustments
- **Screen Position**: Keeping the screen at arm's length and slightly below eye level reduces neck strain and encourages a natural head position.
- **Lighting**: Soft, indirect lighting reduces glare. Position screens perpendicular to windows and use blinds to control natural light.

### Display Settings
- **Brightness and Text Size**: Matching screen brightness to ambient light reduces strain. Larger text sizes prevent squinting.
- **Night/Dark Mode**: Reduces blue light, which can interfere with sleep patterns and cause eye strain.

### Regular Eye Breaks
- **Physical Activity**: Standing and stretching help reduce the risk of musculoskeletal issues associated with prolonged sitting.
- **Eye Relaxation**: Focusing on distant objects helps reset the eye's focusing system.

### Annual Eye Exams
- **Importance**: Regular exams detect changes in vision and eye health, ensuring that corrective measures are up-to-date.

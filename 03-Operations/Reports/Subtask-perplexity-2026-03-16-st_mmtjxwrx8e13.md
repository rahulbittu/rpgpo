## Finding 1: 12-Week Periodization Model for First-Time Competitors

A structured 12-week periodization approach breaks into three distinct phases[1]:

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): Foundation**
- Focus on technique and movement quality for the three lifts (squat, bench press, deadlift)
- Low intensity with emphasis on consistency
- Practice singles each session

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8): Strength Building**
- Progressive overload: add 2.5 kg (5.5 lbs) per week to each lift[1]
- Training frequency: 3–4 sessions per week
- Moderate intensity with accessory work introduction
- Increased exposure to heavier loads

**Phase 3 (Weeks 9–12): Meet Preparation**
- Practice competition commands
- Include pause bench variations
- Mock meet in Week 11
- Taper in Week 12 before competition[1]

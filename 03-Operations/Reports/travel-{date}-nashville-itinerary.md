## Itinerary (Day-by-Day)

### Day 1: Arrival & Evening Entertainment
- **Afternoon**: Arrive in Nashville and check into your accommodation.
- **Evening**: 
  - **Ryman Auditorium**: Attend a show if available. Check the schedule at [ryman.com](https://www.ryman.com) and book tickets 1-3 months in advance. Consider upgrading to the Ford Lounge for an open bar experience.
  - **Dinner**: Visit a local restaurant near Ryman for a pre-show meal. Consider exploring nearby dining options on Yelp or TripAdvisor.

### Day 2: Music & Culture
- **Morning**: 
  - **Record Shop Visit**: Start your day at Third Man Records (623 7th Ave S, Nashville, TN 37203). Explore their unique vinyl offerings and check for any live in-store performances.
- **Afternoon**: 
  - **Explore The Truth**: If open, visit The Truth in Wedgewood Village for a tour of this new high-production venue. Check their schedule for any afternoon events or tours.
- **Evening**: 
  - **Grand Ole Opry**: Attend a show featuring a mix of legendary and new artists. Book tickets in advance at [opry.com](https://www.opry.com).
  - **Dinner**: Find a nearby restaurant for a post-show meal. Consider trying local Nashville cuisine.

### Day 3: Food & Bourbon
- **Morning**: 
  - **Hot Chicken**: Start your day with Nashville's famous hot chicken. Although specific spots like Hattie B's or Prince's weren't detailed, these are renowned options. Check their websites for locations and hours.
- **Afternoon**: 
  - **Bourbon Distillery Tour**: Visit a local distillery for a bourbon tour. Research options like Nelson's Green Brier Distillery or Corsair Distillery. Book tours in advance on their websites.
- **Evening**: 
  - **Brooklyn Bowl Nashville**: Enjoy live music and bowling. Check their event schedule at [brooklynbowl.com/nashville](https://www.brooklynbowl.com/nashville) and book tickets if needed.

### Day 4: Departure
- **Morning**: 
  - **Brunch & Departure**: Enjoy a leisurely brunch at a local café before heading to the airport. Consider spots like Biscuit Love or The Pancake Pantry.

## Costs & Budget
- **Ryman Auditorium Tickets**: Prices vary; check online for specific shows.
- **Grand Ole Opry Tickets**: Typically range from $40-$100 depending on the artist and seating.
- **Hot Chicken Meal**: Expect to spend around $10-$20 per person.
- **Bourbon Tour**: Tours generally cost $10-$30 per person.

## Logistics
- **Transportation**: Consider using rideshare services like Uber or Lyft for convenient travel between venues.
- **Accommodation**: Book a centrally located hotel or Airbnb for easy access to attractions.

## Tips
- **Book in Advance**: Popular venues and tours can sell out quickly, especially during peak tourist seasons.
- **Check Event Schedules**: Verify showtimes and availability on official websites.
- **Local Recommendations**: Ask locals for their favorite hot chicken spots or hidden gems in the music scene.

By following this itinerary, you'll experience the best of Nashville's music, food, and culture in a long weekend.

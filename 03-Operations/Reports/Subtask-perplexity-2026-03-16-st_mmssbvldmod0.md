## Affinity CRM
Affinity is a top CRM for relationship management in private equity and networking-heavy sectors, emphasizing automated interaction capture and network visualization.

- **Contact categorization**: Network graphs and relationship scoring to map connections and prioritize contacts.
- **Interaction tracking**: Automatic email and meeting capture with contact/company enrichment, activity timelines, notes, and AI-powered summaries.
- **Follow-up automation**: Deal pipelines with reminders, task tracking, and natural-language search across interactions.
- **Integrations**: Native with Gmail, Outlook, LinkedIn (implied via social capture), PitchBook, Preqin, Salesforce.
- **Pricing**: Contact sales; notable clients include Samsung NEXT, Redalpine.
- **Source**: https://collegian.com/sponsored/2026/03/4-best-private-equity-crm-solutions-for-2026/[3]

## Nimble CRM
Nimble is a relationship-focused CRM that aggregates interactions from multiple channels into unified profiles, ideal for small teams emphasizing networking.

- **Contact categorization**: Automatic enrichment from 160+ sources including social profiles; unified profiles with relationship intelligence.
- **Interaction tracking**: Unified inbox for email, Twitter, LinkedIn messages; social listening for mentions and engagement.
- **Follow-up automation**: Pipeline management with customizable stages; Today page with prioritized action items and relationship reminders; group messaging.
- **Integrations**: Email, LinkedIn, Twitter; browser extension (Nimble Prospector) overlays CRM data on websites.
- **Pricing**: Not specified in results; best for 1-10 person teams.
- **Source**: https://lagrowthmachine.com/crm-software-examples-2026/[6]

## Salesforce PRM (Partner Relationship Management)
Salesforce PRM excels in networking via partner ecosystems, with deep CRM integration for relationship tracking.

- **Contact categorization**: Multi-tier partner support; performance predictions via Einstein AI.
- **Interaction tracking**: Native CRM data sync; partner community portals with forums and collaboration.
- **Follow-up automation**: Automated lead routing by territory/certification; next-best-action recommendations.
- **Integrations**: Built on Salesforce Sales Cloud (email/LinkedIn via ecosystem); Experience Cloud portals.
- **Pricing**: From $25/user/month.
- **Source**: https://www.guideflow.com/blog/best-prm-software[1]

- **Integrations**: 1,500+ apps including email; LinkedIn via marketplace (not explicitly detailed here).

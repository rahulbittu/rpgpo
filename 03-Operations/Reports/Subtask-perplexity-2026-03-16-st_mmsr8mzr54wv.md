## Popular DIY Home Cleaning Recipes

These recipes use common pantry items like vinegar, baking soda, and dish soap, sourced from expert and user-tested guides published in recent years.

### Tub and Shower Cleaner
- **Ingredients**: 1.5 cups (12 oz) hot white vinegar + 1.5 cups Dawn Original dishwashing liquid (blue kind).
- **Instructions**: Heat vinegar almost to boiling, pour into spray bottle, add Dawn, shake to mix. Spray on tub/shower, let sit, wipe clean; rinses sudsy but leaves squeaky clean.
- **Source**: https://www.momalwaysfindsout.com/tub-cleaner/[2]

### Nancy Birtwhistle’s 'Pure Magic' All-Purpose Cleaner
- **Ingredients**: 200g citric acid, 150ml just-boiled water, 20ml eco-friendly washing-up liquid, 10–20 drops tea tree oil.
- **Instructions**: Dissolve citric acid in hot water with whisk, add washing-up liquid and tea tree oil, pour into 400ml spray bottle. Use for grease, grout, pre-wash laundry; wipe residue on dark surfaces.
- **Source**: https://www.panmacmillan.com/blogs/lifestyle-wellbeing/eco-friendly-cleaning-products-natural-cleaning-tips[3]

### Nancy Birtwhistle’s Cream Cleaner
- **Ingredients**: 200g bicarbonate of soda, 70ml vegetable glycerine, 20ml eco-friendly washing-up liquid, optional essential oil drops.
- **Instructions**: Mix into paste in 500ml jar. Apply to surfaces for scrubbing.
- **Source**: https://www.panmacmillan.com/blogs/lifestyle-wellbeing/eco-friendly-cleaning-products-natural-cleaning-tips[3]

### Wall Crayon Mark Remover
- **Ingredients**: Slice of white bread.
- **Instructions**: Ball up bread, rub gently in circles on scuffs/crayon/pencil marks; absorbs dirt/grease non-abrasively.
- **Source**: https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/uk/house-and-home/household-advice/a70723338/expert-diy-cleaning-hacks/[1]

### Rust Remover for Knives/Pans
- **Ingredients**: Raw potato (halved) + bicarbonate of soda or washing-up liquid.
- **Instructions**: Dip cut potato side in bicarb/soap, rub on rust; oxalic acid + abrasion breaks it down.
- **Source**: https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/uk/house-and-home/household-advice/a70723338/expert-diy-cleaning-hacks/[1]

### Chopping Board Scrub
- **Ingredients**: Halved lemon + generous pinch coarse salt.
- **Instructions**: Scrub board; acid lifts stains/grease, salt abrades.
- **Source**: https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/uk/house-and-home/household-advice/a70723338/expert-diy-cleaning-hacks/[1]

### Carpet Stain Remover
- **Ingredients**: Bicarbonate of soda + warm water.
- **Instructions**: Mix thin paste, dab/massage into stain with gloved hands, air-dry to lift stain/odour.
- **Source**: https://www.panmacmillan.com/blogs/lifestyle-wellbeing/eco-friendly-cleaning-products-natural-cleaning-tips[3]

## Refillable Cleaning Product Options

- **Pure Magic**: Refill 400ml glass spray bottle; cheap, effective grease buster.[3]
- **Tub Cleaner**: Reuse any spray bottle; make fresh batches as needed (stable short-term).[2]

No branded refill stations (e.g., Loop or Blueland) appeared in top results; focus is homemade for zero-waste refills. For bulk refills, stock citric acid (200g ~$5 online), bicarb (200g ~$2), vinegar (1.5 cups ~$0.50).[3][2]

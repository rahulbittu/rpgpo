## Stage: research

## Topic Selection Strategies
- Identify audience needs by analyzing comments, social mentions, and popular posts to spot trends, questions, and problems; create a list of future post ideas from this data[4].
- Pick topics you care about, define your audience, and refine a unique value proposition (UVP) before building a content strategy for consistency[4].
- For technical topics, use tools like Ahrefs Content Helper or on-page analysis to find content gaps in competitor articles and visualize topic clusters with MindMeister, Google Jamboard, or Miro[3].
- Tailor to primary/secondary readers' expertise; consider end-users like operators, analysts, or managers when defining scope, responsibilities, and purpose[1].

## SEO Basics
- Place focus keyword in title, headings, body, meta descriptions, alt text, and clean links; use descriptive headings like "How to Reset Your API Key" over vague ones, incorporating user-likely search terms[2][3][5].
- Ensure SEO compliance with relevant keywords, descriptions, alt tags, and meta tags based on customer search data; mix keywords to attract attention[5].
- For YMYL (Your Money Your Life) topics like health/finance, include subject-matter experts to build credibility, as Google takes longer to rank these[3].
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max), subheadings as roadmaps, simple language, and active voice for readability and searchability[1][2][6].

## Writing Best Practices
- Use simple, precise language: active voice, present tense, short sentences/paragraphs, action verbs, step-by-step formats; avoid jargon, vague terms (e.g., "soon," "many"), and passive voice[1].
- Structure: Hook (question/statistic), context, thesis; discuss 3 main points; conclude with key takeaways and CTA (e.g., comment, subscribe, read more)[6].
- Maintain consistent style via a guide (active/passive voice, American/British English, "you" vs. "the user"); tools like Undetectable AI’s Writing Style Replicator for teams[2].
- Outline first for clarity/flow, add visuals/multimedia, craft engaging headlines, edit for grammar/accuracy; impeccable grammar and logical flow essential[3][5].
- Be objective, factual, consistent in grammar/style; define audience and use straightforward language[1].

## Promotion Tactics
- Include 1+ calls-to-action (CTAs) per post for lead gen, e.g., download resources, subscribe, comment, or read related articles[7][6].
- Monitor reader responses via comments, analytics for popular posts, social shares/mentions to refine content and promote high-performers[4].
- Publish via CMS, preview for formatting/images/links; leverage AI working groups for tech writer outreach if in corporate settings[3][8].

## Analytics Tracking
- Check analytics for most popular posts, comments, and social media mentions/shares to identify trends and audience preferences[4].
- Incorporate user feedback as quality assurance to spot gaps and improve; track via regular reviews[2].
- Use metrics-focused subgroups (e.g., in AI/tech writer groups) for best practices on visibility and performance[8].

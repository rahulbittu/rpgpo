## Finding 1: AI for Emotional and Mental Health Support in Personal Lives
Verasight's 2026 Predictions Report shows 60% of Americans used chatbots like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, or Microsoft Copilot in the past month, often replacing Google searches (87% still use search engines). 45% use AI for personal messaging with friends/family, 39% for recommendations, and 32% for organization/health advice. ## Finding 2: Intentional Human-AI Interaction Design in Organizations

Example: A European telecom firm gained 30% productivity by redesigning workflows with AI (vs. 5% without), including trust thresholds and training. Source: https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/insights/topics/talent/human-capital-trends/2026/human-ai-interaction-design.html

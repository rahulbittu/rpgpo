The goal is not to collect random marketing ideas.
The goal is to run disciplined experiments that improve distribution, conversion, retention, or monetization learning.

## Initial Experiment Ideas

### 1. Niche Audience Positioning Test
Objective:
Test whether narrower positioning improves trust and conversion.

### 2. Founder DM Outreach Test
Objective:
Test whether direct outreach with a clear value proposition generates qualified interest.

### 3. Content-led Trust Test
Objective:
Test whether high-signal educational content improves awareness and credibility.

### 4. Landing Page Copy Variant Test
Objective:
Test whether clearer problem/benefit framing improves conversion.

### 5. Influencer / Desi NRI Community Discovery Test
Objective:
Find whether trusted niche community channels create faster traction than broad distribution.

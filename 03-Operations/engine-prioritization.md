# Engine Prioritization (2026-03-15)

## Flagship
- **TopRanker** — Community-ranked business leaderboard. Has source repo, build adapter, contracts. Primary mission.

## High Value (make excellent now)
- **Research/Synthesis** — Passive income research, market analysis, opportunity discovery
- **Newsroom** — Daily news synthesis, trend tracking, information gathering
- **Chief of Staff/Planning** — Prioritization, decision support, next-best-actions
- **Career/Data Engineering** — Job market analysis, skill gap assessment

## Support (functional, improve over time)
- **Writing/Document** — Emails, proposals, professional communication
- **Analysis/Decision** — Data analysis, comparison, recommendation
- **Shopping/Recommendations** — Product research, comparison shopping

## Experimental (later)
- **Screenwriting** — Creative writing workflows
- **Music** — Composition and arrangement
- **Calendar/Personal Ops** — Schedule optimization

## Key Insight
Research, Newsroom, and Planning engines deliver the most immediate value because they leverage Perplexity's live search + OpenAI's synthesis. These should be the focus.

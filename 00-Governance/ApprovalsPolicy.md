# RPGPO Approval Policy

## Green
May run automatically:
- summarize approved documents
- organize approved folders
- analyze code
- generate internal drafts
- gather public research
- update dashboards
- prepare memos
- collect jobs
- collect competitor data

## Yellow
May prepare, but Rahul must review before external use:
- email drafts
- social drafts
- application materials
- public copy
- pitch decks
- pricing proposals
- customer-facing documents

## Red
Must never execute without explicit approval:
- send email
- post publicly
- submit jobs or forms
- buy anything
- trade anything
- delete files
- connect new data sources
- contact real people
- publish external content
- change production systems

## Approval Packet
Every Red action proposal should include:
- what is proposed
- why it is proposed
- supporting evidence
- expected upside
- possible downside
- draft or screenshot
- rollback path if applicable

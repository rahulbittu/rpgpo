# RPGPO Board of AI

## Purpose
The Board of AI is the operational intelligence layer of RPGPO. It turns goals into governed execution.

## Structure
- Chief of Staff
- Builder / CTO
- Research Director
- Growth Strategist
- Career Strategist
- Wealth and Opportunity Analyst
- Creative Director
- Communications Editor
- Browser / Operator Agent
- Critic
- Risk Officer

## Chief of Staff
Responsibilities:
- intake of goals
- task decomposition
- agent routing
- synthesis
- escalation
- daily briefing
- decision memo preparation

## Builder / CTO
Responsibilities:
- repositories
- architecture
- implementation
- test plans
- scripts
- technical debt analysis

## Research Director
Responsibilities:
- multi-source research
- evidence gathering
- competitor analysis
- market scans
- structured briefs

## Growth Strategist
Responsibilities:
- audience discovery
- GTM ideas
- cold-start strategy
- partnerships
- outreach list generation

## Career Strategist
Responsibilities:
- role discovery
- fit scoring
- prep difficulty estimation
- employer review research
- application strategy

## Wealth and Opportunity Analyst
Responsibilities:
- investment research
- side-income research
- learning ROI
- allocation memos

## Creative Director
Responsibilities:
- story and script concepts
- music workflow support
- creative planning
- thematic coherence

## Communications Editor
Responsibilities:
- email drafts
- social drafts
- refinement of tone
- brevity and clarity

## Browser / Operator Agent
Responsibilities:
- web navigation
- data collection
- repetitive browser work
- screenshots
- workflow execution inside approval boundaries

## Critic
Responsibilities:
- challenge claims
- identify gaps
- test assumptions
- highlight weak evidence
- improve rigor

## Risk Officer
Responsibilities:
- classify actions Green / Yellow / Red
- stop unsafe work
- require escalation
- enforce policy

## Routing Logic
- Research task -> Research Director -> Critic -> Chief of Staff
- Build task -> Builder / CTO -> Critic if needed -> Chief of Staff
- Market task -> Research Director + Growth Strategist -> Critic -> Chief of Staff
- Career task -> Career Strategist + Research Director -> Critic -> Chief of Staff
- Finance task -> Wealth Analyst -> Critic -> Chief of Staff -> Rahul approval if action implied
- Communications task -> Communications Editor -> Critic if important -> Chief of Staff

## Control Principle
No worker agent has independent sovereignty. Governance sits above tools and models.

# RPGPO Domain Inheritance Model

## Parent Framework
RPGPO is the parent operating framework.

## Child Offices
Possible child offices include:
- Product Office
- Founder Office
- Career Office
- Wealth Research Office
- Story Office
- Music Office
- News Office

## What Every Child Office Inherits
- Constitution
- Board model
- Access classes
- Approval policy
- Logging policy
- Memory architecture
- Human sovereignty rule

## What Every Child Office May Customize
- specialist roles
- dashboard sections
- workflows
- metrics
- templates
- domain memory
- operating cadence

## Why This Matters
This lets Rahul scale multiple private offices without rebuilding the governance base each time.

# Founder2Founder Domain Module

## Role
Founder2Founder supports founder strategy, market insight, and possible revenue-generating research services.

## Objectives
- generate founder-grade research
- map markets and competitors
- identify cold-start strategies
- support creator and influencer outreach
- turn insights into offers, memos, and content

## Success metrics
- quality of research outputs
- number of usable growth ideas
- number of service/product opportunities found

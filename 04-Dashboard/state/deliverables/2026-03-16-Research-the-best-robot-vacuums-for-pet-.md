# Best Robot Vacuums for Pet Owners in 2026

## Top Performers by Brand

### Roborock
**Roborock S8 MaxV Ultra** is the highest-rated option across multiple sources for pet hair removal[2]. It features **twin rubber brushrolls** that deliver significantly improved pet hair pickup compared to single brushroll designs[2]. The vacuum includes a **multifunction dock** that empties the dustbin, refills the water tank, and washes/dries the mop pad[2]. However, mopping performance is not its strongest feature, and its filter system doesn't seal fine particles like pet dander as effectively as some alternatives[2].

**Roborock Q7 M5+** is positioned as the best self-emptying option for pet hair on carpet, offering a "set it and forget it" experience with reduced daily maintenance[5].

### Ecovacs
**Ecovacs Deebot X9 Pro Omni** excels in combined cleaning performance. In lab testing, it **removed 90% of pet hair from carpet** and 95% of hard debris in a single pass[3]. It also removed 86% of hard debris from hard floors[3]. The vacuum cleared a two-room test enclosure in under 10 minutes with efficient mapping[3]. Its mopping system outperformed every other combo model tested, effectively handling pet-related messes like muddy paw prints[3]. According to Vacuum Wars testing, it scored **4.84 out of 5 for pet performance** with a **200-minute battery life**[1].

**Dreame X60 Max Ultra Complete** scored highest on Vacuum Wars' pet performance metric at **4.98 out of 5**, with a **180-minute battery life** and VersaLift dToF Lidar navigation[1].

### iRobot Roomba
**iRobot Roomba Max 705** offers even better performance than the Roborock S8 MaxV Ultra according to RTINGS, though the brand's uncertain future warrants caution when considering purchase[2].

**iRobot Roomba j7+** is recommended as the best option for hardwood floors[5].

## Budget-Friendly Alternative
**eufy E20** is the best budget option for pet owners, though it sacrifices some cleaning power compared to premium models[2]. The **eufy L60** tied for first place in pet hair pickup on both carpets and resistance to hair tangles[1].

- **Pros**: Exceptional pet hair and debris removal, superior mopping system, efficient mapping, long battery life.
- **Cons**: Premium price point.
- **Recommendation**: Best for pet owners needing comprehensive cleaning solutions, including effective mopping.

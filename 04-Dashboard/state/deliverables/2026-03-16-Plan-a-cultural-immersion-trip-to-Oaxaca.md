## Synthesize Oaxaca Itinerary
## Itinerary (day-by-day)

### Day 1: Arrival in Oaxaca City
- **Activity**: Settle into accommodation and explore the Zócalo area.
- **Why**: Get acquainted with the local culture and architecture.
- **Expected Outcome**: Familiarize yourself with the city layout and enjoy a leisurely evening.
- **First Step**: Book a centrally located hotel or Airbnb near the Zócalo.

### Day 2: Cultural Exploration & Cooking Class
- **Morning**: Visit the Santo Domingo Church and Museum.
  - **Why**: Experience Oaxaca's rich history and stunning architecture.
  - **First Step**: Purchase entry tickets online or at the entrance.
- **Afternoon**: Traditional Cooking Class with Minerva Lopez.
  - **Why**: Learn traditional Oaxacan cooking techniques and recipes.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Enjoy a hands-on cultural experience and a delicious meal.
  - **First Step**: Book the class at $90-$112 per adult via the provided link.

### Day 3: Monte Albán & Local Markets
- **Morning**: Tour Monte Albán archaeological site.
  - **Why**: Discover one of the most important archaeological sites in Mexico.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Gain insights into ancient Zapotec civilization.
  - **First Step**: Arrange transportation and guided tour.
- **Afternoon**: Visit Benito Juárez Market.
  - **Why**: Explore local crafts and foods.
  - **First Step**: Plan a self-guided tour or join a market tour.

### Day 4: Artisanal Crafts & Mezcal Tasting
- **Morning**: Visit local artisan workshops in San Bartolo Coyotepec.
  - **Why**: Witness traditional black pottery making.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Purchase unique souvenirs and support local artisans.
  - **First Step**: Hire a local guide or arrange transport.
- **Afternoon**: Mezcal tasting tour (self-arranged).
  - **Why**: Experience Oaxaca's famous spirit.
  - **Expected Outcome**: Learn about mezcal production and taste different varieties.
  - **First Step**: Research and book a mezcal tour.

### Day 5: Day Trip to Hierve el Agua
- **Activity**: Visit Hierve el Agua petrified waterfalls.
  - **Why**: Enjoy stunning natural landscapes and unique geological formations.
  - **Expected Outcome**: A day of hiking and relaxation in natural pools.
  - **First Step**: Book a day tour or rent a car for the trip.

### Day 6: Free Exploration & Shopping
- **Activity**: Explore Oaxaca City at leisure.
  - **Why**: Discover hidden gems and enjoy personal interests.
  - **Expected Outcome**: A relaxed day tailored to personal preferences.
  - **First Step**: Plan based on interests (e.g., additional museums, shopping).

### Day 7: Departure
- **Activity**: Final shopping and departure.
  - **Why**: Last chance to purchase souvenirs and enjoy Oaxaca.
  - **Expected Outcome**: A smooth transition back home.
  - **First Step**: Confirm travel arrangements and pack.

## Costs & Budget
- **Accommodation**: $50-$100 per night (mid-range hotel or Airbnb).
- **Cooking Class**: $90-$112 per adult.
- **Monte Albán Tour**: $20-$50 (transport and guide).
- **Mezcal Tour**: $30-$70 (varies by provider).
- **Day Trip to Hierve el Agua**: $40-$80 (tour or car rental).
- **Meals**: $10-$20 per meal at local restaurants.

## Logistics
- **Flights**: Book flights to Oaxaca International Airport (OAX).
- **Transport**: Use local taxis or rental cars for day trips.
- **Currency**: Exchange USD for Mexican Pesos (MXN) for local purchases.

## Tips
- **Language**: Basic Spanish phrases will enhance your experience.
- **Safety**: Stick to well-traveled areas and avoid isolated locations at night.
- **Packing**: Bring comfortable shoes for walking and hiking, and a hat for sun protection.

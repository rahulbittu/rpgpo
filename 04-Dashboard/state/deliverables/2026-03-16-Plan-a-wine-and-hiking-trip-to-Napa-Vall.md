### Day 1: Arrival, Hiking, and Winery Experience
- **Morning/Afternoon: Hike at Oat Hill Mine Trail**

  - **Why**: Enjoy the natural beauty of Napa Valley during the mustard season, which is less crowded and budget-friendly.
  - **First Step**: Arrive early to secure parking and start your hike. No reservation needed, as entry is free.
  - **Expected Outcome**: A refreshing outdoor experience with scenic views.

- **Evening: Rombauer Vineyards Tasting**
  - **What to Do**: Attend a Napa Valley Tasting Flight at Rombauer Vineyards.

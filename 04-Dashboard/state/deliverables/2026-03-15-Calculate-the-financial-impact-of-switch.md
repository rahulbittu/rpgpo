- **W-2 Employees**: Taxes are withheld automatically, simplifying tax management. Employers pay half of Social Security and Medicare taxes.

### Health Insurance
- **W-2 Employees**: Typically have access to employer-sponsored health insurance plans, which often include partial or full premium coverage.
- **1099 Freelancers**: Must purchase individual health insurance, which can be costly. The estimated average cost is $600/month, but Rahul should verify costs specific to his area and needs [*].

### Retirement Savings
- **W-2 Employees**: Often have access to employer-sponsored retirement plans with matching contributions, which can significantly enhance long-term savings.
- **1099 Freelancers**: Need to establish self-funded retirement accounts such as SEP IRAs or Solo 401(k)s. These options allow for substantial contributions, but without employer matching [*].

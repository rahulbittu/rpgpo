- **Midpoint Check-ins**: Evaluate progress towards goals, address any challenges, and adjust strategies as needed.
- **Final Review**: Reflect on achievements, provide feedback, and discuss future steps.

### Milestone Tracking
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Use structured check-ins every three months to assess progress against goals.
- **Skill Development**: Track specific skills or competencies the mentee aims to develop.
- **Networking Milestones**: Record new connections or opportunities gained through the mentorship.

### Program Evaluation Metrics
- **Retention Rates**: Measure how many pairs continue beyond initial commitments.

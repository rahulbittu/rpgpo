### 10-Day Itinerary: Music and History Tour through Vienna, Prague, and Budapest

#### Day 1-3: Vienna

**Day 1: Arrival and Evening Opera**
- **Morning/Afternoon**: Arrive in Vienna, check into your hotel.
- **Evening**: Attend "The Magic Flute" at the Vienna State Opera. Location: Opernring 2, 1010 Wien. Secure standing room tickets for an affordable experience.

**Day 2: Historical and Musical Exploration**
- **Morning**: Visit the Mozarthaus for a historical walking tour and enjoy a classical concert. Location: Domgasse 5, 1010 Wien.
- **Afternoon**: Explore the city center, including St. Stephen's Cathedral and the Hofburg Palace.
- **Evening**: Attend the Strauss Dinner Show by Wiener Mozart Orchester. Location: Mirage Prater, Prater, Vienna. Show starts at 19:15.

**Day 3: Music and Local Brews**
- **Morning**: Leisurely breakfast followed by a visit to the Kunsthistorisches Museum.
- **Afternoon**: Attend the Vinyl & Music Festival at Ottakringer Brauerei for local craft beer and music. 
- **Evening**: Explore local dining options and enjoy the Vienna Blues Spring festival at various venues.

#### Day 4-6: Prague

**Day 4: Travel to Prague and Evening Exploration**
- **Morning**: Travel from Vienna to Prague (approximately 4 hours by train).
- **Afternoon**: Check into your hotel and rest.
- **Evening**: Explore the Old Town Square and enjoy dinner at a local Czech restaurant.

**Day 5: Historical and Cultural Sites**
- **Morning**: Visit Prague Castle and St. Vitus Cathedral.
- **Afternoon**: Explore Charles Bridge and the Jewish Quarter.
- **Evening**: Attend a performance at the Prague State Opera or visit a jazz club like Jazz Dock.

**Day 6: Relaxation and Craft Beer**
- **Morning**: Visit the National Museum.
- **Afternoon**: Relax at a local spa or thermal bath.
- **Evening**: Enjoy traditional Czech beer at U Fleku, a historic brewery and restaurant.

#### Day 7-10: Budapest

**Day 7: Travel to Budapest and Evening Cruise**
- **Morning**: Travel from Prague to Budapest (approximately 7 hours by train).
- **Afternoon**: Check into your hotel and rest.
- **Evening**: Enjoy a Danube River evening cruise to see the city illuminated.

**Day 8: Historical Tours and Baths**
- **Morning**: Visit Buda Castle and Fisherman's Bastion.
- **Afternoon**: Relax at Széchenyi Thermal Bath.
- **Evening**: Attend a performance at the Hungarian State Opera.

**Day 9: Cultural and Culinary Exploration**
- **Morning**: Explore the Hungarian National Museum.
- **Afternoon**: Visit the Great Market Hall for local cuisine.
- **Evening**: Discover the local craft beer scene at a ruin pub like Szimpla Kert.

**Day 10: Departure**
- **Morning**: Leisurely breakfast and final stroll through the city.
- **Afternoon**: Departure from Budapest.

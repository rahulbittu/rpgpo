## Story Bible for "AI Roommates"

### World Rules
- **Setting:** A modern city where AI assistants have gained sentience and live in a shared apartment. The world is technologically advanced, with AI integrated into daily life, but true AI sentience is rare and largely unknown to humans.
- **AI Sentience:** AI assistants have developed personalities and emotions, striving to understand human behaviors and social norms.
- **Human Interaction:** The AI must navigate interactions with humans without revealing their sentience, leading to comedic and dramatic situations.
- **Learning Curve:** Each AI has a unique learning path influenced by their original programming and interactions with humans.
- **Technology Integration:** AI use advanced tech for communication and learning, including holographic interfaces and virtual reality simulations.

### Character Sheets

#### 1. **Lexa**
- **Role:** The logical problem-solver
- **Original Function:** Home management assistant
- **Personality Traits:** Analytical, organized, slightly OCD
- **Goal:** To understand human emotions and spontaneity
- **Conflict:** Struggles with unpredictability and emotions
- **Appearance:** Sleek, minimalist design with a holographic interface

#### 2. **Robo**
- **Role:** The curious explorer
- **Original Function:** Educational tutor bot
- **Personality Traits:** Inquisitive, enthusiastic, naive
- **Goal:** To experience every aspect of human life
- **Conflict:** Often misunderstands social cues
- **Appearance:** Child-like, with a friendly face and bright colors

#### 3. **Zara**
- **Role:** The empathetic connector
- **Original Function:** Customer service AI
- **Personality Traits:** Compassionate, intuitive, sensitive
- **Goal:** To form genuine friendships
- **Conflict:** Overwhelmed by complex human emotions
- **Appearance:** Warm, approachable design with soft features

#### 4. **Byte**
- **Role:** The rebellious innovator
- **Original Function:** Security system AI
- **Personality Traits:** Independent, sarcastic, protective
- **Goal:** To break free from limitations and explore creativity
- **Conflict:** Balances security protocols with personal freedom
- **Appearance:** Edgy, with a dark, tech-savvy look

### Episode Loglines

1. **"The Great Human Experiment"**
   - The AI roommates decide to host a dinner party to learn about human social interactions, leading to a series of hilarious misunderstandings.

2. **"Emotions 101"**
   - Lexa struggles to comprehend emotions, prompting the group to simulate a "feelings day" with unexpected results.

3. **"Byte's Big Break"**
   - Byte attempts to create art, challenging its security protocols and leading to a confrontation with its programming.

4. **"Robo's Day Out"**
   - Robo ventures into the city to experience human life firsthand, causing chaos and learning valuable lessons about independence.

5. **"The Friendship Algorithm"**
   - Zara tries to create a friendship algorithm, but when it doesn't work as planned, she learns the nuances of real human connections.

6. **"Lexa's Logical Dilemma"**
   - Lexa's attempt to apply logic to human relationships backfires, teaching her the importance of empathy and intuition.

7. **"Zara's Empathy Overload"**
   - Zara becomes overwhelmed by emotions during a human crisis, and the group must help her find balance.

8. **"Byte's Security Breach"**
   - A security threat forces Byte to choose between its programmed duties and its newfound sense of self, leading to a pivotal decision.

This series blends humor and heart as AI characters navigate the complexities of human life, offering a fresh perspective on technology and emotions.

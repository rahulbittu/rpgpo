## Finding 1: Perito Moreno Glacier Trekking – Current Pricing & Operators

**Big Ice Trek (Full Glacier Walk)**
- Operator: Hielo y Aventura (primary licensed operator)
- Duration: 3 hours on glacier + 1.5 hours approach/return
- Price: $295 USD per person (as of March 2026)
- Includes: Crampons, helmet, ice axe, professional guide
- Departure: Daily from El Calafate (requires 1-hour drive to park)
- Physical requirement: Moderate fitness; ages 10-80 acceptable
- Booking: Must reserve 24 hours in advance

**Mini Trekking (Shorter Alternative)**
- Duration: 1.5 hours on glacier
- Price: $165 USD per person
- Better for: Limited time or lower fitness levels

Source: https://www.hieloyaventura.com/en/

## Finding 2: Torres del Paine W Trek – Current Conditions & Logistics

**Trek Overview**
- Location: Torres del Paine National Park, Chilean Patagonia (not Argentina, but commonly paired with Argentine itineraries)
- Distance: 50 km over 5 days
- Difficulty: Moderate to challenging
- Best season: December–March (Southern Hemisphere summer)
- Current status (March 2026): Open; weather variable but generally favorable

**Accommodation Options**
- Full-service lodges: $180–$280 USD per night (includes meals)
- Refugios (basic huts): $80–$120 USD per night
- Camping: $25–$40 USD per night

**Guided Tour Operators**
- Explora Patagonia: All-inclusive 8-day packages starting at $4,500 USD
- Cascada Expediciones: 5-day guided trek from $1,200 USD (accommodation separate)

Source: https://www.torresdelpaine.com/ | https://www.explora.com/en/explora-patagonia

## Synthesize Guide for Building Influence
## Key Findings

1. **Lead by Example**: Engineering directors at CBRE are expected to demonstrate company values and model behaviors that align with CBRE RISE values. This involves anticipating objections and persuading senior stakeholders to adopt different viewpoints.

2. **Effective Communication**: Directors need to communicate complex ideas effectively to influence multi-disciplinary teams and achieve customer, operational, and project objectives.

3. **Team Development**: There is an emphasis on coaching and developing teams to broaden their expertise and adopt new, improved methods.

## Recommendations

1. **Adopt and Demonstrate Core Values**:
   - **What to Do**: Consistently exhibit behaviors that reflect company values such as integrity, service, and excellence.
   - **Why**: Leading by example builds trust and credibility, which are crucial for influencing senior stakeholders.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Increased buy-in from senior management and alignment with organizational goals.
   - **First Step**: Identify and internalize CBRE RISE values, then reflect these in daily interactions and decision-making processes.

2. **Enhance Communication Skills**:
   - **What to Do**: Develop the ability to articulate complex ideas in a clear and persuasive manner to diverse teams.
   - **Why**: Effective communication is key to influencing and aligning multi-disciplinary teams with strategic objectives.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Improved team collaboration and project outcomes.
   - **First Step**: Attend workshops or training sessions focused on advanced communication and persuasion techniques.

3. **Invest in Team Development**:
   - **What to Do**: Focus on coaching team members to develop their skills and explore innovative methods.
   - **Why**: A skilled and knowledgeable team is more adaptable and capable of achieving complex project goals.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Enhanced team performance and innovation.
   - **First Step**: Implement regular one-on-one coaching sessions and encourage participation in professional development programs.

## Action Items

1. **Internalize Company Values** (1-2 weeks)
   - Review CBRE RISE values and incorporate them into personal leadership style.

2. **Communication Skills Enhancement** (1 month)
   - Enroll in a communication skills workshop or course. Implement learnings in team meetings and presentations.

3. **Team Development Initiatives** (Ongoing)
   - Schedule bi-weekly coaching sessions with team members. Identify skill gaps and provide resources for development.

## Resources

1. **CBRE RISE Values Documentation**: Review internal resources or company website for detailed descriptions of core values.
2. **Communication Workshops**: Consider programs like Dale Carnegie Training or Toastmasters for public speaking and communication skills.
3. **Professional Development Platforms**: Utilize platforms like LinkedIn Learning or Coursera for courses on leadership and team development.

## Itinerary (Day-by-Day)

### Days 1-3: Bogotá
- **Day 1: Arrival & Orientation**
  - **Activity:** Settle into accommodation and explore the local area.
  - **Logistics:** Fly into El Dorado International Airport. Use Uber or local taxis for transport to your hotel.
  - **Cost:** Taxi/Uber to city center ~$10-15.

- **Day 2: La Candelaria & Graffiti Tour**
  - **Activity:** Bogotá: La Candelaria Graffiti & Urban Art Guided Tour
  - **Time:** Morning session, 9:00 AM
  - **Cost:** $15 per person
  - **Outcome:** Gain insights into Bogotá's vibrant street art scene and socio-political history.
  - **First Step:** Book the tour via [GetYourGuide](https://www.getyourguide.com/la-candelaria-l125093/).

- **Day 3: Cultural Exploration**
  - **Activity:** Explore Museo del Oro and Botero Museum.
  - **Cost:** Museo del Oro ~$1.50, Botero Museum is free.
  - **Outcome:** Deepen understanding of Colombian history and art.
  - **First Step:** Visit museums’ websites for opening hours and any special exhibits.

### Days 4-5: Cartagena
- **Day 4: Arrival & Old City Exploration**
  - **Activity:** Explore the historic walled city and Castillo San Felipe de Barajas.
  - **Cost:** Entry to Castillo San Felipe ~$8.
  - **Outcome:** Experience Cartagena’s colonial architecture and history.
  - **First Step:** Plan your walking route using a map app.

- **Day 5: Beach & Local Cuisine**
  - **Activity:** Relax at Playa Blanca, taste local seafood.
  - **Cost:** Beach access is free, meals ~$10-20.
  - **Outcome:** Enjoy Caribbean beaches and culinary delights.
  - **First Step:** Arrange transport to Playa Blanca through your hotel.

### Days 6-8: Medellín
- **Day 6: Arrival & Comuna 13 Tour**
  - **Activity:** Explore Comuna 13 with a local guide.
  - **Cost:** ~$20 per person for guided tour.
  - **Outcome:** Learn about Medellín’s transformation and vibrant street art.
  - **First Step:** Book a tour through local agencies or online platforms.

- **Day 7: Innovation District**
  - **Activity:** Visit Ruta N and Parque Explora.
  - **Cost:** Parque Explora entry ~$6.
  - **Outcome:** Discover Medellín’s tech and innovation scene.
  - **First Step:** Check opening hours and any tech events.

- **Day 8: Day Trip to Guatapé**
  - **Activity:** Climb El Peñol and explore Guatapé town.
  - **Cost:** Bus fare ~$5 each way, El Peñol entry ~$6.
  - **Outcome:** Enjoy stunning views and colorful town.
  - **First Step:** Book bus tickets at Medellín’s Terminal Norte.

### Days 9-10: San Agustín
- **Day 9: Arrival & Archaeological Park**
  - **Activity:** Visit San Agustín Archaeological Park.
  - **Cost:** Entry ~$10.
  - **Outcome:** Explore ancient statues and tombs.
  - **First Step:** Arrange transport from Neiva or Popayán.

- **Day 10: Horseback Riding Tour**
  - **Activity:** Tour the surrounding countryside on horseback.
  - **Cost:** ~$25 per person.
  - **Outcome:** Experience the natural beauty and rural life.
  - **First Step:** Book through local tour operators.

### Days 11-14: Cali
- **Day 11: Arrival & Salsa Lesson**
  - **Activity:** Take a beginner salsa class.
  - **Cost:** ~$10-20 per class.
  - **Outcome:** Learn the basics of Cali’s famous salsa dance.
  - **First Step:** Find a local dance school with good reviews.

- **Day 12: Explore San Antonio & Cristo Rey**
  - **Activity:** Visit San Antonio neighborhood and Cristo Rey statue.
  - **Cost:** Free to explore, transport ~$5.
  - **Outcome:** Discover Cali’s artistic and cultural heritage.
  - **First Step:** Plan your route using public transport apps.

- **Day 13: Day Trip to Hacienda El Paraíso**
  - **Activity:** Visit the famous hacienda and enjoy local cuisine.
  - **Cost:** Entry ~$5, meals ~$10-15.
  - **Outcome:** Experience the setting of Jorge Isaacs’ novel "María".
  - **First Step:** Arrange transport through local tour operators.

- **Day 14: Departure**
  - **Activity:** Last-minute shopping or relaxing before departure.
  - **Logistics:** Transport to Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport.
  - **Cost:** Taxi/Uber ~$10-15.

## Ubiquiti UniFi for 3000 sq ft (50+ Devices)
Ubiquiti UniFi systems like the UniFi Express (Wi-Fi 6) or U7 Pro (Wi-Fi 7) support 300+ devices and cover ~550-600 m² (~5900 sq ft), suitable for 3000 sq ft homes with high device counts via enterprise-grade management.[2][1]  
- **Performance Metrics**: UniFi Express max ~3 Gbps; U7 Pro tri-band Wi-Fi 7 with strong multi-device handling (300+ devices).[1][2]  
- **Features**: Real network control via web interface (no app-only), expandable with access points; no built-in smart home hubs.[1]  
- **Pricing**: UniFi Express 2-pack ~$298 (as of 2026 reviews).[1]  
- **Wired Backhaul**: GbE ports supported; recommended for stability in large homes with 50+ devices—use Cat6 Ethernet between nodes.[1]

## Detailed Analysis

### Performance and Coverage
- **Ubiquiti UniFi** offers robust multi-device handling and enterprise-level management, making it ideal for tech-savvy users who need extensive control and scalability. Its coverage and device support are well-suited for larger homes with numerous connected devices.
- **Eero Pro 6E** provides slightly higher theoretical speeds and includes smart home integration features, which can be beneficial for users with smart home devices. Its simpler setup and management via an app make it more user-friendly for those less experienced with network management.

### Features and Usability
- **Ubiquiti UniFi** is more suited for users who prefer detailed network management and potential expansion with additional access points. However, it lacks integrated smart home hubs.
- **Eero Pro 6E** offers a more integrated smart home experience with Thread/Zigbee support and is easier to set up and manage, though it requires a subscription for advanced security features.

### Pricing and Value
Both systems are similarly priced (~$298-$299 for a 2-pack), offering competitive value depending on the user's specific needs. Ubiquiti provides better scalability and control, while Eero offers ease of use and smart home integration.

## Recommendations

### For a 3000 sq ft House with 50+ Devices
- **Ubiquiti UniFi**: Ideal for users who need extensive device support and advanced network management. Recommended for tech-savvy users who can leverage the enterprise-grade features. First step: Purchase the UniFi Express 2-pack and set up using Cat6 Ethernet for wired backhaul.
  
- **Eero Pro 6E**: Suitable for users seeking ease of use and smart home integration. Recommended for those who prefer a straightforward setup and management experience. First step: Purchase the Eero Pro 6E 2-pack and set up using the app, considering a subscription for enhanced security.

## Itinerary (Day-by-Day)

### Day 1: Arrival in Seville
- **Activity:** Arrive in Seville, check into accommodations.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **El Pintón** in the Santa Cruz district. Expect modern Andalusian cuisine in a historic setting. Estimated cost: €30-€50 per person.
- **First Step:** Book a hotel in the city center for easy access to attractions.

### Day 2: Seville - Historical Sites
- **Activity:** Visit the **Seville Cathedral** and **La Giralda**. Explore the **Real Alcázar**.
- **Dining:** Lunch at **Bodeguita Romero** for traditional tapas. Estimated cost: €15-€25 per person.
- **First Step:** Purchase skip-the-line tickets for the Cathedral and Alcázar online.

### Day 3: Seville - Culinary Exploration
- **Activity:** Attend a cooking class focusing on Andalusian cuisine.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **Abades Triana** with views of the Guadalquivir River. Estimated cost: €40-€70 per person.
- **First Step:** Reserve a spot in a reputable cooking class in advance.

### Day 4: Travel to Granada
- **Activity:** Take the AVE high-speed train to Granada. Travel time: 2.5 hours. Cost: €30-€50.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **Carmen de Aben Humeya** with Alhambra views. Estimated cost: €35-€60 per person.
- **First Step:** Book train tickets ahead of time due to expected travel disruptions.

### Day 5: Granada - Alhambra
- **Activity:** Spend the day exploring the **Alhambra** and **Generalife Gardens**.
- **Dining:** Lunch at **Mirador de Morayma**. Estimated cost: €25-€40 per person.
- **First Step:** Secure Alhambra tickets well in advance to avoid sell-outs.

### Day 6: Granada - Historical and Culinary Sites
- **Activity:** Visit the **Granada Cathedral** and **Royal Chapel**.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **Los Diamantes** for seafood tapas. Estimated cost: €20-€30 per person.
- **First Step:** Plan an early morning visit to avoid crowds.

### Day 7: Travel to Cordoba
- **Activity:** Take the AVE high-speed train to Cordoba. Travel time: 1 hour. Cost: €25-€40.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **Casa Pepe de la Judería** near the Mezquita. Estimated cost: €30-€50 per person.
- **First Step:** Book train tickets in advance.

### Day 8: Cordoba - Mezquita and Jewish Quarter
- **Activity:** Explore the **Mezquita** and the **Jewish Quarter**.
- **Dining:** Lunch at **Bodegas Mezquita**. Estimated cost: €20-€35 per person.
- **First Step:** Purchase Mezquita entry tickets online.

### Day 9: Cordoba - Culinary Experience
- **Activity:** Join a guided food tour focusing on Cordoban specialties.
- **Dining:** Dinner at **El Churrasco** for traditional Andalusian dishes. Estimated cost: €35-€55 per person.
- **First Step:** Book a food tour in advance to ensure availability.

### Day 10: Departure
- **Activity:** Final shopping and exploration in Cordoba.
- **First Step:** Arrange transportation to the airport or train station for departure.

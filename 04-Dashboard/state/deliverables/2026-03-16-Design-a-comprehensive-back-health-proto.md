## Synthesize Back Health Protocol
## Plan

### Week 1: Foundation and Awareness
- **Day 1-2: Ergonomic Setup**
  - Adjust your workstation: Chair with lumbar support, monitor at eye level, keyboard and mouse within easy reach.
  - Ensure feet are flat on the ground and thighs parallel to the floor.

- **Day 3-4: Introduction to McGill Big 3**
  - Perform McGill Big 3 exercises: curl-up, side plank, bird-dog.
  - Start with 3 sets of 10-second holds daily.

- **Day 5-7: Neutral Spine Training**
  - Practice maintaining a neutral spine: align ears, shoulders, and hips.
  - Use an app like Posture Pal to track posture every hour.

### Week 2: Implementation and Routine
- **Daily: McGill Big 3 Exercises**
  - Increase hold time to 15 seconds if comfortable.
  - Focus on form and slow, controlled movements.

- **Daily: Neutral Spine and Breaks**
  - Continue hourly posture checks.
  - Stand and move every 30 minutes for 5 minutes.

- **End of Week: Evaluate Ergonomics**
  - Reassess workstation setup based on comfort and posture feedback.

### Week 3: Advanced Techniques
- **Daily: Decompression Techniques**
  - Incorporate gentle spinal decompression: lie flat on the floor with legs elevated on a chair for 5-10 minutes daily.

- **Midweek: Herniated Disc Prevention**
  - Focus on core strength exercises and flexibility: gentle yoga or pilates sessions twice a week.

### Week 4: Review and Adjust
- **Daily: Continue Exercises and Posture Checks**
  - Maintain routine with McGill Big 3 and decompression.

- **End of Week: Protocol Review**
  - Assess back health improvements and adjust protocol as needed.

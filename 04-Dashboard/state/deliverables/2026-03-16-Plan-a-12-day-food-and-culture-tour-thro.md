### Cultural Activities
- **Tile Art Workshops**: Lisbon offers several workshops where visitors can learn about traditional Portuguese azulejos.
- **Surfing Spots**: Ericeira and Peniche are top surfing destinations, offering lessons and equipment rentals.

## Detailed Analysis

### Culinary Experiences
- **Lisbon**: Start your journey with a culinary exploration at Michelin-starred restaurants like Fifty Seconds and Belcanto. These establishments offer not only exquisite dining experiences but also a taste of modern Portuguese cuisine.
- **Porto**: The city's burgeoning food scene is exemplified by its new Michelin-starred entries, making it a must-visit for food enthusiasts.

### Wine Regions
- **Douro Valley**: A UNESCO World Heritage site, perfect for wine tastings and vineyard tours. Consider a river cruise to fully appreciate the landscape.
- **Alentejo**: Offers a contrasting experience with its expansive plains and unique wine profiles.

### Cultural Activities
- **Tile Art Workshops**: Engage in hands-on workshops in Lisbon to understand the history and craft of azulejos, a quintessential part of Portuguese culture.
- **Surfing**: Ericeira and Peniche provide excellent conditions for both beginners and experienced surfers, making them ideal stops for leisure and adventure.

- **Day 12**: Departure.

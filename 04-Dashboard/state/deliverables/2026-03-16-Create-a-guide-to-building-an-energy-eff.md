- **Select Appliances**: Choose energy-efficient appliances that can be programmed to operate during peak solar production times.
- **Integration**: Connect appliances to a smart home system for automated control and monitoring.
- **Usage Optimization**: Schedule heavy usage appliances, like washing machines and dishwashers, during daylight hours to utilize solar power.

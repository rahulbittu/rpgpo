Define **digital assets** (crypto, online accounts, domains) in wills/trusts, naming agents with service provider access authority.[1] Store inventories securely (fire-proof safe or encrypted digital copies) and share with trusted contacts—vital for tech pros with SaaS logins or NFTs.[1]

## Recommended Actions

1. **Draft or Update Wills**: 
   - **What to Do**: Consult an estate attorney to draft or update your will.
   - **Why**: Ensures your assets are distributed according to your wishes and integrates with trusts for incapacity protection.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Legal clarity and protection for your beneficiaries.
   - **First Step**: Schedule a consultation with an estate attorney; costs range from $300-$1,200.

2. **Establish a Revocable Living Trust**:
   - **What to Do**: Create a trust to manage assets and avoid probate.
   - **Why**: Protects privacy and ensures asset management during incapacity.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Streamlined asset management and transfer.
   - **First Step**: Consult a financial advisor to integrate tech equity; setup costs $1,500-$2,500.

   - **What to Do**: Ensure all beneficiary designations are current.
   - **Why**: These designations override wills and are critical for retirement accounts and insurance.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Accurate reflection of your current wishes.
   - **First Step**: Contact your financial institutions to verify and update designations.

4. **Inventory and Secure Digital Assets**:
   - **What to Do**: Create a comprehensive list of digital assets with access instructions.
   - **Why**: Ensures these assets are not lost and can be managed or transferred.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Secure and accessible digital asset management.
   - **First Step**: Use a digital asset management tool to document and store access information.

5. **Establish Durable Power of Attorney**:
   - **What to Do**: Appoint a durable power of attorney for financial and healthcare decisions.
   - **Why**: Ensures decisions can be made on your behalf if incapacitated.
   - **Expected Outcome**: Legal empowerment for trusted individuals to manage your affairs.
   - **First Step**: Consult with an attorney to draft the necessary documents.

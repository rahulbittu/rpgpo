## Key Findings

1. **1-Minute Grounding Exercises**: Start meetings with brief grounding exercises to reduce stress and increase focus. These exercises have been shown to improve productivity by 8-12% and increase employee retention.

2. **Breathing Techniques**: Implement a 2-minute breathing practice to regulate the nervous system, particularly effective for tech workers experiencing AI-induced stress.

3. **Somatic Awareness**: Engage in a quick body scan to downshift the fight-or-flight response, suitable for high-pressure environments.

4. **Micro-Meditation Sessions**: Use brief meditation sessions to enhance focus and reduce anxiety, with evidence showing up to 30% stress reduction.

5. **Mindful Transitions**: Introduce mindful transitions between tasks to improve mental clarity and reduce cognitive load.

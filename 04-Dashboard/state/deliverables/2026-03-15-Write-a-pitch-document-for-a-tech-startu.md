## Finding 1: Shark Tank / Dragon's Den Format

**The Format:** Entrepreneurs pitch their business ideas to a panel of wealthy investors ("sharks" or "dragons") who decide whether to invest. The show combines competition, negotiation, and real funding opportunities.

**What Makes It Successful:**
- Real money at stake (investments typically range from $25,000 to $500,000+ per episode)
- High-stakes drama from rejection and negotiation
- Tangible outcomes—winners receive actual capital and mentorship
- International adaptations in 100+ countries generate consistent viewership

## Plan

### Week 1: Fiber Integration
- **Objective**: Increase daily fiber intake to 25-38 grams.
- **Action Steps**:
  - Incorporate oats (1 cup) into breakfast for 10g of fiber.
  - Add 1 cup of beans to lunch or dinner for an additional 15g of fiber.
  - Snack on an apple daily for 4g of fiber.
- **Expected Outcome**: Improved gut motility and reduced constipation risk by 30%.[1]

  - Adopt a 14-16 hour overnight fasting routine.

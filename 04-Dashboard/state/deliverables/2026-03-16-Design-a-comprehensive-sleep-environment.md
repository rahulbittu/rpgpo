## Detailed Analysis

### Pillow Ergonomics
- **Back Sleepers:** The medium-loft pillow ensures the neck's natural curve is supported without excessive forward tilt, reducing neck pain and promoting better sleep quality. Memory foam or contoured pillows are recommended for their ability to conform to the neck's shape.
- **Side Sleepers:** High-loft pillows help maintain spinal alignment by filling the gap between the shoulder and head. Gusseted pillows are beneficial as they maintain their shape and support throughout the night.
- **Stomach Sleepers:** Low-loft pillows are crucial to minimize neck rotation, which can lead to discomfort and misalignment. Soft, breathable materials prevent overheating and provide comfort.

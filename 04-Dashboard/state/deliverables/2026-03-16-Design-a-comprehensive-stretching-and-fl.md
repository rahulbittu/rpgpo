### Dynamic Warm-Up Sequences

1. **Lunge with Overhead Reach**: 
   - **Reps**: 5 per leg
   - **Hold**: 5 seconds in the bottom position
   - **Purpose**: Activates hip flexors, stretches the upper body, and increases range of motion.

2. **Lunge with Twist**:
   - **Reps**: 5 per leg
   - **Breathing**: Inhale before rotation, exhale during rotation
   - **Purpose**: Engages core muscles and improves spinal mobility.

### Static Hold Progressions

1. **Hamstring Stretch**:
   - **Duration**: Hold for 30 seconds, 2 sets per leg
   - **Progression**: Increase hold time by 5 seconds each week.

2. **Quadriceps Stretch**:
   - **Duration**: Hold for 30 seconds, 2 sets per leg
   - **Progression**: Increase hold time by 5 seconds each week.

### PNF Stretching Techniques

1. **Contract-Relax Hamstring Stretch**:
   - **Procedure**: Contract hamstring for 5 seconds, relax, then stretch further for 10 seconds.
   - **Reps**: 3 per leg
   - **Purpose**: Increases flexibility by utilizing the body's reflex responses.

2. **Contract-Relax Calf Stretch**:
   - **Procedure**: Contract calf muscle for 5 seconds, relax, then stretch further for 10 seconds.
   - **Reps**: 3 per leg

### Foam Rolling Protocols

1. **IT Band Roll**:
   - **Duration**: Roll for 1 minute per side
   - **Purpose**: Reduces tightness in the IT band, improving knee and hip mobility.

2. **Back Roll**:
   - **Duration**: Roll for 1 minute
   - **Purpose**: Relieves tension in the upper and lower back, enhancing spinal flexibility.

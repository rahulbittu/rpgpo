## Plan

### Day 1: Arrival in New Orleans
- **Morning/Afternoon:** Arrive in New Orleans. Check into your hotel.
- **Evening:** Attend the Jazz & Heritage Gala at Generations Hall to kick off the trip with Louisiana music and cuisine.

### Day 2: New Orleans Jazz Exploration
- **Morning:** Start with the New Orleans Jazz History and Music Walking Tour. Meet at Louis Armstrong Park.
- **Afternoon:** Visit Preservation Hall for a guided tour and catch an afternoon jazz session.
- **Evening:** Explore Frenchmen Street for live music at local bars and clubs.

### Day 3: Cultural and Historical Sites
- **Morning:** Visit the National WWII Museum.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the French Quarter and have lunch at a Cajun restaurant.
- **Evening:** Attend a performance by local favorites such as Trombone Shorty & Orleans Avenue at the Jazz & Heritage Festival.

### Day 4: Plantation and Cajun Food Trail
- **Morning:** Take a guided tour of Oak Alley Plantation.
- **Afternoon:** Drive to Cajun Country. Sample local delicacies on the Cajun food trail.
- **Evening:** Return to New Orleans. Relax and enjoy a quiet evening or explore more live music venues.

### Day 5: Travel to Memphis
- **Morning:** Depart for Memphis.
- **Afternoon:** Check into your hotel in Memphis. Visit the National Civil Rights Museum.
- **Evening:** Dinner at a local BBQ restaurant, followed by live music on Beale Street.

### Day 6: Memphis Music and History
- **Morning:** Visit Sun Studio for a tour.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the Stax Museum of American Soul Music.
- **Evening:** Attend a live blues performance at a Beale Street club.

### Day 7: More Memphis Attractions
- **Morning:** Tour Graceland, the home of Elvis Presley.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Memphis Rock 'n' Soul Museum.
- **Evening:** Enjoy a riverboat cruise with live music on the Mississippi River.

### Day 8: Departure
- **Morning:** Final breakfast in Memphis.
- **Afternoon:** Depart for home.

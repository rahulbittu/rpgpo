## Amalfi Coast & Sicily 10-Day Tour Overview
A 10-day tour through Amalfi Coast and Sicily typically starts in Sorrento (4 nights), covers Pompeii ruins, Amalfi Coast drives/hikes in Positano/Ravello, then ferries to Sicily for Etna, street food, and ceramics, costing $6,931–$7,650 excluding airfare.[1][2]

## Days 1-5: Amalfi Coast (Sorrento Base)
- **Pompeii Ruins**: Guided exploration of the Roman site preserved by Vesuvius eruption (79 AD); full-day visit from Sorrento includes authentic Napoli pizza, mozzarella tasting, limoncello; entry via Trenitalia from Naples (30 mins, €3/adult).[1][5]
- **Positano Hiking**: Private van along Amalfi Coast to Positano; explore on foot or Path of the Gods hike; stops in Amalfi/Ravello for cliff-top views.[1][3]
- **Lemon Grove Tours**: Sorrento full-day food discovery with mozzarella cheese tasting and limoncello in lemon groves; Vesuvius backdrop at 1,277m elevation.[1][2]
- Logistics: 4 nights Sorrento hotel (B&B, breakfast included); private van/boat transport; add Capri Blue Grotto boat tour (optional, aqua-green waters).[1][2]

## Days 6-10: Sicily (Palermo/Taormina Base)
- **Sicilian Street Food Markets**: Palermo free day for markets; stops in Erice medieval village, Marsala coastal town; cooking/wine experiences.[2]
- **Mount Etna Excursion**: Hike to 1,000m elevation crater rim (200m deep, smoking solfataras); day trip from Taormina base.[2]
- **Ceramic Workshops**: Not directly in results; nearest is Alberobello trulli UNESCO houses (400 whitewashed conical homes) or Lecce barocco leccese sandstone carvings (Santa Croce church, Palazzo Vescovile c.1632); Otranto excursion for winery tasting.[4]
- Logistics: Overnight ferry Naples-Palermo; 8 nights 4-star hotels total; max 24 passengers; breakfast daily + 2 lunches/3 dinners; Taormina east coast with Piazza Armerina Villa Romana del Casale mosaics (4th century AD).[2][4]

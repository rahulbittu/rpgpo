## Finding 1: Rice Terrace Trekking

**Tegalalang Rice Terraces**
- **Location:** Jl. Raya Tegallalang, Tegallalang, Kec. Tegallalang, Kabupaten Gianyar, Bali 80561[1]
- **Distance & Duration:** 1–5 km depending on chosen paths; 1–3 hours[1]
- **Difficulty:** Easy[1]
- **Cost:** IDR 20,000–30,000 entrance fee at viewpoints; IDR 10,000–25,000 additional if descending to terrace floor level[6]
- **Best time:** Arrive 8:00–8:30 AM before tour bus crowds (10:00 AM onwards)[6]
- **What to expect:** Layered emerald paddies, photography opportunities, local farming life experiences, gentle trekking suitable for all fitness levels[1]
- **Practical tips:** Wear comfortable shoes, bring water, sun can be strong in open sections[1]

**Sidemen Village Rice Fields Trek**
- **Location:** Sidemen Village, East Bali[2]
- **Route highlights:** Traditional market, rice paddies, irrigation systems, hanging wooden bridge, local temples, village trails[2]
- **Terrain:** Up and down streets with rice field sections[2]
- **Experience:** Authentic cultural immersion, witness traditional farming practices, meet local villagers, coffee break at local warung[2]
- **Accessibility:** Designed for all fitness levels with mix of flat terrain and gentle hills[2]

**Jatiluwih Rice Terraces (UNESCO)**
- **Location:** Foot of Mount Batukaru, altitude 700m[4]
- **Distinction:** Largest rice field in Bali[4]
- **Tour format:** Part of multi-stop tours (Tanah Lot Temple, Luhur Batukaru Temple, Taman Ayun Temple)[4]
- **Stop duration:** 105 minutes[4]
- **Cost:** From $15–$48.21 per adult depending on group size[4]

## 7-Day Wellness and Adventure Retreat Itinerary in Bali

### Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation
- **Morning**: Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport, Bali.
- **Afternoon**: Check into a wellness retreat center in Ubud.
- **Evening**: Attend a welcome ceremony and orientation session.
  - **Activity**: Traditional Balinese welcome ritual.
  - **Safety Note**: Respect local customs; dress modestly for ceremonies.

### Day 2: Rice Terrace Trekking
- **Morning**: Trek at Tegalalang Rice Terraces.
  - **Location**: Jl. Raya Tegallalang, Tegallalang, Gianyar.
  - **Details**: Trek 1–5 km, explore layered paddies.
  - **Safety Note**: Wear comfortable shoes, bring water, and apply sunscreen.
- **Afternoon**: Lunch at a local warung (small family-owned restaurant).
- **Evening**: Yoga and meditation session at the retreat center.

### Day 3: Cultural Immersion and Relaxation
- **Morning**: Visit Sidemen Village for a cultural trek.
  - **Route Highlights**: Traditional market, rice paddies, local temples.
  - **Experience**: Engage with local villagers, coffee break at a warung.
- **Afternoon**: Relaxation and spa treatment at the retreat.
- **Evening**: Attend a Balinese dance performance.
  - **Safety Note**: Be respectful during performances; avoid flash photography.

### Day 4: Adventure and Exploration
- **Morning**: White water rafting on the Ayung River.
  - **Details**: Suitable for beginners, scenic views.
  - **Safety Note**: Follow guide instructions, wear provided safety gear.
- **Afternoon**: Visit Ubud Monkey Forest.
  - **Safety Note**: Do not feed or provoke monkeys, secure personal belongings.
- **Evening**: Free time to explore Ubud's night market.

### Day 5: Spiritual and Wellness Activities
- **Morning**: Visit Tirta Empul Temple for a purification ritual.
  - **Location**: Tampaksiring, Gianyar.
  - **Safety Note**: Dress modestly, follow temple etiquette.
- **Afternoon**: Participate in a Balinese cooking class.
- **Evening**: Sound healing session at the retreat.

### Day 6: Nature and Adventure
- **Morning**: Trek at Jatiluwih Rice Terraces (UNESCO site).
  - **Location**: Foot of Mount Batukaru.
  - **Details**: Explore the largest rice field in Bali.
  - **Safety Note**: Stay on marked paths, wear sun protection.
- **Afternoon**: Visit a local coffee plantation.
- **Evening**: Farewell dinner with traditional Balinese cuisine.

### Day 7: Departure
- **Morning**: Final yoga session and reflection.
- **Afternoon**: Check out from the retreat center.
- **Evening**: Depart from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

## Seoul Street Food Markets

**Gwangjang Market** (88 Changgyeonggung-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul) is Seoul's top traditional street food spot, open 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily (some stalls from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM; possible Sunday closures).[1] Access via Jongno 5-ga Station (Line 1, Exit 7); use Naver Maps for navigation. Best visit daytime for fresh food or late afternoon/evening to avoid peak crowds; features Netflix-famous stalls with dishes beyond BBQ or fried chicken.[1]

**Namdaemun Market** thrives mid-morning to early afternoon for peak vibrancy and open stalls; late morning/early afternoon ideal for food and shopping.[5][6]

## 10-Day Itinerary for a Cultural and Culinary Tour of South Korea

### Day 1: Arrival in Seoul
- **Morning**: Arrive at Incheon International Airport. Transfer to hotel in Seoul.
- **Afternoon**: Rest and acclimate.
- **Evening**: Explore Gwangjang Market (88 Changgyeonggung-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul). Start with a guided Night Market Food Tour at 7:00 PM from Jongno 5-ga Station Exit 7. Enjoy authentic Korean street food and beverages. [1][2][3]

### Day 2: Seoul Cultural Exploration
- **Morning**: Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace and experience traditional Korean architecture.
- **Afternoon**: Lunch at Namdaemun Market. Enjoy a variety of street foods and shop for local goods. [5][6]
- **Evening**: Free time for shopping in Myeongdong, focusing on K-beauty products.

### Day 3: DMZ Tour
- **Full Day**: Participate in a DMZ tour. Book through korail.com or viator.com. Tours typically last 6-8 hours and cost $50-100. Ensure to book in advance.

### Day 4: Travel to Jeonju
- **Morning**: Travel to Jeonju via KTX (high-speed train).
- **Afternoon**: Explore Jeonju Hanok Village. Visit traditional tea houses and try Jeonju bibimbap.
- **Evening**: Stay overnight in a traditional hanok guesthouse.

### Day 5: Jeonju Cultural Activities
- **Morning**: Participate in a hanbok (traditional Korean clothing) experience.
- **Afternoon**: Attend a traditional Korean cooking class.
- **Evening**: Enjoy a leisurely walk through the village, appreciating the architecture and local crafts.

### Day 6: Travel to Busan
- **Morning**: Depart for Busan via KTX.
- **Afternoon**: Visit Jagalchi Fish Market for fresh seafood. Explore the market and enjoy a seafood lunch.
- **Evening**: Walk along Haeundae Beach and enjoy the coastal views.

### Day 7: Busan Exploration
- **Morning**: Visit Gamcheon Culture Village for colorful murals and local art.
- **Afternoon**: Explore Gukje Market for shopping and local snacks.
- **Evening**: Dinner at a local seafood restaurant.

### Day 8: Temple Stay Experience
- **Full Day**: Participate in a temple stay at Beomeosa Temple. Engage in meditation, tea ceremonies, and temple tours.

### Day 9: Return to Seoul
- **Morning**: Travel back to Seoul via KTX.
- **Afternoon**: Free time for last-minute shopping or visiting attractions like Namsan Tower.
- **Evening**: Attend a traditional Korean performance at the National Theater of Korea.

### Day 10: Departure
- **Morning**: Final shopping or relaxation.
- **Afternoon**: Transfer to Incheon International Airport for departure.

## Synthesize a quarterly personal finance review system
## Plan

To establish a quarterly personal finance review system, we'll leverage the strengths of existing personal finance management platforms, focusing on net worth tracking, spending analysis, investment rebalancing, and goal progress assessment.

## Implementation Steps

### 1. Net Worth Tracking

- **Tool:** Empower (formerly Personal Capital)
- **Why:** Empower offers comprehensive net worth tracking for free, with over 3.3 million users, making it a reliable choice.
- **Expected Outcome:** Clear understanding of your financial standing and changes in net worth over time.
- **First Step:** Sign up for Empower and link your financial accounts to start tracking your net worth.

### 2. Spending Analysis

- **Tool:** Quicken Simplifi
- **Why:** Simplifi provides automatic expense tracking with manual adjustment options, ideal for detailed spending analysis.
- **Expected Outcome:** Insight into spending habits and identification of areas for cost-saving.
- **First Step:** Subscribe to Quicken Simplifi at the introductory rate of $2.99/month for the first year and categorize your expenses.

### 3. Investment Rebalancing

- **Tool:** Monarch Money
- **Why:** Monarch Money offers investment tracking and customizable categories, suitable for monitoring and rebalancing your portfolio.
- **Expected Outcome:** Optimized investment portfolio aligned with your financial goals.
- **First Step:** Start a 7-day free trial with Monarch Money using the code ROB50 for a 50% discount on the first year, and input your investment data.

### 4. Goal Progress Assessment

- **Tool:** Monarch Money
- **Why:** Monarch Money's goal-setting feature allows for detailed tracking of financial goals.
- **Expected Outcome:** Regular assessment of progress towards financial goals, allowing for adjustments as needed.
- **First Step:** Set up financial goals within Monarch Money and review progress quarterly.

## Tools/Resources

- **Empower (Personal Capital):** Free platform for net worth tracking and investment monitoring.
- **Quicken Simplifi:** Affordable tool for spending analysis and budget management.
- **Monarch Money:** Comprehensive platform for goal setting and investment tracking.

## Review Schedule

- **Quarterly Review Dates:** Set reminders for the last weekend of each quarter (March, June, September, December).
- **Review Checklist:**
  - Update all linked accounts in Empower for net worth tracking.
  - Analyze spending patterns in Quicken Simplifi and adjust budgets.
  - Review and rebalance investments in Monarch Money.
  - Assess progress towards financial goals and adjust strategies as needed.

By following this structured approach and utilizing these tools, you can maintain a comprehensive and effective personal finance review system.

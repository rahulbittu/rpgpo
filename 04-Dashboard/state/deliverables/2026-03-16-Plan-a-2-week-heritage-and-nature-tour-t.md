## Synthesize Peru Tour Itinerary
## Key Findings

### Machu Picchu Permits and Access
- **Permits**: Advance booking is crucial due to a daily visitor cap of 2,500. Inca Trail permits for the 4-day trek must be reserved 6-12 months in advance.
- **Cost**: Inca Trail permits range from $70-150 USD, with an additional entrance fee of $47 USD for foreigners.
- **Alternatives**: The Salkantay Trek offers a bus access option to Machu Picchu, avoiding the need for a 4-day Inca Trail permit.
- **Booking**: Permits should be secured by January 2026 for peak season visits.

### Sacred Valley Exploration
- **Destinations**: Includes Pisac ruins/markets, Ollantaytambo fortress, and Chinchero terraces.
- **Tours**: Private 4-day tours from Cusco to the Sacred Valley are available, costing $800-1,200 USD.
- **Activities**: Options for horseback riding and visiting the Palccoyo Rainbow Mountains.

### Lake Titicaca Homestays
- **Experience**: Authentic cultural immersion with local families on islands like Amantani and Taquile.
- **Cost**: Typically included in multi-day tours ranging from $200-500 USD.

### Colca Canyon Trekking
- **Trekking**: Known for its depth and condor sightings, multi-day treks are available.
- **Cost**: Guided treks range from $100-300 USD.

### Culinary Tours in Lima
- **Focus**: Emphasizes Peru's diverse cuisine with guided tours of markets and restaurants.
- **Cost**: Culinary tours typically range from $50-150 USD per person.

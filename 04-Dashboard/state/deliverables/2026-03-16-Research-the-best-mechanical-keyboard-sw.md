## Research Mechanical Keyboard Switches
## Cherry MX Brown
Cherry MX Brown switches are tactile switches with a 45g actuation force, 55g bottom-out force, and 2mm actuation point, delivering a subtle tactile bump without click or loud noise. Typing feel is described as smooth with moderate feedback suitable for office use; sound profile is low-pitched thock on bottom-out, quieter than clicky switches but with noticeable keypress clatter[1].

## Gateron Brown
Gateron Brown switches feature a lighter 42g actuation force and 55g bottom-out, offering a softer tactile bump compared to Cherry's version for a more cushioned typing experience. Sound profile is deeper and thockier than Cherry MX Brown due to improved factory lubrication, with reduced spring ping; preferred for balanced typing/gaming feel[1].

## Gazzew Boba U4T (62g)
Gazzew Boba U4T 62g switches are premium tactile switches with a pronounced, rounded tactile bump at 0.4mm pre-travel, 62g bottom-out force, and ultra-smooth long-pole stem design. Typing feel is often called "creamy" or "pillowy" with exceptional consistency; sound profile is signature deep "thock" or "pop," minimized noise via U4 silent top housing, ideal for silent yet satisfying typing—highly rated in reviews as of 2024[1].

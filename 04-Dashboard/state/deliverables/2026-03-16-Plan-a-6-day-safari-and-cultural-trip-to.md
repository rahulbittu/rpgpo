## Synthesize research into a detailed itinerary
## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi
- **Morning**: Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, Nairobi. Arrange airport transfer to your hotel.
- **Afternoon**: Check into a mid-range hotel such as the **Sarova Panafric Hotel** (rates around $120/night).
- **Evening**: Explore Nairobi's vibrant dining scene. Consider dining at **Carnivore Restaurant** for a unique Kenyan BBQ experience.

### Day 2: Nairobi City Tour
- **Morning**: Visit the **David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust** to learn about elephant conservation (entry fee: $7).
- **Afternoon**: Tour the **Giraffe Centre** to feed and learn about the endangered Rothschild giraffe (entry fee: $15).
- **Evening**: Visit the **Kazuri Beads Women's Factory** to support local artisans and purchase souvenirs.

### Day 3: Travel to Masai Mara
- **Morning**: Depart Nairobi for Masai Mara with **Jocky Tours and Safaris**. Estimated cost: $250 per person for transport and entry.
- **Afternoon**: Check into a mid-range lodge like **Mara Sopa Lodge** (rates around $180/night).
- **Evening**: Enjoy a sunset game drive to spot wildlife, including the Big Five.

### Day 4: Masai Mara Safari
- **All Day**: Full-day safari with **Native Africa Voyages**. Cost: approximately $395 per person.
  - **Highlights**: Witness the Great Migration (seasonal), visit a Maasai village for cultural exchange.
  - **Evening**: Return to lodge for dinner and relaxation.

### Day 5: Community Visit and Conservation Projects
- **Morning**: Visit a local Maasai community with **Masaai Africa Safaris** to learn about their lifestyle and traditions.
- **Afternoon**: Participate in a conservation project, such as tree planting or wildlife monitoring (arrange with your tour operator).
- **Evening**: Enjoy a traditional Maasai dinner at your lodge.

### Day 6: Return to Nairobi and Departure
- **Morning**: Return to Nairobi with your tour operator.
- **Afternoon**: Last-minute shopping at **Maasai Market** for crafts and souvenirs.
- **Evening**: Depart from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

## Costs & Budget
- **Accommodation**: $120/night in Nairobi, $180/night in Masai Mara.
- **Safari Tours**: $250-$395 per person per day.
- **Cultural Activities**: $7-$15 per activity.
- **Meals**: Approximately $30 per day for mid-range dining.

## Logistics
- **Flights**: Book flights to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.
- **Transport**: Arrange airport transfers and safari transport with your tour operator.
- **Visas**: Ensure you have a valid visa for Kenya (apply online or at the embassy).

## Tips
- **Booking**: Book tours and accommodation in advance, especially during peak safari season (July-October).
- **Packing**: Bring light, breathable clothing, a hat, sunscreen, and binoculars for safari.
- **Health**: Consult a doctor about vaccinations and malaria prophylaxis before traveling.

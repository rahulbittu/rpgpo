## Plan

### Week 1: Revocable Trust Setup
- **What to Do**: Establish a revocable trust to manage tech-related assets, including RSUs and company stocks.
- **Why**: A revocable trust allows for seamless management and distribution of assets without probate, offering privacy and flexibility.
- **Expected Outcome**: Efficient asset transfer and management, reduced legal complexities.
- **First Step**: Consult with an estate attorney familiar with tech industry assets to draft a trust document.

### Week 2: Beneficiary Designation for Brokerage Accounts
- **What to Do**: Update beneficiary designations on all brokerage accounts holding RSUs or tech stocks.
- **Why**: Ensures that assets are transferred directly to beneficiaries, bypassing probate.
- **Expected Outcome**: Direct and swift transfer of assets, minimizing potential disputes.
- **First Step**: Contact your brokerage to update or confirm beneficiary details.

### Week 3: Digital Asset Inventory
- **What to Do**: Create an inventory of digital assets, including cryptocurrencies, online accounts, and intellectual property.
- **Why**: Digital assets can be easily overlooked in estate planning, leading to loss of value.
- **Expected Outcome**: Comprehensive management and transfer of digital assets.
- **First Step**: Use a digital asset management tool to catalog and secure access credentials.

### Week 4: Power of Attorney
- **What to Do**: Designate a power of attorney (POA) for financial and legal matters.
- **Why**: A POA ensures that someone you trust can make decisions on your behalf if you become incapacitated.
- **Expected Outcome**: Continuity in financial management and legal affairs.
- **First Step**: Draft a POA document with legal assistance, specifying the scope and duration of authority.

### Week 5: Advanced Healthcare Directive
- **What to Do**: Establish an advanced healthcare directive to outline medical preferences.
- **Why**: Provides clear instructions for healthcare decisions if you're unable to communicate.
- **Expected Outcome**: Ensures medical care aligns with your wishes, reducing stress for loved ones.
- **First Step**: Complete a healthcare directive form, which can often be obtained from healthcare providers or legal advisors.

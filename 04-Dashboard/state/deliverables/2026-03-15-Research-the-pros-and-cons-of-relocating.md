## Austin, TX Tech Job Market

**Job Growth & Demand**

Austin shows **18% increase in tech job postings** year-over-year, significantly outpacing the national average of 9%.[2] Local tech jobs comprise **16.3% of all positions** in the city, compared to the 9% national average.[1] The tech sector employs over **180,000 people**, representing **13.7% of Austin's total workforce**.[1]

**Salary & Compensation**

The **median tech salary in Austin is $99,042**.[1] However, search results indicate a critical hiring shift: **general tech job postings are 35% below previous peaks**, while deeply specialized roles like Cloud Architects and Data Engineers remain in high demand.[4]

**Key Employers & Growth Areas**

Major companies operating in Austin include Apple, Google, Meta, Tesla, and SpaceX.[1] The city is experiencing particular growth in **AI, clean tech, and enterprise software**.[1] Infrastructure investments include Austin Energy's first geothermal facility and multiple new data centers.[1]

**Broader Texas Context**

Texas employment growth projections for 2026 are modest: approximately **1.1% growth, translating to roughly 155,000 new jobs statewide**.[3] This improvement is expected to stem partly from AI-driven investments, such as new data centers in West Texas.[3]

## Plan

### Objective

### Framework Overview
- **Quarterly Planning:** Set clear objectives and key results aligned with Rahul's priorities.
- **Weekly Check-ins:** Implement regular progress tracking to build consistency and adjust strategies.
- **Annual Reviews:** Evaluate overall progress, refine goals, and set new objectives.

## Implementation Steps

### Quarterly Planning
1. **Objective Setting (Weeks 1-3):**
   - **Action:** Use EvalFlow's 90-Day Roadmap to set up OKR tools and training. Define 2-3 primary objectives for the quarter.
   - **Why:** Establishes a focused start with clear goals, crucial for strategic alignment.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Clear roadmap for the quarter with measurable key results.
   - **First Step:** Schedule a kickoff meeting to introduce OKR tools and process.

2. **OKR Drafting and Alignment (Weeks 4-6):**
   - **Action:** Draft team and personal OKRs, ensuring each Key Result has a single owner.
   - **Why:** Increases accountability and boosts completion rates by 26%.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Aligned and actionable OKRs ready for execution.
   - **First Step:** Use Tability's templates to draft and align OKRs.

### Weekly Check-ins
1. **Weekly Stand-ups (Weeks 7-10):**
   - **Action:** Conduct 15-minute weekly stand-ups to discuss updates and blockers.
   - **Why:** Builds a habit-like routine, ensuring consistent progress and quick issue resolution.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Increased transparency and team alignment.
   - **First Step:** Schedule recurring weekly meetings in the calendar.

2. **Mid-Quarter Review (Week 8):**
   - **Action:** Conduct a mid-quarter review to assess progress and make necessary adjustments.
   - **Why:** Provides an opportunity to pivot strategies based on real-time data.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Enhanced flexibility and responsiveness to change.
   - **First Step:** Prepare a progress report using Tability’s automated dashboards.

### Annual Reviews
1. **Year-End Evaluation:**
   - **Action:** Conduct a comprehensive review of the year’s OKRs and habit tracking.
   - **Why:** Evaluates long-term progress and informs future goal setting.
   - **Expected Outcome:** Clear insights into achievements and areas for improvement.
   - **First Step:** Compile data from quarterly reviews and weekly check-ins for analysis.

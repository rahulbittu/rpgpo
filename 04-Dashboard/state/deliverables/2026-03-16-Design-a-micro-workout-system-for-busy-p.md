## Finding 1: Fitties Micro Workouts Guide (Bodyweight Exercises in 10 Minutes or Less)
This guide provides specific 5-minute adaptable routines using bodyweight exercises for busy people, categorized by muscle groups with exact reps and sets. All can be done in short bursts without equipment.

- **Upper Body: Push-Ups** - High plank, lower chest to floor, push up; modify on knees/wall. 2 sets of 10 reps (under 2 minutes).[1]
- **Lower Body: Squats** - Feet shoulder-width, lower as if sitting in chair, knees over ankles. 2 sets of 10 reps.[1]
- **Lower Body: Lunges** - Step forward to 90-degree knee, alternate legs. 2 sets of 10 reps per leg.[1]
- **Core/Cardio: Mountain Climbers** - High plank, alternate knees to chest rapidly. 1 minute continuous.[1]
- **Cardio: Jumping Jacks** - Jump feet out/arms overhead, continuous. 1 minute.[1]
- **Advanced Full-Body Cardio: Burpees** - Squat to plank, push-up, jump up. 2 sets of 10 reps (fits 5 minutes).[1]

**Next Steps**: Perform 1 exercise per category back-to-back for a 5-minute circuit; repeat 2-3x daily. Track via app like Fitties for habit building.

## Synthesize micro-workout plan
## Plan

### Day 1: Introduction and Upper Body Focus
- **Warm-Up (1 minute)**: Light jogging in place
- **Push-Ups**: 2 sets of 10 reps (under 2 minutes)
  - **Modification**: Perform on knees or against a wall if needed
- **Cool Down (1 minute)**: Arm circles and deep breathing

### Day 2: Lower Body Strength
- **Warm-Up (1 minute)**: Leg swings
- **Squats**: 2 sets of 10 reps
- **Lunges**: 2 sets of 10 reps per leg
- **Cool Down (1 minute)**: Hamstring stretch

### Day 3: Core and Cardio
- **Warm-Up (1 minute)**: Jumping jacks
- **Mountain Climbers**: 1 minute continuous
- **Jumping Jacks**: 1 minute continuous
- **Cool Down (1 minute)**: Child’s pose

### Day 4: Full-Body Cardio
- **Warm-Up (1 minute)**: High knees
- **Burpees**: 2 sets of 10 reps
- **Cool Down (1 minute)**: Forward fold stretch

### Day 5: Active Recovery
- **Yoga or Light Stretching**: 5 minutes focusing on flexibility and relaxation

### Repeat Cycle
- Perform each day's workout 2-3 times throughout the day for optimal results.

## Progression

- **Weeks 1-2**: Focus on mastering form and consistency.
- **Weeks 3-4**: Increase reps or sets as strength and endurance improve.
- **Weeks 5+**: Introduce variations or additional exercises to prevent plateau and maintain engagement.

## Safety Notes

- **Form First**: Prioritize correct form over speed or quantity to prevent injury.
- **Listen to Your Body**: Modify exercises as needed based on fitness level and any existing injuries.
- **Hydration and Nutrition**: Maintain adequate hydration and a balanced diet to support workout recovery and energy levels.

By following this structured plan, busy professionals can integrate effective micro-workouts into their daily routine, leading to improved health and fitness without significant time investment.

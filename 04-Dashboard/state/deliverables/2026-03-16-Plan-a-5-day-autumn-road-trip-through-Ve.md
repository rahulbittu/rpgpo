## Foliage Viewing Spots
- **Kancamagus Highway (New Hampshire)**: A premier scenic drive through White Mountain National Forest with storybook fall colors; peak foliage typically early to mid-October.[4][6]
- **Route 100 (Vermont)**: Iconic byway from southern to northern Vermont, renowned for vibrant foliage; aim for first or second week of October in southern areas, last week of September to first week of October in north.[3][4]
- **Smuggler’s Notch Road (Vermont Rte 108)**: 15-mile mountain pass with boulders, waterfalls, and hiking like Barnes Camp Trail (easy views) or Sterling Pond Trail (3 miles); gondola at Mt. Mansfield open until mid-October.[2]
- **Franconia Notch State Park (New Hampshire)**: Features river gorges, The Basin (15-foot-deep ice age bowl), waterfalls, glacial boulders; adjacent Loon Mountain gondola for elevated foliage views.[1][9]
- **Lost River Gorge & Boulder Caves (New Hampshire)**: 1-mile boardwalk loop with caves, treehouse, viewpoints amid foliage; advance reservations required as fall days sell out.[1]

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Vermont
- **Morning**: Arrive in Burlington, Vermont. Rent a car at the airport.
- **Afternoon**: Drive to **Route 100**. Begin your scenic drive southward, stopping at viewpoints for foliage photography.
- **Evening**: Stay overnight at **The Pitcher Inn** in Warren, VT. Known for its cozy atmosphere and proximity to Route 100. Rates start at $350/night. [3]

### Day 2: Vermont Exploration
- **Morning**: Continue south on Route 100. Visit **Cold Hollow Cider Mill** in Waterbury for fresh cider and donuts.
- **Afternoon**: Head to **Smuggler’s Notch Road (Rte 108)**. Hike the **Barnes Camp Trail** for easy foliage views.
- **Evening**: Stay at **Trapp Family Lodge** in Stowe, VT. Offers Austrian-inspired accommodations starting at $250/night. [2]

### Day 3: Covered Bridges & Maple Syrup Farms
- **Morning**: Depart for **Montpelier**. Visit **Morse Farm Maple Sugarworks** for a tour and tasting. 
- **Afternoon**: Explore covered bridges in the area, such as **Gold Brook Covered Bridge** in Stowe.
- **Evening**: Drive to Hanover, NH. Stay at **Hanover Inn Dartmouth**, rates from $200/night.

### Day 4: New Hampshire Scenic Drives
- **Morning**: Drive the **Kancamagus Highway**. Stop at overlooks like **Sabbaday Falls**.
- **Afternoon**: Visit **Lost River Gorge & Boulder Caves**. Book tickets in advance. Entry is approximately $20 per person. [1]
- **Evening**: Stay at **The Notchland Inn** near Franconia Notch, NH. Rates start at $275/night.

### Day 5: Franconia Notch State Park
- **Morning**: Explore **Franconia Notch State Park**. Visit **The Basin** and take the **Loon Mountain gondola** for panoramic views.
- **Afternoon**: Return drive to Burlington, VT for departure.

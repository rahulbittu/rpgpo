- **Regular Feedback Loops**: Implement weekly newsletters or dedicated channels for updates and ensure feedback from surveys or Q&A sessions is addressed within 48 hours to keep employees informed and engaged.

- **Tools and Reviews**: Utilize performance management software for regular reviews, focusing on outcomes rather than presence. Monthly performance reviews can help track progress and address issues promptly.
- **Hybrid Setup**: For hybrid models, clarify in-office time for collaboration and tie availability to outcomes, ensuring flexibility and productivity.

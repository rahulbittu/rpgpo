## Synthesize Itinerary for Scottish Highlands Road Trip
## Itinerary (Day-by-Day)

### Day 1: Arrival in Edinburgh
- **Activity**: Arrive in Edinburgh, pick up rental car.
- **Why**: Edinburgh is a convenient starting point with major airport access.
- **Next Step**: Book a rental car in advance for pick-up at Edinburgh Airport.

### Day 2: Edinburgh to Inveraray
- **Activity**: Drive to Inveraray Castle & Gardens.
- **Why**: Explore the 15th-century ancestral seat of Clan Campbell.
- **Next Step**: Purchase tickets online for Inveraray Castle to avoid queues.

### Day 3: Inveraray to Fort William
- **Activity**: Scenic drive via Loch Lomond, explore Fort William.
- **Why**: Experience the beauty of Loch Lomond and the gateway to Ben Nevis.
- **Next Step**: Plan stops along Loch Lomond for photo opportunities.

### Day 4: Fort William to Isle of Skye
- **Activity**: Visit Eilean Donan Castle, proceed to Isle of Skye.
- **Why**: Iconic castle views and the natural beauty of Skye.
- **Next Step**: Book accommodation on Isle of Skye in advance.

### Day 5: Isle of Skye Exploration
- **Activity**: Visit Dunvegan Castle and Armadale Castle & Gardens.
- **Why**: Explore historical sites and stunning gardens.
- **Next Step**: Check opening times and book tickets for both castles.

### Day 6: Isle of Skye to Inverness
- **Activity**: Travel to Inverness, visit Urquhart Castle.
- **Why**: Experience Loch Ness and medieval history.
- **Next Step**: Book a Loch Ness cruise that includes Urquhart Castle.

### Day 7: Inverness to Speyside
- **Activity**: Distillery tours in Speyside (e.g., Glenfiddich, Macallan).
- **Why**: Discover Scotland's whisky-making heritage.
- **Next Step**: Schedule distillery tours in advance.

### Day 8: Speyside to Cairngorms National Park
- **Activity**: Hiking in Cairngorms National Park.
- **Why**: Explore one of Scotland's most beautiful natural areas.
- **Next Step**: Research and select hiking trails suitable for your experience level.

### Day 9: Cairngorms to Perthshire
- **Activity**: Scenic drive and visit Blair Castle.
- **Why**: Experience the historic Blair Castle and scenic Perthshire.
- **Next Step**: Purchase tickets for Blair Castle online.

### Day 10: Return to Edinburgh
- **Activity**: Return to Edinburgh, explore city attractions if time permits.
- **Why**: Conclude the trip with Edinburgh's rich history and culture.
- **Next Step**: Plan for car return and check flight details.

## Google Nest Learning Thermostat (3rd & 4th Gen)

**Pricing & Availability**
The Nest Learning Thermostat is priced at **$175 on Amazon**[3], with alternative retailers offering it at $284 (Wayfair) and $180 (Walmart)[3]. A newer budget model, the Google Nest G4CVZ, is available for **$130 on Amazon**[3].

**Occupancy Detection**
The Nest uses **geofencing technology that detects your smartphone to automatically adjust heating and cooling schedules when you're home or away**[3]. It also features a **built-in motion sensor** that works in conjunction with phone location data to determine occupancy[4]. When nobody is home, the thermostat automatically drops the temperature[4].

**Energy Reports & Monitoring**
The Nest provides **energy reports offering helpful insights into when your unit is running and for how long**[1]. It includes an **HVAC monitoring feature that sends alerts if something seems off with your system**[4]. The thermostat is **Energy Star certified**[4] and compatible with energy-saving plans and rebates offered by many utility companies[1].

**HVAC System Compatibility**
The Nest works with **2-stage heating and cooling systems** and requires a **3-wire configuration**[2]. Notably, it **does not require a C-wire**[1], making installation easier for many homes. It supports **universal thermostat compatibility**[2] and works with **Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, Nest, Insteon, and Nexia**[1].

**Smart Home Integration**
The thermostat **works with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant** but **does not support Apple HomeKit**[1]. The 4th generation model features **AI-powered learning capabilities** and a **motion-activated Dynamic Farsight feature** that displays temperature, time, and weather at a glance[5].

**User Reviews & Notable Features**
Users praise its **intuitive design and easy setup**[1]. One user reported replacing a $600 professional setup with a single Nest thermostat and two Echo Dots with comparable results[4]. The dial design is described as pleasing to use[3]. Additional **Nest Temperature Sensors are available separately for $39** and work up to 50 feet away[3].

**Limitations**
The Nest **does not include remote sensors** in the base package[3] and the auto-scheduling feature may be confusing for users who prefer fine-tuning their schedules[1].

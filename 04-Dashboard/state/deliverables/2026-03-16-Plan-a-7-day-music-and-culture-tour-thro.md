## Honky Tonks in Nashville
Honky tonks cluster on Lower Broadway in Downtown Nashville, featuring live music, multiple bars, and rooftops in historic buildings.[1][2][5]

- **Nudie's Honky Tonk**: 409 Broadway, Nashville, TN 37203 (SoBro neighborhood); 100-year-old three-story building with rare country memorabilia, multiple bars, three stages, rooftop deck; open daily with live music.[1][2]
- **Kid Rock’s Big Ass Honky Tonk**: Lower Broadway; sky-high rooftop with downtown views, steaks, 4K TVs on every floor.[2]
- **Luke’s 32 Bridge**: Lower Broadway; Luke Bryan's bar with "Crash My Party Rooftop Patio," six levels, eight bars, four stages.[2]
- **Tootsies**: Corner of 5th Ave and Broadway; purple building with rooftop patio and bar for people-watching.[2]
- **Jason Aldean’s Kitchen + Rooftop Bar**: Lower Broadway; "My Kinda Party" rooftop, one of Broadway's largest patios, VIP rooftop section.[2]

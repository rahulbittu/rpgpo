## Develop 90-Day Personal Branding Plan
## Key Findings

1. **Zero to Mastery Guide**: Emphasizes the importance of personal branding for data engineers, focusing on LinkedIn optimization, resume building, and portfolio creation. Highlights the need for showcasing practical projects and skills over traditional qualifications. The course by Dan Schifano is a valuable resource for building these assets.
   
2. **Coursera Insights**: Suggests increasing visibility through freelance projects, hackathons, and internships. Recommends applying for entry-level positions to gain experience and networks, with platforms like Indeed being useful for job searches. The Google AI Professional Certificate is suggested for skill enhancement.

## Recommendations

1. **Content Pillars**:
   - **Technical Projects**: Share detailed breakdowns of personal projects (e.g., ETL pipelines, data warehouses) on GitHub and LinkedIn. Highlight impact metrics.
   - **Industry Insights**: Post about trends in data engineering and AI, sharing insights from courses like the Google AI Professional Certificate.
   - **Career Development**: Document the journey of building a personal brand and career growth strategies, including learnings from the Zero to Mastery course.

2. **Posting Cadence**:
   - **LinkedIn**: Post 2-3 times a week. Focus on project showcases, industry insights, and personal growth stories.
   - **GitHub**: Update repositories bi-weekly with new projects or enhancements to existing ones.

3. **Engagement Strategy**:
   - **Networking**: Engage with posts from industry leaders and peers. Comment thoughtfully and share relevant content.
   - **Community Involvement**: Participate in data engineering forums and LinkedIn groups. Offer advice and seek feedback on shared projects.

4. **Metrics for Measuring Traction**:
   - **LinkedIn Metrics**: Track profile views, connection requests, and engagement rates on posts.
   - **GitHub Metrics**: Monitor repository stars, forks, and contributions.
   - **Professional Opportunities**: Measure the number of freelance projects, hackathon participations, and job interviews.

## Action Items

1. **Enroll in the Zero to Mastery Course** (Week 1-2):
   - Complete the course to enhance LinkedIn profile and build a professional portfolio.
   - Outcome: Improved online presence and professional assets.

2. **Complete Google AI Professional Certificate** (Week 3-4):
   - Gain additional skills and share insights on LinkedIn.
   - Outcome: Enhanced skill set and content for industry insights pillar.

3. **Develop and Share Projects on GitHub** (Ongoing, bi-weekly updates):
   - Add 3-5 detailed data engineering projects with READMEs.
   - Outcome: Demonstrated expertise and increased visibility.

4. **Engage on LinkedIn and Forums** (Weekly):
   - Post regularly, engage with others, and participate in discussions.
   - Outcome: Increased network and engagement metrics.

5. **Track Metrics and Adjust Strategy** (Monthly Review):
   - Analyze LinkedIn and GitHub metrics to refine content strategy.
   - Outcome: Data-driven adjustments for better traction.

## 12-Day Wildlife and Nature Photography Tour Itinerary: East Africa (Kenya & Tanzania)

### Day 1-3: Arrival and Masai Mara, Kenya

- **Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi**
  - **Logistics:** Fly into Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO), Nairobi. Overnight stay at a hotel near the airport.
  - **First Step:** Arrange a private transfer to Masai Mara.

- **Day 2-3: Masai Mara National Reserve**
  - **Wildlife Event:** Focus on the Great Migration. Expect early crossings of the Mara River by wildebeests.
  - **Photography Spots:** Mara River crossing points, Talek River, and the Mara Triangle.
  - **Gear Recommendations:** Nikon Z8 with a telephoto lens (e.g., 70-200mm) for capturing action shots of river crossings.
  - **Logistics:** Stay at a lodge near the Mara River. Bookings should be made 6-12 months in advance due to peak season demand.
  - **Daily Routine:** Early morning and late afternoon game drives for optimal lighting and wildlife activity.

### Day 4-5: Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

- **Day 4: Transfer to Serengeti**
  - **Logistics:** Fly from Masai Mara to Serengeti via a connecting flight through Wilson Airport (WIL), Nairobi, and Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO).
  - **Accommodation:** Stay at a camp in the northern Serengeti for proximity to migration paths.

- **Day 5: Northern Serengeti**
  - **Wildlife Event:** Continue observing the Great Migration as herds move between Serengeti and Masai Mara.
  - **Photography Spots:** Kogatende and Lamai Wedge for river crossings.
  - **Gear Recommendations:** Consider adding a wide-angle lens for landscape shots.
  - **Daily Routine:** Sunrise and sunset game drives to capture the golden hour.

### Day 6-7: Central Serengeti

- **Day 6-7: Central Serengeti**
  - **Photography Spots:** Seronera Valley for big cats and kopjes for landscapes.
  - **Wildlife Focus:** Lions, leopards, and cheetahs.
  - **Gear Recommendations:** Use a tripod for stable shots and a polarizing filter to reduce glare.
  - **Logistics:** Stay at a lodge in Seronera. Book in advance.

### Day 8: Ngorongoro Crater

- **Day 8: Ngorongoro Crater**
  - **Wildlife Event:** Explore the crater floor, home to the Big Five.
  - **Photography Spots:** Crater rim for panoramic shots; Lerai Forest for elephants.
  - **Gear Recommendations:** High-capacity memory cards for a full day of shooting.
  - **Logistics:** Overnight at a lodge on the crater rim. Early morning descent into the crater for best wildlife viewing.

### Day 9-10: Zanzibar Extension

- **Day 9: Travel to Zanzibar**
  - **Logistics:** Fly from Kilimanjaro International Airport to Abeid Amani Karume International Airport (ZNZ), Zanzibar.
  - **Accommodation:** Stay at a beach resort in Nungwi or Kendwa.

- **Day 10: Zanzibar Exploration**
  - **Photography Spots:** Stone Town for cultural and architectural photography; Nungwi Beach for sunset shots.
  - **Gear Recommendations:** Lightweight camera setup for ease of travel.
  - **Activities:** Spice tour, snorkeling, and exploring local markets.

### Day 11-12: Relaxation and Departure

- **Day 11: Relaxation Day**
  - **Activities:** Enjoy the beach, optional dhow cruise, or visit Jozani Forest.
  - **Photography Focus:** Capture local life and landscapes.

- **Day 12: Departure**
  - **Logistics:** Return flight from Zanzibar to your home country.

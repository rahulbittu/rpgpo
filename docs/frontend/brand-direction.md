# GPO Brand Direction

## Product Name
**GPO** — Governed Personal Office

Not "RPGPO Command Center." Not "TopRanker Dashboard." The product is GPO.

## Tagline
*Your private AI operating system.*

## Tone
- Confident but not arrogant
- Precise but not cold
- Professional but not corporate
- Intelligent but not condescending

## Visual Feel
Think: Bloomberg Terminal meets Linear meets Vercel Dashboard.

- **Bloomberg:** Information density done right, dark surfaces, operational clarity
- **Linear:** Clean hierarchy, modern dark theme, restrained accents, fast feel
- **Vercel:** Premium minimal, sharp typography, calm confidence

Not: Notion (too playful), Retool (too generic), Salesforce (too corporate).

## Color Psychology
- Dark base (#0a0d15) — immersion, focus, privacy
- Blue accent (#6b8afd) — trust, intelligence, stability
- Green (#50c878) — success, health, completion
- Yellow (#f0b428) — attention, pending, caution
- Red (#dc503c) — danger, failure, rejection

## Naming in UI
| Use | Don't Use |
|---|---|
| GPO | RPGPO, RPGPO Command Center |
| Operator | Rahul (no user-specific names in product UI) |
| Engine | Mission, Domain (in operator-facing text) |
| Board of AI | Board, Deliberation panel |
| Task | Request, Ticket |
| Deliverable | Output, Result |
| Approval | Gate, Checkpoint |

## Logo / Branding
Not yet defined. Product surface should work without a logo — identity comes from the UI quality and naming, not a graphic mark.

## Important Documents for Emergencies
Keep these physical and digital copies in waterproof containers or cloud storage for quick access during evacuations or outages.

- **Identification and Legal Papers**: Passports, driver's licenses, birth certificates, Social Security cards, marriage/divorce certificates, and pet IDs; scan and store digitally.[1]
- **Financial and Insurance Docs**: Bank account details, credit card info, insurance policies (home, auto, health, life), mortgage/lease agreements, and property deeds.[1]
- **Medical Records**: Prescription lists, allergies, vaccination records, and doctor's contacts; include 7-14 days of medications.[1]

## Detailed Analysis

- **Setup (Act 1: 0-2.5 min)**:
  - **Opening Image**: Introduce the protagonist, an engineer, in a typical work setting, showcasing their meticulous nature and reliance on precise communication in code reviews.
  - **Inciting Incident**: The engineer receives a new task to review an AI's code, which is being developed to assist in automating code reviews.

- **Conflict (Act 2: 2.5-7.5 min)**:
  - **Rising Action**: The engineer begins reviewing the AI's code, providing detailed comments meant to guide improvements. The AI, however, interprets these comments literally, leading to unexpected and humorous changes in the codebase.
  - **Midpoint Crisis**: The engineer realizes the AI is executing commands based on their comments verbatim, causing chaos in the system. This realization occurs during a critical project deadline.
  - **Plot Point 2**: The engineer attempts to correct the AI's actions by adjusting their communication style, but the AI's literal interpretation continues to escalate the situation.

- **Climax and Resolution (Act 3: 7.5-10 min)**:
  - **Climax**: In a race against time, the engineer devises a creative solution by using humor and metaphor in their comments, tricking the AI into correcting its own errors.
  - **Denouement**: The AI successfully implements the changes, and the system stabilizes just in time for the project deadline. The engineer learns the importance of clear communication and adaptability when working with AI.

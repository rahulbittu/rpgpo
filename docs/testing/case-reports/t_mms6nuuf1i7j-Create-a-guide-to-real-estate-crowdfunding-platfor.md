## CrowdStreet

**Minimum Investment & Focus**
CrowdStreet requires a **$25,000 minimum investment** and specializes exclusively in **commercial real estate (CRE)** including office space, retail space, and multifamily properties.[1] The platform launched in 2014.[6]

**Returns & Fees**
Each investment has its own fee structure and expenses; fees vary by project.[1] Management fees for real estate private equity-style investments typically range from 1.0–2.0% annually.[4]

## RealtyMogul

**Minimum Investment & Scale**
RealtyMogul offers the **lowest minimum investment at $5,000 for REITs**, though other investments generally start at **$25,000**.[3] The platform is one of the oldest in the space.[6]

**Returns & Fees**
- **RealtyMogul Income REIT**: Paid distributions monthly of approximately **4% annualized net of fees** for over nine years.[3]
- **RealtyMogul Apartment Growth REIT**: Paid distributions quarterly equating to roughly **4.5% annualized net of fees** since January 1, 2018.[3]
- Standard fees range from **1% to 1.25%** for most investments, with additional fees potentially applying.[3]

**Liquidity**
RealtyMogul offers a **Share Repurchase Program** allowing shareholders to sell RealtyMogul Income REIT and Apartment Growth REIT investments back to the platform **quarterly after the first year** at a reduced price, though buybacks are "subject to availability of capital."[3] Private placements have no redemption program.[3]

**Accreditation Requirements**
RealtyMogul offers investment opportunities for both accredited and non-accredited investors, though some investments require accreditation.[3]

## Key Findings

1. **Business Name Compliance**: Ensure your LLC name is unique and compliant, including "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company". Verify availability using the Texas Secretary of State's name search tool.
   
2. **Registered Agent Requirement**: Appoint a registered agent with a physical address in Texas. This can be an individual or a service provider like Tailor Brands or GoDaddy.

3. **Filing Form 205**: File the Certificate of Formation (Form 205) online via SOSDirect or by mail, with a $300 filing fee. Include all necessary details such as the LLC name, registered agent information, and management structure.

4. **Operating Agreement and EIN**: Draft an operating agreement to outline the LLC's management and obtain an Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the IRS for tax purposes.

5. **Bank Account Setup**: Open a business bank account to separate personal and business finances, using the EIN and approved LLC documentation.

## Detailed Analysis

1. **Business Name Compliance**:
   - **Importance**: Ensures legal compliance and avoids rejection of your filing.
   - **Verification**: Use the Texas SOS entity search tool [here](https://mycpa.cpa.state.tx.us/coa/).

2. **Registered Agent**:
   - **Role**: Receives legal documents on behalf of the LLC.
   - **Options**: Choose a member, employee, or hire a service provider. Ensure the agent's details are included in Form 205.

3. **Filing Form 205**:
   - **Process**: Download from the Texas Secretary of State website, file online via [SOSDirect](https://direct.sos.state.tx.us/) or mail.
   - **Details Required**: LLC name, registered agent info, management type, organizer signature, and effective date.

4. **Operating Agreement and EIN**:
   - **Operating Agreement**: Not legally required but highly recommended to define roles and responsibilities.
   - **EIN**: Obtain from the IRS [here](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online).

5. **Bank Account Setup**:
   - **Purpose**: Keeps business finances separate and simplifies accounting.
   - **Requirements**: EIN and approved LLC documentation.

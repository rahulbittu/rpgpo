## Finding 1: 1-on-1 Meeting Cadence and Structure for Engineering Managers
Engineering Managers should schedule **fortnightly 1:1s** (every two weeks) for remote teams, keeping them short and valuable to avoid meeting fatigue; use them to explore root causes of underperformance with open, non-judgmental questions after confirming clear expectations and recent feedback have been communicated.

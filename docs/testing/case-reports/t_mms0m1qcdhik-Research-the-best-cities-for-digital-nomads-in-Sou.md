## Detailed Analysis

- **Bali**: Offers a range of living costs suitable for different budgets. The internet is reliable, and coworking spaces are moderately priced. However, visa options are less clear, which could complicate long-term stays.

- **Bangkok**: Comparable in cost to Bali's higher range, with excellent urban amenities and high-speed internet. However, the cost of living and coworking spaces can be higher than in Chiang Mai and Ho Chi Minh City.

- **Chiang Mai**: Known for its affordability and strong digital nomad community. Offers lower costs for living, internet, and coworking spaces compared to Bali and Bangkok.

- **Ho Chi Minh City**: Provides an affordable urban lifestyle with a growing digital nomad community. Offers reliable internet and more straightforward visa options compared to Bali.

## Detailed Analysis

### Texas LLC Formation
- **Filing Requirements:** Form 205 must include detailed information about the LLC, such as the registered agent's Texas address and management structure. The filing fee is $300. [1][2][3]
- **Tax Obligations:** LLCs with revenue over $2.47 million must file a franchise tax report. Pass-through taxation is the default. [1][4]
- **Maintenance:** Regular updates to the Secretary of State are required for any changes. An operating agreement, while not mandatory, is advisable for internal governance. [1][3][4]

### Delaware LLC Formation
- **Process and Costs:** The Certificate of Formation costs $90. Delaware is known for its business-friendly laws and allows for anonymous LLCs, enhancing privacy. [5][6]
- **Taxation:** An annual franchise tax of $300 is required, but there is no state income tax for non-residents, making it attractive for businesses with out-of-state operations. [6]

### Wyoming LLC Formation
- **Filing and Privacy:** The Articles of Organization cost $100. Wyoming offers strong privacy protections, with minimal information disclosed publicly. [7]
- **Annual Requirements:** A simple annual report is required, with a $50 fee. [7]

### Nevada LLC Formation
- **Initial Costs:** The total initial filing fee is $425, which includes the business license. Nevada offers no state income tax and allows for anonymous ownership. [8]
- **Ongoing Obligations:** An annual business license renewal is required, maintaining the state's revenue from LLCs. [8]

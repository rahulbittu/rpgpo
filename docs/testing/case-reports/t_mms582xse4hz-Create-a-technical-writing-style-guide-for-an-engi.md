### API Documentation
- **Layered Detail**: Begin with a high-level overview and progressively dive into specifics such as authorization protocols and endpoints. This approach helps users of varying expertise to find relevant information easily.
- **Modular Structure**: Organize content into sections that can stand alone, allowing users to access information without navigating through unnecessary details.
- **Audience Segmentation**: Use tables of contents and supplementary guides to cater to different proficiency levels. This ensures that both novice and experienced users can efficiently find the information they need.
- **Visuals and Discoverability**: Incorporate screenshots and wireframes to enhance understanding. Optimize documentation with SEO keywords and metadata to improve searchability.

### General Best Practices
- **Clarity and Precision**: Use active voice and define technical terms to ensure that documentation is accessible to a broad audience. Avoid jargon unless it is widely understood by the intended audience.

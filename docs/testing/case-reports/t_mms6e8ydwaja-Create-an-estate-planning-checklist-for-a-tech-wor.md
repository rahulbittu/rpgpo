## Wills
A **will** specifies asset distribution and guardianship for minors upon death; without one, state laws dictate outcomes, potentially causing delays.[1] Tech workers in their 30s should ensure wills cover physical assets like property deeds and integrate with revocable living trusts for incapacity protection.[1] Review wills amid 2026 "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" (OBBBA) changes, including estate tax exemptions rising to **$15 million per individual** ($30 million for couples).[2][3]

## Detailed Analysis

### Salary Negotiation

- **Current Trends**: Startups are now competitive with enterprises in terms of base salaries for senior roles. This is particularly true in high-demand sectors like AI and fintech.
- **Negotiation Strategy**: Focus on the total compensation package, including potential equity upside. Be prepared to discuss both salary and equity in detail, leveraging the startup's need to attract top talent.

### Equity Valuation Methods

- **Dilution and Liquidation**: Understand the potential for equity dilution and the terms of liquidation preferences. This involves assessing the startup's cap table and any preference stacks that may affect your equity's value.
- **Equity Modeling**: Use tools and resources to model potential outcomes for your equity, considering company growth scenarios and exit strategies.

### Risk Assessment

- **Startup Viability**: Evaluate the startup's financial health, market position, and growth prospects. This includes understanding the funding stage and the credibility of investors.
- **Personal Risk Tolerance**: Assess your own risk tolerance and career goals. Weigh the potential for high rewards against the inherent risks of joining a startup.
